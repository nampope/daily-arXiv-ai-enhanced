### [1] [Asymptotically geodesic hypersurfaces and the fundamental groups of hyperbolic manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24869)
*Xiaolong Hans Han,Ruojing Jiang*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了双曲流形中的闭超曲面，证明了渐近测地超曲面的存在与基本群的性质相关，并构造了特定算术流形中的渐近测地超曲面序列。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究双曲流形中闭超曲面的渐近几何性质，特别是渐近测地超曲面的存在性与流形基本群的代数性质之间的关系，以及回答Al Assal--Lowe提出的相关问题。

Method: 采用几何群论和双曲几何的方法，分析闭双曲流形中渐近测地超曲面的存在性条件，并针对特定类型的算术流形构造具体的渐近测地超曲面序列。

Result: 证明了如果闭双曲流形包含渐近测地超曲面序列，则其基本群是virtually special的，从而在整数上是线性的。对于类型I的算术流形，构造了渐近测地、强填充且在Grassmann丛上等分布的闭超曲面序列。

Conclusion: 该研究建立了双曲流形中渐近测地超曲面的存在性与基本群代数性质之间的深刻联系，为理解双曲流形的几何与代数结构提供了新视角，并部分回答了Al Assal--Lowe的问题。

Abstract: We consider closed hypersurfaces smoothly immersed in hyperbolic manifolds up to homotopy and commensurability. We prove that if a closed hyperbolic manifold $M$ contains a sequence of asymptotically geodesic hypersurfaces, then $π_1(M)$ is virtually special and hence linear over integers. If $M$ (dimension at least 3) is, in addition, arithmetic of type I, we constructs a sequence of hypersurfaces which are asymptotically geodesic (but not totally geodesic), strongly filling, and equidistributing in the Grassmann bundle over $M$. This partially answers a question of Al Assal--Lowe. As a corollary, for each cocompact arithmetic lattice $Γ$ of $SO(n+1,1)$ of type I, there exist infinitely many arithmetic and infinitely many non-arithmetic cocompact lattices $H$ of $SO(n,1)$ that admit monomorphisms into $Γ$ which do not extend to a Lie group homomorphism from $SO(n,1)$ into $SO(n+1,1)$.

Result: 成功构造了辫子和纽结的新不变量——割线-拟群(SQ)

Abstract: We construct a novel invariant of braids and knots, secant-quandle (SQ),with generic secants serving as generators and generic horizontal trisecants serving as relations, i.e., $SQ = Γ\left< \mathcal{S}_M\mid \mathcal{S}_T,\mathcal{E}_M \right>$, where $M$ is a braid or link.

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: A型系数多项式能从HOMFLY-PT多项式恢复出无限序列的链环不变量，且低阶系数易于计算。作者希望将这一视角扩展到B型设置

Method: 引入与B型skein关系相关的系数多项式，证明其生成级数能恢复Kauffman多项式。由于B型的四项skein关系，需要全新的归纳方案

Conclusion: 将A型系数多项式构造推广到B型设置，为Kauffman多项式提供了类似的系数多项式序列，扩展了skein理论中的多项式不变量研究

Abstract: An A-type coefficient polynomial introduced by Kawauchi recovers the HOMFLY-PT polynomial as a formal power series within skein theory. More precisely, we introduce coefficient polynomials associated with the B-type skein relation and show that their generating series recovers the Kauffman polynomial. We further prove that these coefficient polynomials are well-defined and that the resulting generating series is invariant under the corresponding Reidemeister moves.

### [7] [Spectral Homotopy and the Spectral Fundamental Group](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25701)
*Biswajit Mitra,Sourav Koner*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 为交换pm-环引入谱基本群π_k^{alg}(A)，证明其基本性质，建立与拓扑映射空间基本群的同构，并构造具有非平凡谱基本群但不能嵌入特征零域上函数环的例子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为交换pm-环引入代数拓扑不变量，捕捉环的代数同伦动力学，特别是那些不能嵌入函数环但仍具有非平凡同伦结构的环。

Method: 通过环的k-代数自同态幺半群生成的诱导谱映射空间中的环路同伦类定义谱基本群π_k^{alg}(A)，研究其代数性质，建立与拓扑基本群的对应关系，并利用广义对偶数构造具体例子。

Result: 证明π_k^{alg}(A)是阿贝尔群，自然保持直积，对全不变子环有自然态射；建立特定连续函数环的谱基本群与对应拓扑映射空间经典基本群的显式同构；构造出具有非平凡谱基本群但不能嵌入特征零域上函数环的pm-环。

Conclusion: 谱基本群π_k^{alg}(A)成功捕捉了交换pm-环的代数同伦动力学，为研究不能嵌入函数环的环提供了新的代数拓扑工具，揭示了代数结构与同伦理论之间的深刻联系。

Abstract: In this paper, we introduce an algebraic-topological invariant for commutative pm-rings, termed the spectral fundamental group, which is denoted by $π_{k}^{alg}(A)$. This group is defined via homotopy classes of loops within the space of induced spectral maps, which are generated by the $k$-algebra endomorphism monoid of the ring. We establish foundational properties of this invariant, proving that $π_{k}^{alg}(A)$ is an abelian group that naturally respects direct products and admits natural morphisms with respect to fully invariant subrings. Further, we establish an explicit isomorphism between the spectral fundamental group of certain continuous function rings and the classical fundamental group of their associated topological mapping spaces. Finally, utilizing a generalized dual number construction, we present an explicit example of a pm-ring that cannot be embedded into any function ring over a field of characteristic zero, yet possesses a nontrivial spectral fundamental group. This demonstrates that $π_{k}^{alg}(A)$ captures homotopical dynamics that are intrinsically algebraic.

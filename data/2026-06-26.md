### [1] [On quasi-holomorphic homotopies of immersions of 3-manifolds into 5-manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26820)
*András Csépai*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文提出拟全纯同伦的概念，扩展了3-流形到5-流形的浸入正则同伦，允许经过带有孤立奇点（交叉帽/复Whitney伞）的映射，并完整描述了该同伦如何改变浸入像补集的基本群（结群），证明某些3-球面到5-球面的标准嵌入的拟全纯同伦不改变其结群。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将正则同伦概念推广到允许通过带孤立奇点的映射，建立与复几何中全纯映射芽（C²→C³）理论的联系，并探索结群在奇点通过过程中的变化规律。

Method: 通过局部刻画拟全纯同伦的形式，利用全纯映射芽理论（复Whitney伞）分析同伦路径经过孤立奇点的行为，并基于基本群的计算描述结群的变化。

Result: 完整描述了3-流形到5-流形浸入像补集的基本群（结群）在拟全纯同伦下的变化机制；证明了特定拟全纯同伦（针对S³→S⁵的标准嵌入）不改变结群。

Conclusion: 拟全纯同伦为研究流形浸入的奇点理论和结的不变性提供了新工具，某些情况下结群在通过交叉帽奇点后保持不变，暗示更深层的拓扑刚性。

Abstract: The notion of a quasi-holomorphic homotopy of two immersions of a $3$-manifold into a $5$-manifold extends the notion of their regular homotopy by also allowing the homotopy to pass through instances of maps with an isolated singularity around which the path of the homotopy forms a cross-cap (complex Whitney umbrella). We describe the local form of such homotopies and explain connections with the theory of holomorphic map germs from $\mathbb{C}^2$ to $\mathbb{C}^3$. Our main result is a complete a description of how the fundamental group of the complement of the image of an immersion of a $3$-manifold into a $5$-manifold (i.e. its knot group) changes under a quasi-holomorphic homotopy. As a corollary we will see that certain quasi-holomorphic homotopies of the standard embedding of the $3$-sphere into the $5$-sphere do not change its knot group.

### [4] [On L-space surgeries on two-bridge links](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27145)
*Diego Santoro,Hugo Zhou*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文对双桥链环的所有L-空间 surgeries 进行了分类，首次发现存在双曲链环，其L-空间 surgery 集合无法用有限个有理数平面上的矩形并集描述。证明引入了持续叶状性（persistently foliar）的图解条件、双分支L-空间链环有理surgery的Heegaard Floer同调简化模型，以及Turaev挠率计算等方法。作为推论，得到了双桥链环surgery所得双曲L-空间体积的最优一致上界，并分类了所有与双桥链环相关的L-空间卫星结。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 双桥链环作为低维拓扑中一类经典研究对象，其L-空间surgery集合的结构尚不明确。此前已知的L-空间surgery集合均为有限个矩形并集，本文旨在探究是否存在双曲链环打破这一规律，并发展更一般的分析工具。

Method: 1. 提出"持续叶状性"的图解充分条件，证明非平凡surgery支持可定向叶状结构；2. 基于Manolescu-Ozsváth、Liu和Zemke的工作，建立双分支L-空间链环有理surgery的Heegaard Floer同调简化模型，用于导出L-空间surgery的障碍；3. 通过显式计算Turaev挠率，确定广义L-空间链环的L-空间surgery集合。

Result: 完整分类了双桥链环的所有L-空间surgery集合，首次发现某些双曲双桥链环的L-空间surgery集合不能用有限个Q²中矩形并集描述。获得了双桥链环surgery所得双曲L-空间体积的最优一致上界，并完全分类了与双桥链环相关的L-空间卫星结。

Conclusion: 双桥链环的L-空间surgery集合可以突破传统的有限矩形并集结构，展现出更复杂的模式。所引入的持续叶状性条件、简化Floer同调模型和Turaev挠率方法具有更广泛的适用性，为研究其他类链环的L-空间surgery问题提供了新工具。

Abstract: We classify the sets of $L$-space surgeries on all two-bridge links, providing the first examples of hyperbolic links for which such sets cannot be described as unions of finitely many rectangles in $\mathbb{Q}^2$. The proof relies on several different techniques, each of which is applicable in greater generality: we introduce a sufficient diagrammatic condition for links in $S^3$ to be persistently foliar, a property that implies that every non-trivial surgery on such links supports a coorientable taut foliation. We define a simplified model for the Heegaard Floer homology of rational surgeries on two-component $L$-space links, following the work of Manolescu-Ozsváth, Liu, and Zemke, and use it to obtain obstructions to $L$-space surgeries. Finally, we use explicit computations of Turaev torsions to determine $L$-space surgeries in the case of generalised $L$-space links. Among the consequences of our results, we obtain an optimal uniform bound on the volume of any hyperbolic $L$-space that is surgery on a two-bridge link, together with a classification of all $L$-space satellite knots whose associated two-component pattern link is a two-bridge link.

### [5] [Avalanche homology of digraphs via sandpile dynamics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26786)
*Henri Riihimäki,Jason P. Smith*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于沙堆模型动力学的新型（有向）图同调理论——雪崩同调，它由沙堆动态过程中不稳定顶点集合生成的单纯复形的单纯同调定义。主要结果给出了有向路径和有向环在某些初始配置下雪崩复形的同伦类型，并与有向旗复形及燃烧同调进行了比较，同时引入了持续雪崩同调。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将沙堆模型的动力学过程与代数拓扑工具相结合，发展一种新的图同调理论，以捕捉沙堆动力学中雪崩过程的拓扑特征。

Method: 基于沙堆模型动力学中每一步不稳定顶点集合生成雪崩复形，定义其单纯同调为雪崩同调；进一步利用动力学过程的滤过结构引入持续雪崩同调。

Result: 得到了有向路径和有向环在某些初始配置下雪崩复形的同伦类型，发现即使对于这些简单有向图也能产生丰富的拓扑结构。

Conclusion: 雪崩同调是一种新的图同调理论，能够刻画沙堆动力学的拓扑性质，与有向旗复形和燃烧同调有可比性，且持续版本可追踪动力学演化中的拓扑变化。

Abstract: We introduce avalanche homology as a new (di)graph homology theory, based on the dynamics of the sandpile model. Avalanche homology is the simplicial homology of the avalanche complex generated from the sets of unstable vertices at the time steps of the sandpile dynamics. In this work we focus on digraphs, and our main results give the homotopy types of the avalanche complex for directed paths and directed cycles for certain initial configurations of the sandpile dynamics. Even for such simple digraphs a wide range of topologies can arise, and we compare this to the directed flag complex and to the recently introduced burning homology. Furthermore, the dynamics yields very naturally a filtered simplicial complex, and hence persistent avalanche homology.

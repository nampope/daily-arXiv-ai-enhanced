### [1] [Kirby diagrams for an infinite family of exotic $\mathbb{R}^4$'s](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07684)

Method: 通过为奇数n≥3的T_{2,n}#T_{2,-n}纽结的slice圆盘补空间附加最简单的正Casson手柄CH^+，构造两种无穷族奇异ℝ^4的Kirby图，证明它们等价，然后将构造推广到pretzel纽结P(n,-n,2k)的ribbon圆盘补空间。

### [3] [A Dehornoy-Type Ordering on Plat Presentation Classes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07790)
*Makoto Ozawa*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 使用Dehornoy序在辫群上定义严格全序，应用于链环的桥位置分类，并定义了典范类，将桥有限性猜想重新表述为典范代表的有界性问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究链环的几何位置非常复杂，需要结构化模型。桥位置提供了有限层次的模型来研究链环的几何构型，但需要系统化的排序方法来组织这些桥位置。

Method: 对每个整数n≥1，在辫群B_{2n}上固定适当的复杂度函数，利用Dehornoy序在2n-辫表示类集合P_{2n}=H_{2n}\B_{2n}/H_{2n}上定义严格全序。对于桥数b(L)≤n的链环类型L，这诱导了对应n-桥位置的桥同痕类子集P^{(n)}(L)上的严格全序。定义了典范类CanPlat_D^{(n)}(L)。

Result: 证明了全局选择的Dehornoy典范辫与关联的Hilden双陪集的分陪集典范代表一致。将固定层次桥有限性猜想重新表述为典范代表的有界性问题。

Conclusion: 桥位置作为研究链环几何位置的结构化有限层次模型具有重要作用，Dehornoy序提供了一种系统化的排序方法，典范代表的有界性为研究桥有限性猜想提供了新视角。

Abstract: For each integer $n\ge 1$, after fixing a proper complexity function on the braid group $\B_{2n}$, we use the Dehornoy order to define a strict total order on the set \[ \mathcal P_{2n}=H_{2n}\backslash \B_{2n}/H_{2n} \] of $2n$--plat presentation classes. For a link type $\mathcal L$ with bridge number $b(\mathcal L)\le n$, this induces a strict total order on the subset $\mathcal P^{(n)}(\mathcal L)$ corresponding to bridge isotopy classes of $n$--bridge positions of $\mathcal L$. We also define a distinguished class $\CanPlat_D^{(n)}(\mathcal L)$ and show that the globally chosen Dehornoy canonical braid agrees with the cosetwise canonical representative of the associated Hilden double coset. As an application, we reformulate the fixed-level bridge finiteness conjecture in terms of boundedness of canonical representatives. This viewpoint supports the role of bridge positions as a structured finite-level model for studying the otherwise vast collection of geometric positions of a link.

### [5] [Thurston norm and the Euler class](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08096)
*Mehdi Yazdi*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 讨论3-流形上不同几何结构如何产生Thurston范数对偶单位球中的整点，以及这些结构对Thurston欧拉类猜想的影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Thurston在3-流形上引入了一个范数，并猜想对偶单位球边界上的整点都可以实现为紧叶状结构的欧拉类。本文旨在探讨不同几何结构如何产生这样的整点，并检验该猜想在这些背景下的成立情况。

Method: 通过分析多种几何、拓扑和动力结构：紧叶状结构、紧接触结构、伪Anosov流、拟测地流和基本群上的循环序，研究它们如何产生Thurston范数对偶单位球中的整点。

Result: 讨论了这些不同结构在产生Thurston范数对偶单位球整点方面的关系，以及Thurston欧拉类猜想在这些具体背景下的已知结果和进展。

Conclusion: 多种几何结构都与Thurston范数有深刻联系，Thurston的欧拉类猜想在不同背景下呈现出不同的验证状态，这一猜想仍然是3-流形几何拓扑中的重要开放问题。

Abstract: In his influential work, Thurston introduced a norm on the second homology group of compact orientable 3-manifolds M, which by duality also determines a dual norm on the second cohomology group. A natural question, initiated by Thurston, is whether integral points on the boundary of the dual norm ball have a geometric interpretation. Thurston showed that the Euler class of the oriented tangent plane field to any taut foliation of M lies in the dual unit ball, and conjectured that, conversely, any integral point on the boundary of the dual unit ball is realised as the Euler class of a taut foliation. In this chapter, we discuss how several geometric, topological, and dynamical structures on a 3-manifold give rise to integral points in the dual unit ball of the Thurston norm, and what is known about Thurston's Euler class one conjecture in these contexts. These structures are taut foliations, tight contact structures, pseudo-Anosov flows, quasigeodesic flows, and circular orders on the fundamental group.

### [6] [L-fuzzy simplicial homology](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08166)
*Javier Perera-Lago,Alvaro Torras-Casas,Rocio Gonzalez-Diaz*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文提出L-模糊单纯同调理论，将经典单纯同调推广到L-模糊子复形，其中每个单纯形被赋予完全分配格L中的值，并探讨其在拓扑数据分析中的应用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 经典单纯同调是研究单纯复形拓扑性质的重要工具，但传统方法无法处理模糊性或不精确性。为了将同调理论扩展到模糊设置，需要发展适用于模糊子复形的同调理论，以处理拓扑数据分析中更复杂的数据结构。

Method: 引入L-模糊单纯同调的定义，其中每个单纯形被赋予完全分配格L中的值。建立该理论的主要性质，描述计算其结构的方法。将偏序集上的过滤和彩色数据集解释到这一框架中。

Result: 成功构建了L-模糊单纯同调理论，提供了计算其结构的方法，并展示了如何将偏序集过滤和彩色数据集纳入该框架，为拓扑数据分析开辟了新的应用途径。

Conclusion: L-模糊单纯同调是经典单纯同调的有力推广，能够处理模糊性数据结构，为拓扑数据分析提供了新的理论工具和应用可能性，特别是在处理模糊、不精确或彩色数据集方面具有潜力。

Abstract: Simplicial homology is a classical tool that assigns a sequence of modules to a simplicial complex, providing invariants for the study of its topological properties. In this article, we introduce the notion of L-fuzzy simplicial homology, a generalization of simplicial homology for L-fuzzy subcomplexes, in which each simplex is assigned a value from a completely distributive lattice L. We present its definition and main properties and describe methods to compute its structure. In addition, we interpret filtrations over a poset and chromatic datasets in this setting, opening a door to further applications in topological data analysis.

</details>

### [1] [Knotting and linking in 4 and 5 dimensions from barbell diffeomorphisms](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20130)
*Seungwon Kim,Gheehyun Nahm,Alison Tatsuoka*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文构造了无限多个非等价的5维球面中的3维纽结，每个纽结在标准高度函数下只有4个临界点，这推翻了Scharlemann定理在更高维度的类比，并解决了Budney和Gabai猜想中关于纽结化实心环面的最后一个情况。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究高维纽结理论中的临界点性质，特别是探索Scharlemann定理（2维纽结在4维球面中若有4个临界点则必为平凡纽结）在更高维度是否成立，同时解决Budney和Gabai关于纽结化实心环面存在的猜想。

Method: 使用"杠铃微分同胚"（barbell diffeomorphisms）构造具体的例子，包括纽结化的实心环面、圆盘和球面等，在4维球面、5维球和5维球面中构造各种纽结和链环。

Result: 成功构造了无限多个非等价的5维球面中的3维纽结，每个只有4个临界点；构造了4维球面和5维球中的纽结化实心环面，解决了Budney-Gabai猜想的最后一个情况；扩展了Hughes、Miller等人的工作。

Conclusion: Scharlemann定理不能推广到更高维度，存在无限多个具有4个临界点的非平凡3维纽结；Budney-Gabai猜想得到完全解决；杠铃微分同胚是构造高维纽结的有效工具。

Abstract: In this paper, we construct infinitely many non-isotopic 3-knots in the 5-sphere, each of which has four critical points with respect to the standard height function of the 5-sphere. This contrasts with a theorem of Scharlemann which says that any 2-knot in the 4-sphere with four critical points is unknotted, and also provides infinitely many knotted solid tori in the 4-sphere and 5-ball, which resolves the last remaining case of the conjecture by Budney and Gabai on the existence of knotted handlebodies.
  We also construct various knotted and linked handlebodies, discs, and spheres in the 4-sphere, 5-ball, and 5-sphere, extending recent works of Hughes, Miller, and the first author, and a recent work of the authors. All of our examples are explicit and are constructed using barbell diffeomorphisms.

### [2] [Arboreal Objects and Their Homotopy Theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20140)
*Atabey Kaygun*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 该论文构建了一个有序森林范畴OrdFor作为Δepi的树状扩展，建立了半单纯对象与有序森林上预层之间的伴随关系，并证明了在适当条件下相应的模型结构是Quillen等价的。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究半单纯对象与树状结构之间的关系，通过构建有序森林范畴来扩展半单纯范畴，从而在更一般的范畴设置中研究模型结构。

Method: 构造有序森林范畴OrdFor作为Δepi的树状扩展，定义从OrdFor到Δepi^op的满函子π，建立半单纯对象与OrdFor上预层之间的伴随关系，并分析相应的模型结构。

Result: 证明了半单纯对象与有序森林上预层之间的伴随关系，以及在适当条件下，树状对象的Gabriel-Zisman局部化等价于半单纯对象的局部化，相应的模型结构是Quillen等价的。

Conclusion: 有序森林范畴为研究半单纯对象提供了一个丰富的框架，在模型范畴理论中建立了树状对象与半单纯对象之间的紧密联系，为相关研究提供了新的工具。

Abstract: We construct a category $\OrdFor$ as an arboreal extension of $Δ_{\mathrm{epi}}\subseteqΔ$, whose morphisms are ordered forests composed by grafting. We define a full functor $π\colon \OrdFor\toΔ_{\mathrm{epi}}^{op}$ extracting the semisimplicial shadow. For every complete category $\mathcal C$, this induces a fully faithful functor from semisimplicial objects in $\mathcal C$ to $\mathcal C$-valued presheaves on $\OrdFor$, with right adjoint given by right Kan extension. We show that if weak equivalences of arboreal objects are detected by this right adjoint, then their Gabriel--Zisman localization is equivalent to that of semisimplicial objects. For bicomplete cofibrantly generated model categories, under the usual acyclicity hypothesis for right-induced transfer, the corresponding model structure on arboreal objects is Quillen equivalent to the Reedy model structure on semisimplicial objects.

### [3] [Curve-flat functions and Lipschitz quotients](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20177)
*Jaan Kristjan Kaasik,Andrés Quilis*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 论文证明了对于任何完全度量空间M，存在另一个具有相同密度特征的完全度量空间N，使得N的曲线平坦商等距于M。对于紧致空间，可以构造任意阶的曲线平坦商使其双Lipschitz等价于M。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究度量空间的曲线平坦商结构，探索度量空间通过曲线平坦商变换的可能性，特别是研究紧致度量空间的高阶曲线平坦商性质及其在Lipschitz商普遍性问题中的应用。

Method: 提出了一种新的构造（紧致）度量空间的方法：通过迭代地在可数对点上附加紧致空间到给定（紧致）度量空间的雪花状扭曲上。利用这种构造方法证明关于曲线平坦商的主要结果。

Result: 1. 对于任何完全度量空间M，存在具有相同密度特征的完全度量空间N，使得N的曲线平坦商等距于M。
2. 对于紧致度量空间M和任何可数序数α，存在紧致空间N，使得其α阶曲线平坦商双Lipschitz等价于M，且失真可以任意小。
3. 应用这些结果证明了不存在一个紧致度量空间K，使得每个紧致度量空间都是K的Lipschitz商。

Conclusion: 论文建立了度量空间曲线平坦商的新构造方法，获得了关于曲线平坦商存在性和性质的重要结果，并应用这些结果解决了Lipschitz商普遍性问题，证明了紧致度量空间不存在"通用"Lipschitz商空间，这与Banach空间的情况形成对比。

Abstract: We show that for every complete metric space $M$ there exists another complete metric space $N$ of the same density character such that the curve-flat quotient of $N$ is isometric to $M$. Moreover, we show that if $M$ is compact and $α$ is any countable ordinal, there exists a compact $N$ such that its curve-flat quotient of order $α$ is bi-Lipschitz equivalent to $M$, with arbitrarily small distortion. Our constructions rely on a new method for constructing (compact) metric spaces, which consists in attaching iteratively compact spaces at countably many pairs of points to a snowflake-like distortion of a given (compact) metric space.
  We apply our results on high-order curve-flat quotients to obtain a new result concerning universality of Lipschitz quotients. Specifically, we show that there cannot exist a compact metric space $K$ such that every compact metric space is a Lipschitz quotient of $K$. This result stands in contrast to a theorem of Johnson, Lindenstrauss, Preiss and Schechtman, who showed that any separable Banach space containing $\ell_1$ has every separable geodesic complete metric space as a Lipschitz quotient.

</details>

### [1] [Uniform spectral gap of scl in $2$-orbifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21059)
*Lvzhou Chen,Nicolaus Heuer*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文证明了所有紧致双曲2-orbifolds（除三锥点球面外）关于边缘子群具有统一的稳定换位子长度谱隙1/36，为3-流形中的稳定换位子长度研究提供了基础。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解3-流形中的稳定换位子长度（stable commutator length）需要紧致双曲2-orbifolds的谱隙估计作为基础工具。

Method: 对一般情形使用显式拟同态（quasimorphisms）构造；对三锥点球面这一例外情形则使用双曲几何方法（pleated surfaces，褶曲曲面）。

Result: 除三锥点球面外，所有紧致双曲2-orbifolds关于边缘子群存在统一的谱隙1/36。

Conclusion: 建立了紧致双曲2-orbifolds上稳定换位子长度的统一下界，填补了3-流形研究中所需的关键估计。

Abstract: We show a uniform spectral gap of stable commutator length for all compact hyperbolic $2$-orbifolds relative to the peripheral subgroups. Except for the case of a sphere with three cone points, we have an explicit uniform gap $1/36$. These estimates are needed in understanding stable commutator length in $3$-manifolds. Our methods use explicit quasimorphisms for the generic case, and use hyperbolic geometry (pleated surfaces) for the exceptional case of a sphere with three cone points.

### [2] [A central limit theorem for the signatures of 2-bridge knots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21107)

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文给出了2-桥纽结在给定交叉数$c$和符号差$\sigma$时的数量$s(c,\sigma)$的封闭公式，并证明了当$c$趋于无穷时，2-桥纽结符号差的分布趋近于正态分布。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 此前Cohen等人通过引入$s(c,\sigma)$计算了所有交叉数为$c$的2-桥纽结的签名绝对值平均值，但缺乏该数量的显式封闭公式。本文旨在补全这一缺口。

Method: 推导了$s(c,\sigma)$的封闭公式，并利用该公式分析符号差分布的极限行为。

Result: 得到了$s(c,\sigma)$的封闭表达式，并证明了2-桥纽结符号差分布随交叉数$c\to\infty$趋于正态分布。

Conclusion: 填补了2-桥纽结符号差计数公式的空白，并揭示了其渐近正态性。

Abstract: Cohen, Lowrance, Madras, and Raanes computed the average (absolute value of) signature over all 2-bridge knots with crossing number $c$ by introducing the number $s(c,σ)$ of 2-bridge knots of crossing number $c$ and signature $σ$. Here we provide a closed formula for this number. We use these calculations to show that the distribution of the signatures of 2-bridge knots with crossing number $c$ approaches a normal distribution as $c$ tends to infinity.

<details>

Conclusion: 实链Floer同调是实Heegaard Floer同调与实缝合Heegaard Floer同调的推广，其组合版本为强可逆结的研究提供了有效工具，计算数据揭示了值得进一步探索的现象。

Abstract: In this paper, we define real link Floer homology for strongly invertible and doubly periodic links in closed real $3$-manifolds with connected fixed sets, which generalizes real Heegaard Floer homology and real sutured Heegaard Floer homology. We give a combinatorial description of the theory in $S^3$ via real grid diagrams and use it to investigate structural properties of the theory as well as properties of strongly invertible knots. A computer implementation was written by Zhenkun Li. An appendix including real grid homology for 50+ small knots is made jointly by Zhenkun Li and the author, from which we observe several interesting phenomenon.

### [5] [One-dimensional non-Hausdorff manifolds and CW complexes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21868)
*Igor Vlasenko,Sergiy Maksymenko*

Main category: math.GT

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究一维非Hausdorff流形的结构，特别是其与图（CW复形）之间的关系，并寻找其最小Hausdorff商空间。

Method: 通过定义商映射π，将非Hausdorff点映射到CW复形的顶点，并证明该商空间满足最小Hausdorff商的泛性质。

Result: 在给定条件下，存在商映射将M映到一维开CW复形Γ，且Γ是M的最小Hausdorff商（满足泛性质）。

Conclusion: 一维非Hausdorff流形在一定条件下可被“Hausdorff化”为一个一维开CW复形，且该过程是唯一的、最小的Hausdorff商。

Abstract: This paper studies one-dimensional non-Hausdorff manifolds that are similar to "graphs with split vertices". It is shown that if $M$ is a connected one-dimensional non-Hausdorff manifold such that the set of its "non-Hausdorff" points is locally finite, and each component of its complement has a countable base, then there exists a quotient map $π\colon M \to Γ$ onto an open one-dimensional CW complex, which maps the non-Hausdorff points of $M$ to the vertices of $Γ$.
  Moreover, $Γ$ is the minimal Hausdorff quotient of $M$, that is, for every continuous map $f\colon M \to N$ into a Hausdorff space $N$, there exists a unique continuous map $\hat{f}\colon Γ\to N$ such that $f = \hat{f} \circ π$.

### [6] [Eclipses on Zippers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21792)
*KyeongRo Kim*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究了闭双曲3-流形群边界中"拉链"(zippers)结构的群作用，证明了固定点二分性定理，回答了Calegari和Loukidou提出的问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 回答Calegari和Loukidou关于拉链结构在π₁(M)作用下的固定点行为的开放问题。

Method: 通过分析π₁(M)在极小拉链上的作用，研究非平凡元素的固定点模式，运用双曲3-流形群的几何性质。

Result: 证明了固定点二分性：每个非平凡元素要么在每个树中恰好固定一个点，要么在两个树上都自由作用（无固定点）。作为推论，存在一个元素在每个树中有且仅有一个固定点。

Conclusion: 拉链结构中的群作用具有严格的二分性，这加深了对双曲3-流形边界中不变实树结构的理解。

Abstract: Calegari and Loukidou introduced zippers, consisting of a disjoint pair of invariant real trees in the boundary of a closed hyperbolic 3-manifold group $π_1(M)$, which ensure the existence of a universal circle. We study the action of $π_1(M)$ on a minimal zipper and prove a fixed point dichotomy: every nontrivial element either fixes a unique point in each tree or acts freely on both. This answers a question of Calegari and Loukidou. As a consequence, there exists an element with exactly one fixed point in each tree.

</details>

### [7] [Bounding the density of spherical polygon packings](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21451)
*Fernando Mário de Oliveira Filho,Andreas Spomer,Frank Vallentin*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文通过光滑流形优化确定规则球面多边形的近似最优填充，并利用SO(3)上的Lovász theta数及半定规划证明某些情况下的最大性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了系统性地解决规则球面多边形的填充问题，并建立最大性判据。

Method: 在光滑流形上进行优化；将Lovász theta数推广到SO(3)上的Cayley图；引入代数准则刻画不相交内部条件；利用SO(3)调和分析将theta数化为三角平方和问题，通过半定规划求解。

Result: 确定了若干规则球面多边形的候选最优填充；对部分情形建立了最大性。

Conclusion: 所提方法为球面多边形填充问题提供了统一且可计算的框架，并展示了代数与调和分析工具在此类问题中的有效性。

Abstract: We determine putative optimal packings of regular spherical polygons via optimization on smooth manifolds. For several cases, we establish maximality by extending the Lovász theta number to Cayley graphs on the special orthogonal group ${\rm SO}(3)$. To this end, we introduce an algebraic criterion characterizing when congruent regular spherical polygons have disjoint interiors, leading to a unified formulation of the packing constraints. Using harmonic analysis on ${\rm SO}(3)$, we reduce the theta number to a trigonometric sum-of-squares problem, which can be solved via semidefinite programming.

</details>

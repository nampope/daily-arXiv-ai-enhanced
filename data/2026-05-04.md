### [1] [Aspherical $PD_3$-pairs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00346)
*Jonathan A. Hillman*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文将两个关于aspherical 3-流形的结果推广到具有aspherical ambient space的$PD_3$-pair $(P,\partial{P})$上。每个这样的$PD_3$-pair可以通过将1-柄附着到具有$π_1$-内射边界的$PD_3$-pair上组装而成。此外，若$π$是$FP$型群且其不可分解因子$G_i$满足$χ(G_i)=0$，则仅存在有限多个$π_1(P)\congπ$的$PD_3$-pair。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将aspherical 3-流形的已知结果推广到更一般的$PD_3$-pair $(P,\partial{P})$上，旨在建立这类代数拓扑对象的结构分解与有限性定理。

Method: 通过1-柄附着操作将一般$PD_3$-pair分解为具有$π_1$-内射边界的更简单$PD_3$-pair的组装；利用群的$FP$性质与Euler示性数为零的条件进行有限性论证。

Result: 证明了两个主要结果：(1) 任意具有aspherical ambient space的$PD_3$-pair可由更简单的$PD_3$-pair通过1-柄附着构造；(2) 在特定条件下，满足$π_1(P)\congπ$的$PD_3$-pair仅有有限多个。

Conclusion: 该工作将3-流形的经典结果拓展至$PD_3$-pair框架，为研究这类对偶空间的结构分类与有限性提供了代数化途径。

Abstract: We extend two results known for aspherical 3-manifolds to $PD_3$-pairs $(P,\partial{P})$ with aspherical ambient space $P$. Every such $PD_3$-pair may be assembled by attaching 1-handles to $PD_3$-pairs with aspherical; ambient space and $π_1$-injective boundary. (Thus the study of such pairs reduces to the study of $PD_3$-pairs of groups.) If $π$ is a group of type $FP$ whose indecomposable factors $G$ each have $χ(G_i)=0$ then there are only finitely many such $PD_3$-pairs with $π_1(P)\congπ$.

</details>

### [2] [Scissors automorphism groups II: Solomon-Tits theorems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00541)
*Alexander Kupers,Ezekiel Lemann,Cary Malkiewich,Jeremy Miller,Robin J. Sroka*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文证明了欧氏、双曲或球面几何中由点或超平面生成的测地子空间集合的 Solomon-Tits 定理变体，为后续计算剪刀自同构群同调奠定基础。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 扩展经典的 Solomon-Tits 定理（关于向量子空间复形）到更一般的几何测地子空间场景，以服务于剪刀自同构群的同调计算。

Method: 利用几何方法，考虑欧氏、双曲或球面几何中由点或超平面生成的测地子空间集合，证明其偏序集复形等价于球面的楔形。

Result: 成功证明了在三种几何（欧氏、双曲、球面）中，由点或超平面生成的测地子空间复形具有楔和球面的同伦类型。

Conclusion: 该结果为系列论文的第三部分提供了关键工具，最终将结合同调稳定性定理计算剪刀自同构群的同调。

Abstract: The Solomon-Tits theorem says that the poset of proper non-trivial subspaces of a finite-dimensional vector space has realisation equivalent to a wedge of spheres. In this paper we prove a variant of this result for collections of geodesic subspaces of Euclidean, hyperbolic, or spherical geometry, assuming the collection is generated either by points or by hyperplanes. In the third paper of this series of papers, we will combine this with the homological stability theorems from the first paper to compute the homology of groups of scissors automorphisms in these geometries.

</details>


### [3] [Unbounded Weight Structures: (Re)construction and Completion](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00783)
*Thomas Nikolaus,Phil Pützstück*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文发展了稳定范畴上重量结构的完备性理论（与t-结构的完备性理论对偶），证明了完备重量结构由其重量心唯一确定，并引入弱t-结构的概念，在部分完备的情形下建立从加性范畴出发的两步完备化构造。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立与经典t-结构完备性理论对偶的重量结构完备性理论，处理自然出现的仅部分重量完备的例子（如导出范畴、环谱上的模范畴），并建立从加性范畴到完备重量范畴的普适构造。

Method: (1) 发展重量结构完备性理论，证明完备重量结构由其重量心决定，给出普适构造A↦K(A)；(2) 给出可表现稳定范畴上由小集合生成重量结构的一般构造，推广Bondarko的结果；(3) 引入弱t-结构的概念，处理部分完备情形；(4) 证明在兼容重量结构和弱t-结构且满足左重量完备和右t完备的条件下，可通过两步完备化过程A↦K̂(A)从心重构范畴。

Result: 建立了完备重量结构的理论，给出了由加性范畴构造完备重量范畴的普适方法；在可表现稳定范畴上构造了重量结构（包括谱上的标准重量结构和与Anderson对偶相关的奇特结构）；引入了弱t-结构，并给出了部分完备情形下的两步重构定理。

Conclusion: 重量结构的完备性理论可以与t-结构的完备性形成完美对偶，且弱t-结构的引入有效处理了自然出现的部分完备情形，为稳定范畴的结构分析提供了统一的框架。

Abstract: We develop a theory of completeness for weight structures on stable categories, dual to the theory of complete t-structures. As in the bounded case, we show that complete weight structures are determined by their weight heart, giving rise to a universal construction $A \mapsto K(A)$ that assigns a complete weight category to an additive category and recovers classical examples such as homotopy categories of chain complexes.
  We also give a general construction of weight structures on presentable stable categories generated by a small set of objects, generalizing a result of Bondarko. This recovers the standard weight structure on spectra and an exotic one related to Anderson duality. We identify their completions with Bousfield--Kan completions arising in Adams-type spectral sequences.
  To treat naturally occurring examples - such as derived categories of abelian categories and module categories over ring spectra - which are often only partially weight complete, we introduce the notion of weak t-structures. Within this framework, we prove that any stable category equipped with compatible weight and weak t-structures, and satisfying left weight completeness and right t-completeness, can be reconstructed from its heart via a two-step completion process $A \mapsto \widehat{K}(A)$.

</details>

### [4] [On convex bodies with constant non-central sections](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00299)
*J. Haddad,D. Ryabogin*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文证明了在R^4中，如果一个对称凸旋转体包含单位欧氏球，且所有与单位球相切的超平面截口面积恒为A>0，则该凸体必为欧氏球，前提是某个与A相关的角度值满足特定的连分数算术性质。同时证明满足这些性质的A值集合具有正Hausdorff维数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究对称凸体的截面性质与几何形状之间的关系，特别是通过恒定截面积条件来刻画欧氏球的唯一性。

Method: 利用连分数展开的算术性质来刻画满足条件的面积参数A，结合几何分析与数论方法。

Result: 给出了凸体为欧氏球的充要条件（基于某个角度值的连分数性质），并证明满足该条件的A值集合具有正Hausdorff维数。

Conclusion: 在四维空间中，恒定切截面积条件结合旋转对称性几乎必然迫使凸体是欧氏球，且这种"几乎必然"在测度论意义下是显著的。

Abstract: We prove that if $C$ is a symmetric convex body of revolution in $\mathbb R^4$ containing the unit Euclidean ball $\mathbb B_4$, such that the sections of $C$ by hyperplanes tangent to $\mathbb B_4$ have constant area $A>0$, then $C$ is a Euclidean ball, provided $\frac 1π \arctan((\frac{3A}{4π})^{1/3})$ satisfies certain arithmetic properties that can be read from its expansion as a continued fraction.
  We show that the set of values $A$ satisfying these properties has positive Hausdorff dimension.

</details>

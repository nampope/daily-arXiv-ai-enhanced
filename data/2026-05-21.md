### [1] [Ramification Subgroups of Knot Groups and their Profinite and Cohomological Structure](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20365)
*Marina Palaisti,Federico W. Pasini*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文为纽结外形的有限覆盖建立了分歧理论，定义了经线惯性子群和全局分歧子群，并从有限商、profinite完备化和上同调三个角度进行了分析。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将数论中Galois分歧理论的思想类比到低维拓扑，系统研究纽结群有限覆盖中的分歧现象。

Method: 通过定义经线惯性子群及其正规闭包得到分歧子群，然后从有限商、profinite完备化和上同调三个互补视角进行分析。

Result: (1) U/M_U是"最大经线无分歧"商；(2) profinite完备化中闭分歧子群由闭惯性生成，保经线的同构保持惯性和分歧；(3) "无分歧"的H^1类恰为在惯性子群上消失的类。

Conclusion: 建立了与数论Galois上同调中惯性条件直接类比的上同调刻画，建立了拓扑分歧理论与数论分歧理论之间的形式对应。

Abstract: We formalize a ramification theory for finite covers of knot exteriors. Given a knot group $G_K$ and a finite-index subgroup $U\le G_K$, we define meridional inertia subgroups $U\cap g\langle m\rangle g^{-1}$ and the global ramification subgroup $M_U\triangleleft U$ as their normal closure. We then analyze $M_U$ from three complementary viewpoints:
  (1) finite quotients, where $U/M_U$ is shown to be the universal ``maximal meridionally unramified'' quotient of $U$;
  (2) profinite completions, where we identify the closed ramification subgroup $\widehat M_{\widehat U}$ as the closed normal subgroup generated by closed inertia and prove that meridian-preserving isomorphisms of profinite completions preserve inertia and ramification;
  (3) cohomology, where ``unramified'' $H^1$-classes (discrete and profinite) are characterized as those vanishing on all inertia subgroups, in direct analogy with number-theoretic inertia conditions in Galois cohomology.

### [2] [The Gompf $θ$-Invariant of Canonical Contact Structures via Legendrian Surgery](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21152)
*Mohan Bhupal,Burak Ozbagci*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文对极小负定平展树对应的规范接触结构给出了显式的Legendre手术描述，推导了Seifert纤维化情形下Gompf θ-不变量基于连分数的闭式公式及一般情形的递归公式，并证明该接触结构在可图接触结构中严格极小化θ，进而排除了大量Stein可填充接触有理同调球的有理辛球填充。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究极小负定平展树Γ对应的规范接触结构ξ_can，希望给出其显式的Legendre手术描述，并计算其Gompf θ-不变量，进而探讨接触结构的极小性与有理辛球填充的存在性问题。

Method: 利用Legendre手术描述联系平展树与接触结构；在Seifert纤维化情形使用Hirzebruch-Jung连分数展开推导θ-不变量的闭式公式；对一般平展树建立递归的叶到根公式；通过θ-不变量的极小性分析排除有理辛球填充。

Result: 给出了ξ_can作为唯一一致可图接触结构的Legendre手术描述；导出了Seifert纤维化情形下θ-不变量的闭式公式（涵盖透镜空间、二面体流形、小Seifert纤维化等已知结果）；建立了一般情形的递归公式；证明了ξ_can在所有可图接触结构中严格极小化θ；基于此排除了大量Stein可填充接触有理同调球的有理辛球填充。

Conclusion: 规范接触结构具有可图性下的最优θ-不变量，这一性质可用于限制有理辛球填充的存在性，为接触几何与奇点理论的交叉提供了新工具。

Abstract: Let $Γ$ be a minimal connected negative-definite plumbing tree with all vertices of genus zero, and let $Y_Γ$ be the oriented link of the corresponding normal complex surface singularity, equipped with its canonical contact structure $ξ_{\rm can}$. We give an explicit Legendrian surgery description of $ξ_{\rm can}$, showing that it is the unique consistent diagram-realizable contact structure on $Y_Γ$, up to isomorphism. We then derive a closed-form formula for Gompf's $θ$-invariant of $ξ_{\rm can}$ in the Seifert fibered case, expressed purely in terms of the Hirzebruch--Jung continued fraction expansions of the normalized Seifert invariants, and prove a recursive leaf-to-root formula for arbitrary plumbing trees. The Seifert formula recovers previously known formulas for lens spaces, dihedral manifolds, and small Seifert fibered spaces with complementary legs, and agrees with the Némethi--Nicolaescu expression via the classical Hirzebruch--Zagier identity. As a final application we show that $ξ_{\rm can}$ strictly minimizes $θ$ among all diagram-realizable contact structures on $Y_Γ$, and we use this to rule out symplectic rational homology ball fillings for a large class of Stein fillable contact rational homology $3$-spheres.

</details>


### [3] [Flow loops and quantum groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21382)
*Sunghyuk Park*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文建立了量子群不变量与Morse流动力学之间的新联系，针对纤维化纽结定义了双变量级数不变量，并证明它与BPS q-级数在辫同质纽结上等价。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 连接量子群不变量（代数方法）与Morse流动力学（几何/动力系统方法）这两个看似独立的纽结研究途径。

Method: 对纤维化纽结，通过计数补空间中的Morse流环来定义双变量级数不变量；提出该动力学级数与来自量子群Verma模的BPS q-级数等价的猜想；并在辫同质纽结上证明该对应关系。

Result: 建立了动力学级数与BPS q-级数之间的对应关系，并在辫同质纽结类上给出了严格证明。

Conclusion: 该工作揭示了量子群不变量与Morse流动力学之间的深层联系，为理解彩色Jones多项式提供了新的几何/动力学视角，并在非平凡的一类纽结上得到了验证。

Abstract: This paper connects two seemingly different ways of studying knots: quantum group invariants and the dynamics of Morse flows. For fibered knots, we define a two-variable series invariant by counting Morse flow loops in the complement. This dynamical series is conjectured to agree with the BPS $q$-series of the knot complement, which arises from Verma modules for quantum groups and encodes all colored Jones polynomials. We prove this correspondence for all braid-homogeneous knots.

### [4] [Topological Field Theories and the Algebraic Structures of the Two-Sphere](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20846)
*Chris Li*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文在三维有向配边范畴中给出了S^2配边的两种表示，定义了P-幺半群和L-幺半群。它们都是交换Frobenius幺半群，分别带有由闭可约素三维流形标记的自同态和单位态射。两者等价，且满足与交换Frobenius结构相关的“legs关系”。在代数限制下，legs关系迫使素自同态通过乘法作用，使结构变得简单。作者还提出了编码这些素结构的∞-operad。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了理解三维配边范畴中S^2的代数结构，并揭示闭可约素三维流形在拓扑场论中如何编码新的代数结构（如P-幺半群和L-幺半群）。

Method: 通过给出S^2在三维有向配边范畴中的两种表示，引入P-幺半群（带素自同态）和L-幺半群（带单位态射），证明它们等价，并推导legs关系。进一步限制到代数范畴，分析legs关系对素自同态的约束。最后构造一个包含无穷小3-立方operad的∞-operad。

Result: 证明了P-幺半群与L-幺半群等价；legs关系在代数限制下迫使素自同态退化为乘以素单位的简单作用；提出的∞-operad编码了这些素结构。

Conclusion: 本文揭示了三维配边范畴中S^2的代数结构可由标记素三维流形的自同态或单位态射刻画，且这些结构在代数层面高度简化，为理解三维TFT的分类提供了新视角。

Abstract: We give two presentations for bordisms of $S^2$ in the 3-dimensional oriented bordism category $\operatorname{Cob}(3) $, encoding the algebraic structures on $S^2$. After passing through topological field theories, we define two kinds of monoids which we call P-monoids and L-monoids. In addition to both being commutative Frobenius monoids, P-monoids are equipped with a class of endomorphisms while L-monoids are equipped with a class of unit morphisms, all of which are labelled by closed oriented irreducible prime 3-manifolds. They turn out to be equivalent. The new prime structures satisfy some countable relations with the commutative Frobenius structure, the most notable of which we call "legs relations." We then restrict to the setting of algebras and show that the legs relations place strong constraints on the new prime endomorphisms which forces them to act by multiplications by prime units, rendering the additional prime structures remarkably simple. We also propose an $\infty$-operad which encodes these prime structures and contains the $\infty$-little 3-cube operad as a sub-operad.% We briefly discuss the relations between P/L-algebras and J-algebras which classify 3-dimensional TFTs.

</details>

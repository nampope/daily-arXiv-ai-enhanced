### [1] [Convergence of cataclysm deformations on Anosov representations and applications](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05313)
*Hongtaek Jung*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究了Anosov表示的灾变变形，证明了当扭曲横向余环弱收敛时，对应的灾变变形在紧集上一致收敛，并由此推广了Goldman乘积公式，同时证明了在特定条件下强稠密Hitchin表示在Hitchin分量中非开集。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为Anosov表示的研究提供更强大的工具，特别是理解灾变变形的收敛性质，并探究强稠密表示在Hitchin分量中的拓扑性质。

Method: 通过分析扭曲横向余环的弱收敛性，建立灾变变形在Anosov表示空间上的一致收敛性，进而应用该收敛结果推导Goldman乘积公式的推广及强稠密Hitchin表示的非开集性。

Result: 证明了扭曲横向余环的弱收敛性蕴含灾变变形在紧集上的一致收敛；推广了Goldman乘积公式；证明了当Weyl群包含-1时，强稠密G-Hitchin表示集在G-Hitchin分量中不是开集。

Conclusion: 灾变变形是研究Anosov表示的有效工具，其收敛性良好，可用于推广经典公式和揭示Hitchin分量的拓扑结构。

Abstract: A cataclysm deformation, that shears and twists a given Anosov representation according to data known as a twisted transverse cocycle, is an intuitive and powerful tool for studying Anosov representations. We show that if a sequence of twisted transverse cocycles converges weakly, the sequence of corresponding cataclysm deformations on the space of Anosov representations converges uniformly on compact sets. This result leads to two applications. First, we obtain an extension of the Goldman product formula. Second, we consider strongly dense representations, introduced by Breuillard--Green--Guralnick--Tao and Long--Reid. Using cataclysm deformations, we show that, for a split real form $\mathsf{G}$ whose Weyl group contains $-1$, the set of strongly dense $\mathsf{G}$-Hitchin representations is not open in the $\mathsf{G}$-Hitchin component.

### [2] [Exotic disks and singular instanton Floer homology](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05819)
*Irving Dai,Abhishek Mallick,Masaki Taniguchi*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文利用带陈-西蒙斯滤过的奇异瞬子Floer同调，构造了异种切片盘对，并进一步构造了一个强可逆的Z-切片纽结，其任意对称Z-盘对都是异种的，且在多种稳定化操作下保持异种性。此外，给出了一个强可逆纽结是Z-切片且等变切片，但非等变Z-切片的例子，并通过分析二桥纽结的无迹SU(2)-特征簇，部分计算了奇异瞬子Floer复形上的对称作用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 构造并证明存在异种（exotic）切片盘对，特别是在高维稳定化操作下仍保持异种性的例子，以及区分等变切片与等变Z-切片的概念。

Method: 利用带陈-西蒙斯滤过的奇异瞬子Floer同调理论，分析二桥纽结的无迹SU(2)-特征簇，计算对称群在Floer复形上的作用。

Result: 构造了在n个CP²、n个\overline{CP}²、n个RP²或-n个RP²（以及更一般的单连确定流形）稳定化下仍保持异种性的对称Z-盘对；给出了一个非等变Z-切片的纽结构造；部分计算了二桥纽结的对称作用。

Conclusion: 奇异瞬子Floer同调是研究切片盘异种性和等变切片问题的有效工具，能处理多种稳定化操作，揭示了几何拓扑中对称性与切片性的深层联系。

Abstract: We show that singular instanton Floer homology with the Chern--Simons filtration can be used to produce exotic pairs of slice disks. We moreover construct a strongly invertible $\mathbb{Z}$-slice knot for which any symmetric pair of $\mathbb{Z}$-disks are exotic, and remain exotic after stabilizing by $n\smash{\mathbb{CP}}^2$ or $n\smash{\overline{\mathbb{CP}}}^2$ (or by standard $n\smash{\mathbb{RP}}^2$ or $-n\smash{\mathbb{RP}}^2$) for any $n$. Our methods apply more generally to stabilization by any simply connected definite manifold, or by any number of exotic embedded projective planes of the same sign. We also provide an example of a strongly invertible knot which is $\mathbb{Z}$-slice and equivariantly slice, but not equivariantly $\mathbb{Z}$-slice. Along the way, we partially compute various symmetry actions on the singular instanton Floer complexes of two-bridge knots via an explicit analysis of their traceless $\mathit{SU}(2)$-character varieties.

### [4] [Smooth stable isotopy of topologically isotopic surfaces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06299)
*Daniel Galvin,Patrick Orson,Mark Powell*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究4维流形中的光滑曲面在同痕意义下的稳定性。若两个光滑曲面在4维流形X中拓扑同痕，探讨它们是否在X的某个稳定化（即与若干$S^2\times S^2$做连通和）中光滑同痕。证明了当曲面在$\mathbb{Z}/2$同调中平凡时该结论成立，并给出了一类使得结论成立的基群（包括经典纽结群的自由积及自由群）。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在4维流形中，拓扑同痕与光滑同痕之间的关系是一个核心问题。通过稳定化（添加$S^2\times S^2$）来降低问题的难度，从而在更弱的条件下建立光滑同痕的存在性。

Method: 利用4维流形的稳定化技术，结合$\mathbb{Z}/2$同调条件和基群结构的分析，证明在特定条件下拓扑同痕的曲面在稳定化后光滑同痕。

Result: 证明了当曲面在$\mathbb{Z}/2$同调中平凡时，拓扑同痕的曲面在稳定化后光滑同痕；同时给出了一个包含经典纽结群自由积和自由群的大类基群使得结论成立。

Conclusion: 稳定化可以有效解决4维流形中曲面光滑同痕性问题，且该结论在较弱的同调条件和较广的基群类下成立。

Abstract: A stabilisation of a $4$-manifold $X$ is the connected sum of $X$ with some number of copies of $S^2\times S^2$. If two smooth surfaces in a $4$-manifold are topologically isotopic, we investigate whether they must moreover be smoothly isotopic in some stabilisation of $X$. We prove this result holds whenever the surfaces are trivial in the $\mathbb{Z}/2$-homology of $X$. We also produce a large class of fundamental groups of the ambient $4$-manifold for which the result holds; this class includes free products of classical knot groups and, in particular, free groups.

</details>

*Aled Williams*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种关于原点对称凸体的连续极小与商覆盖半径的尖锐混合不等式，该不等式在经典覆盖密度下界与Minkowski第二定理下界之间进行插值。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 作者试图建立一种新的不等式，能够将凸体几何中两个经典的下界（覆盖密度下界和Minkowski第二定理下界）统一起来，填补两者之间的理论空白。

Method: 通过研究原点对称凸体相对于格点的连续极小与商覆盖半径之间的关系，推导出一个混合不等式，该不等式在两个经典下界之间实现平滑插值。

Result: 得到了一个尖锐的混合不等式，成功地在覆盖密度下界与Minkowski第二定理下界之间建立了联系。

Abstract: In this paper we present a sharp mixed inequality relating successive minima and quotient covering radii of origin-symmetric convex bodies with respect to lattices. The inequality interpolates between the (classical) covering-density lower bound and the lower bound in Minkowski's second theorem.

</details>

### [1] [The prime decomposition fibre sequence for moduli spaces of reducible 3-manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02099)
*Rachael Boyd,Corey Bregman,Jan Steinebrunner*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了可约3-流形M的微分同胚群分类空间B Diff⁺(M)，通过构造分裂映射建立了与不可约素因子分解相关的纤维序列，并计算了具体例子的有理上同调环。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究可约3-流形微分同胚群分类空间的同伦类型，特别是如何通过不可约素因子的微分同胚群来理解整体结构，这是3-流形拓扑中的重要问题。

Method: 构造从B Diff⁺(M)到B Diff⁺(P₁⊔...⊔Pₙ)的分裂映射，得到素分解纤维序列。纤维H_g(P₁,...,Pₙ)描述为1-手柄附着空间，可表示为不可约素因子上某些构型空间的同伦余极限。

Result: 证明了当n>0时纤维等价于有限连通胞腔复形；将该纤维序列作为有效计算工具，计算了(S¹×S²)²的B Diff⁺的有理上同调环。

Conclusion: 通过构造分裂映射和素分解纤维序列，为研究可约3-流形微分同胚群分类空间提供了新的几何理解和计算方法，将复杂问题分解为更简单的不可约因子问题。

Abstract: We study the moduli space $B\textrm{Diff}^+(M)$, for $M$ a reducible, oriented 3-manifold with irreducible prime factors $P_1,\ldots,P_n$. A programme of César de Sá-Rourke, Hendriks-Laudenbach, and Hendriks-McCullough studies the homotopy type of $\textrm{Diff}^+(M)$ in terms of the $\textrm{Diff}^+(P_i)$. Inspired by a delooping proposed by Hatcher, we construct a map from $B\textrm{Diff}^+(M)$ to $B\textrm{Diff}^+(P_1 \sqcup \dots \sqcup P_n)$, called the splitting map, that yields a prime decomposition fibre sequence. The fibre $H_g(P_1, \dots, P_n)$ is a space of $1$-handle attachments which we describe geometrically as a homotopy colimit of certain configuration spaces on the $P_i$. Firstly, this allows us to show that for $n>0$ the fibre is equivalent to a finite, connected cell complex. Secondly, this makes the fibre sequence an effective tool for computations, which we illustrate by computing the rational cohomology ring of $B\textrm{Diff}^+\!\left((S^1\times S^2)^{\sharp 2}\right)$.

### [2] [Dehn filling and the knot group II: Ubiquity of persistent elements](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01697)
*Tetsuya Ito,Kimihiko Motegi,Masakazu Teragaito*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究了纽结群中的"持久元素"——在所有非平凡Dehn填充下保持非平凡性的群元素。证明了每个非平凡纽结都存在无限多个持久元素，且对于一大类双曲纽结，持久元素在群结构中普遍存在。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究纽结群中在所有Dehn填充下保持非平凡性的元素。Property P猜想（已由Kronheimer和Mrowka解决）表明纽结的经线是第一个这样的持久元素，本文旨在探索是否存在更多这样的元素及其在群结构中的普遍性。

Method: 基于Kronheimer和Mrowka解决的Property P猜想，通过群论和双曲几何方法分析纽结群结构。特别关注那些不存在手术导致流形基本群有挠的双曲纽结类。

Result: 1. 每个非平凡纽结群都有无限多个持久元素，且这些元素的自动轨道两两不相交，都不包含经线的幂。
2. 对于一大类双曲纽结（不存在手术导致流形基本群有挠），持久元素在群结构中普遍存在：
   (i) 任何不被包含在周边元素正规闭包中的子群都包含持久元素
   (ii) 持久元素存在于每个真子群之外

Conclusion: 持久元素不是稀有的奇特性，而是纽结群结构中的普遍特征。对于一大类双曲纽结，持久元素在群结构中广泛存在，反映了纽结群在Dehn填充下的稳定性特征。

Abstract: Let $K$ be a nontrivial knot in $S^3$. We say that an element of the knot group $G(K)$ is \textit{persistent} if it remains nontrivial under all nontrivial Dehn fillings. Such elements exist for every nontrivial knot. Indeed, Property P is equivalent to the statement that the meridian of $K$ is a persistent element, and this represents the first instance of such elements. Building on the solution to the Property P conjecture due to Kronheimer and Mrowka, we show that every nontrivial knot group admits infinitely many persistent elements with pairwise disjoint automorphic orbits, none of which contains a power of the meridian. We then develop this further to show that for a broad class of hyperbolic knots - namely those admitting no surgery whose resulting manifold has torsion in its fundamental group - persistent elements are not rare curiosities, but rather structurally pervasive in $G(K)$. This is reflected in the following two properties:
  (i) Every subgroup of $G(K)$ that is not contained in the normal closure of a peripheral element contains persistent elements.
  (ii) Persistent elements exist outside every proper subgroup of $G(K)$.

### [5] [On the universality of multiexcisive functors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02232)
*Tobias Barthel,Kaif Hilman,Nikolay Konovalov*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 该论文将谱的多项式自函子的乘法分类与Mackey函子的交叉效应联系起来，建立了多变量切除函子范畴与谱Mackey函子变体的对称幺半等价关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究谱的多项式自函子的分类问题，特别是通过Mackey函子的视角来理解切除函子的结构，为Goodwillie微积分提供新的理论框架。

Method: 引入称为"子对角函子"的多变量函子，利用参数化范畴论方法，通过归纳证明这些函子都具有Mackey函子描述，并赋予它们对称幺半范畴结构和普遍性质。

Result: 证明了多变量切除函子范畴与谱Mackey函子变体之间的对称幺半等价，强化了Glasman关于谱的d-切除自函子的结果，并在素数d的情况下证明了Goodwillie微积分中的"Segal猜想"。

Conclusion: 该工作建立了谱的多项式自函子与Mackey函子之间的深刻联系，为Goodwillie微积分提供了新的视角和工具，特别是在素数阶的情况下证明了重要的Segal型结果。

Abstract: We provide a multiplicative classification of polynomial endofunctors on spectra in terms of their Mackey functors of cross--effects. More precisely, we prove that various categories of multivariable excisive functors from spectra to spectra are symmetric monoidally equivalent to the corresponding variants of spectral Mackey functors. The symmetric monoidal structures appearing here are the Day convolutions on both sides, and the Mackey functors we consider involve variations on the category of finite sets and surjections. The method is first to introduce certain multivariable functors we call subdiagonal functors. By considering them all at once using parametrised category theory, we prove inductively that they all admit Mackey functor descriptions as symmetric monoidal categories, endowing them with a universal property along the way. In particular, specialising this to univariate functors gives a new proof and strengthening of Glasman's result about d-excisive endofunctors on spectra. As application of our perspective, we prove a ``Segal conjecture'' in the context of Goodwillie calculus when d is a prime number.

### [6] [Self perimeter of convex sets](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01950)
*Gershon Wolansky*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文为n维赋范空间中的单位球引入了一个自然的体积定义，保持欧几里得关系中周长与体积的比例P(B)/V(B)=n，并研究了该定义的仿射不变性、对偶性及相关的Alexandrov型问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在赋范空间中，如何定义单位球的体积使其保持欧几里得几何中周长与体积的比例关系P(B)/V(B)=n，并研究该定义的数学性质。

Method: 1. 提出满足P(B)/V(B)=n的体积定义；2. 证明该定义在原点保持仿射变换和极对偶下的不变性；3. 对n=2推导单位球自周长的显式积分公式并推广到非中心对称集；4. 通过边界递归积分将构造扩展到ℝⁿ；5. 提出并讨论相关表面积测度的Alexandrov型问题。

Result: 1. 建立了满足欧几里得比例关系的体积定义；2. 证明了该定义的仿射不变性和极对偶不变性；3. 获得了二维情况下的显式积分公式；4. 将构造推广到高维空间；5. 在二维情况下证明了表面测度的扰动通常产生四重对称凸集。

Conclusion: 本文成功为赋范空间中的单位球建立了自然的体积定义，该定义保持了欧几里得几何的基本比例关系，具有良好的不变性，并为相关的Alexandrov型问题提供了初步解，特别是在二维情况下揭示了扰动产生的四重对称性。

Abstract: This paper introduces a natural definition for the volume of the unit ball in $n$-dimensional normed spaces $\mathbb{R}^n$. This definition preserves the Euclidean relation $P(B)/V(B)=n$ between the perimiter and the volume of the unit ball $B$ in $R^n$.
  We show that this volume definition is invariant under origin-preserving affine transformations and polar duality. For $n=2$, we derive an explicit integral formula for the self-perimeter of the unit ball, extend it to non-centrally symmetric sets;. The construction is extended to $\mathbb{R}^n$ via a recursive integration over the boundary, utilizing $(n-1)$-dimensional volumes of planar intersections. Finally, we pose and discuss an Alexandrov-type problem for the associated surface measure, providing perturbative solutions in the 2D case. In particular we prove that, generically, any perturbation of the surface measure of the Euclidean 2-D disk yields a 4-fold symmetric convex set in the leading order.

</details>

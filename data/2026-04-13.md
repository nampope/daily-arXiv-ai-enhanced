Motivation: 研究强可逆结的等变解结数，建立其与反变Bar-Natan同调的联系，寻找传统解结数与等变解结数不同的具体例子

Method: 利用反变Bar-Natan同调理论，定义H-扭阶，证明其对等变解结数的下界约束，并通过计算验证具体结的例子

Abstract: We demonstrate that the equivariant unknotting number $\widetilde{u}(K)$ of a strongly invertible knot $K$ is bounded below by the $H$-torsion order $\widetilde{\mathrm{ord}}(K)$ of the involutive Bar-Natan homology $\widetilde{\mathrm{BN}}(K)$. This result serves as an equivariant analogue to the bound established by Alishahi. As an application, we identify five strongly invertible prime knots with crossing numbers at most $9$ for which the strict inequality $u(K) < \widetilde{u}(K)$ holds.

### [2] [A Neukirch-Uchida Theorem for 3-Manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.09469)
*Nadav Gropper,Jun Ueki,Yi Wang*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文证明了三维流形中绝对伽罗瓦群的拓扑类似物，建立了算术拓扑中的Neukirch-Uchida定理类比：两个三维球面分支覆盖同胚当且仅当其绝对伽罗瓦群通过特征保持同构同构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将数论中的Neukirch-Uchida定理（绝对伽罗瓦群决定数域同构）推广到三维流形的算术拓扑框架中，建立数论与拓扑之间的深刻对应关系。

Method: 在三维流形中研究具有Chebotarev密度性质的无限链环（类似素数集），定义三维流形的绝对伽罗瓦群为有限子链环补集的投射极限，利用无限覆盖的Hilbert分歧理论和局部-全局原理。

Result: 证明了两个三维球面在稳定Chebotarev链环上的分支覆盖同胚当且仅当其绝对伽罗瓦群通过特征保持同构同构，为链环作为素数拓扑类似物提供了系统论证。

Conclusion: 成功将数论关键思想翻译到拓扑中，建立了算术拓扑中的Neukirch-Uchida定理类比，为Chebotarev链环作为素数精确拓扑类似物提供了理论基础。

Abstract: The classical Neukirch-Uchida theorem states that the absolute Galois group determines a number field up to isomorphism. We prove an analogue of this theorem for 3-manifolds in the framework of arithmetic topology. We study infinite links in 3-manifolds that behave like the set of primes, satisfying a Chebotarev density property. Relative to such a stably Chebotarev link, we define the absolute Galois group of a 3-manifold as the inverse limit of profinite completions of finite sublink complements. Our main result shows that two branched covers of the three-sphere over a stably Chebotarev link are homeomorphic if and only if their absolute Galois groups are isomorphic via a characteristic-preserving isomorphism. The proof translates the key ideas from the number-theoretic argument into topology, relying on Hilbert ramification theory for infinite covers and local-global principles. In doing so, it also provides a systematic justification for viewing Chebotarev links as the precise topological analogue of prime numbers in anabelian geometry. In addition, we discuss further conditions for links to play the role of prime numbers

### [3] [Cross effects for functors from posets](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.09264)
*Bjørnar Gullikstad Hem*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 建立了函子演算与多持久性模射影维数之间的精确关系，提出了新的函子演算概念，并给出了射影维数不超过n-1和n-2的充要条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立函子演算与多持久性模射影维数之间的精确关系，为理解多参数持久性模的结构提供新的理论工具。

Method: 开发了从偏序集到函子的新函子演算概念，检测立方体的消失总纤维；给出了这种函子演算的通用逼近函子的显式构造。

Result: 证明了两个新定理：为n参数多持久性模的射影维数不超过n-1和不超过n-2提供了必要且充分的条件。

Conclusion: 成功建立了函子演算与多持久性模射影维数之间的精确对应关系，为多参数持久性模的代数结构分析提供了新的理论框架。

Abstract: We establish a precise relationship between functor calculus and the projective dimension of multipersistence modules. Specifically, we develop a new notion of functor calculus for functors from posets, which detects vanishing total fibers of cubes. We give an explicit construction of the universal approximation functors of this functor calculus. We then use these approximations to prove two new theorems, providing necessary and sufficient conditions for an $n$-parameter multipersistence module to have projective dimension at most $n-1$ and at most $n-2$.

</details>

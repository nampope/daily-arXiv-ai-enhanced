Motivation: 锥多面体框架已被证明是刚性的，但其精确的刚性阶数尚未确定。本文旨在确定其刚性阶数，证明其是二阶刚性（预应力稳定）。

Method: 引入Wachspress应力；通过证明Schlenker-Souam提出的向量值Schläfli型公式，将应力-柔性猜想在Wachspress应力情形下转化为对偶形式，从而建立预应力稳定性。对该广义Schläfli公式给出了纯离散几何的新证明。

Result: 证明了锥多面体框架是预应力稳定的，即二阶刚性的；解决了Wachspress应力情形下的应力-柔性猜想。

Conclusion: Wachspress应力是分析锥多面体框架刚性的有效工具，向量值Schläfli公式在其证明中起关键作用，且该公式的纯离散几何证明丰富了相关理论。

Abstract: A coned polytope framework (CPF) is the bar-joint framework obtained from the 1-skeleton of a convex polytope by coning over some interior point. It was recently shown that CPFs are rigid, though the exact order of rigidity remained open. In this paper we introduce the Wachspress stress and use it to show that CPFs are prestress stable, in particular, second-order rigid. To this end, we resolve the stress-flex conjecture in the case of the Wachspress stress by identifying its dual formulation as a corollary of a vector-valued Schläfli-type formula introduced by Schlenker and Souam. We give a new and purely discrete-geometric proof of this generalized Schläfli formula.

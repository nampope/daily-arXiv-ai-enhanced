### [1] [On quasi-holomorphic homotopies of immersions of 3-manifolds into 5-manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26820)
*András Csépai*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究三维流形到五维流形的浸入的拟全伦同伦，该概念通过允许同伦经过孤立奇点（交叉帽）推广了正则同伦，并完整描述了该同伦下补空间基本群（纽结群）的变化规律，证明三维球面标准嵌入的五维球面中某些拟全伦同伦不改变其纽结群。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 推广正则同伦概念，允许同伦路径经过带有孤立奇点的映射（交叉帽/复Whitney伞），从而探索浸入在更高维空间中的拓扑变换及其对纽结群的影响。

Method: 通过分析拟全伦同伦的局部形式，结合从复二维到复三维的全纯映射芽理论，研究该同伦过程中补空间基本群的变化机制。

Result: 完整描述了三维流形浸入五维流形时，其补空间基本群在拟全伦同伦下的变化规律；并证明三维球面标准浸入五维球面的某些拟全伦同伦不改变纽结群。

Conclusion: 拟全伦同伦作为正则同伦的推广，在允许经过交叉帽奇点的情况下，对纽结群的影响具有可描述的结构，且某些情况下该同伦保持纽结群不变。

Abstract: The notion of a quasi-holomorphic homotopy of two immersions of a $3$-manifold into a $5$-manifold extends the notion of their regular homotopy by also allowing the homotopy to pass through instances of maps with an isolated singularity around which the path of the homotopy forms a cross-cap (complex Whitney umbrella). We describe the local form of such homotopies and explain connections with the theory of holomorphic map germs from $\mathbb{C}^2$ to $\mathbb{C}^3$. Our main result is a complete a description of how the fundamental group of the complement of the image of an immersion of a $3$-manifold into a $5$-manifold (i.e. its knot group) changes under a quasi-holomorphic homotopy. As a corollary we will see that certain quasi-holomorphic homotopies of the standard embedding of the $3$-sphere into the $5$-sphere do not change its knot group.

### [4] [On L-space surgeries on two-bridge links](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27145)
*Diego Santoro,Hugo Zhou*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文分类了所有双桥链环上的L-空间 surgeries 的集合，首次给出了双曲链环的例子，其 surgeries 集合不能用 Q² 中有限个矩形的并集来描述。证明综合运用了多种技术，包括持续性叶状结构的图条件、有理 surgeries 的 Heegaard Floer 同调简化模型，以及 Turaev 挠率的计算。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 1) 提出持续性叶状结构的充分图解条件；2) 建立有理 surgeries 的 Heegaard Floer 同调简化模型；3) 利用 Turaev 挠率进行显式计算。

Result: 首次发现双曲链环的 L-空间 surgeries 集合不能用 Q² 中有限个矩形的并集描述；得到双曲 L-空间体积的最优一致上界；分类了相关的 L-空间卫星结。

Conclusion: 给出了双桥链环 L-空间 surgeries 的完整分类，揭示了双曲情形下 surgeries 集合的复杂结构，并建立了新的不变量与几何性质之间的深刻联系。

Abstract: We classify the sets of $L$-space surgeries on all two-bridge links, providing the first examples of hyperbolic links for which such sets cannot be described as unions of finitely many rectangles in $\mathbb{Q}^2$. The proof relies on several different techniques, each of which is applicable in greater generality: we introduce a sufficient diagrammatic condition for links in $S^3$ to be persistently foliar, a property that implies that every non-trivial surgery on such links supports a coorientable taut foliation. We define a simplified model for the Heegaard Floer homology of rational surgeries on two-component $L$-space links, following the work of Manolescu-Ozsváth, Liu, and Zemke, and use it to obtain obstructions to $L$-space surgeries. Finally, we use explicit computations of Turaev torsions to determine $L$-space surgeries in the case of generalised $L$-space links. Among the consequences of our results, we obtain an optimal uniform bound on the volume of any hyperbolic $L$-space that is surgery on a two-bridge link, together with a classification of all $L$-space satellite knots whose associated two-component pattern link is a two-bridge link.

*Henri Riihimäki,Jason P. Smith*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于沙堆模型动力学的新型(有向)图同调理论——雪崩同调，并研究了有向路径和有向环的雪崩复形的同伦类型。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将沙堆模型的动力学过程与代数拓扑工具相结合，发展一种新的图同调理论，以揭示沙堆动力学过程中产生的拓扑结构。

Method: 定义雪崩复形（由沙堆动力学时间步中不稳定顶点集合生成的单纯复形），并研究其单纯同调；对有向路径和有向环在不同初始构型下计算雪崩复形的同伦类型。

Result: 给出了有向路径和有向环在不同初始构型下雪崩复形的同伦类型，发现即使对于这些简单图也能产生丰富的拓扑结构；并与有向旗复形和燃烧同调进行了比较。

Avalanche homology is the simplicial homology of the avalanche complex generated from the sets of unstable vertices at the time steps of the sandpile dynamics. Even for such simple digraphs a wide range of topologies can arise, and we compare this to the directed flag complex and to the recently introduced burning homology. Furthermore, the dynamics yields very naturally a filtered simplicial complex, and hence persistent avalanche homology.

*J. Mainik*

Main category: math.MG

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 高斯证明了正n边形可尺规作图的充分条件（n=2^m n_1…n_l，其中n_k为费马素数），但未证明必要性。Wantzel在1837年完成了必要性的证明。本文旨在提供Wantzel定理的新证明。

Gauss also knew that only these regular polygons can be constructed but did not prove it.\linebreak P. Wantzel completed the result of Gauss and proved it in 1837. The present paper provides a new proof for Wantzel's theorem.

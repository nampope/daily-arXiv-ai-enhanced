On the other hand, we show that a hyperbolic geodesic sampled with respect to a natural measure on the sphere spends an asymptotically negligible proportion of its time close to a fiber. For a more restricted class of circle measures, namely the Lebesgue measure and stationary measures from geometric random walks on the surface group, we also prove an effective result for the proportion of time spent close to a fiber.

### [8] [Quantitative stability of the intersection body operator near the ball, and the dynamical origin of the two--dimensional degeneracy](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09412)

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Milman-Shabelman-Yehudayoff定理解决了IB² K = cK仅当K为中心椭球体的定性刚性结论（以及IB K = cK仅当K为球的长期猜想），本文希望在球邻域内给出定量的稳定性分析，并解释为什么二维平面是例外。

The gap degenerates precisely as $n\to 2^+$, giving a transparent \emph{dynamical} explanation of the well--known exceptional status of the plane, where $\IB K = 2K$ for every origin--symmetric star body. We also record the reduced normal form of $\IB$ on the ellipsoidal directions and observe that the centered ellipsoids constitute a normally attracting invariant manifold for the shape under iterated intersection bodies. The methods are perturbative and do not address the global periodic problem $\IB^m K = cK$ for $m\ge 3$, which we discuss.

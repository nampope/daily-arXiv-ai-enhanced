### [1] [Six-dimensional GKM manifolds with four fixed points](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16225)
*Donghoon Jang,Shintaro Kuroki,Mikiya Masuda,Takashi Sato*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文对具有4个不动点的6维GKM流形进行分类，识别出6种类型，并构造了对应的流形实例。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究6维GKM流形在具有4个不动点情况下的分类问题，探索可能的GKM图类型及其对应的几何实现。

Method: 首先对所有可能的GKM图进行分类，然后为每种图类型构造具体的流形实例，证明其存在性。

Result: 发现了6种不同的类型：P1（标准复结构的CP³）、P2（S⁶在不动点处的爆破）、P3（非标准近复结构的CP³）、Q1（标准复结构的复二次曲面Q₃）、Q2（S⁶沿等变2-球面的爆破）、S（S²×S⁴）。

Conclusion: 6维具有4个不动点的GKM流形共有6种类型，每种类型都有具体的几何实现，完成了该分类问题的研究。

Abstract: In this paper, we study $6$-dimensional GKM manifolds with $4$ fixed points. We classify all possible GKM graphs, and for each type of graph we construct a manifold, proving the existence. We show that six types occur.
  (P1) complex projective space $\mathbb{C} P^3$ with standard complex structure
  (P2) blow up of $S^6$ at a fixed point, diffeomorphic to $\mathbb{C} P^3$
  (P3) $\mathbb{C} P^3$ as the homogeneous space $\mathrm{Sp}(2)/(\mathrm{U}(1) \times \mathrm{Sp}(1))$ with non-standard almost complex structure
  (Q1) complex quadric $Q_3$ with standard complex structure
  (Q2) blow up of $S^6$ along isotropy $2$-sphere, diffeomorphic to $Q_3$
  (S) $S^2 \times S^4$, obtained as equivariant gluing along orbits of two $S^6$'s

### [5] [A refinement of the Pontryagin-Thom theorem for unstable Thom spectra and its applications](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16270)
*Naoki Kuroda*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 该论文改进了Pontryagin-Thom构造，为不稳定Thom谱的同伦群提供了更明确的几何解释，并计算了先前未知的配边群。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Pontryagin-Thom构造在配边群和Thom谱同伦群之间建立了基本联系，但需要更明确的几何解释。作者希望改进这一构造，为不稳定Thom谱的同伦群提供更清晰的几何意义，并计算一些先前未知的配边群。

Method: 1. 改进Pontryagin-Thom构造，为不稳定Thom谱的同伦群提供更明确的几何解释；2. 利用改进的结果，将先前未知的配边群表示为不稳定Thom谱的同伦群；3. 使用Smith同态计算这些群。

Result: 1. 成功改进了Pontryagin-Thom构造，为不稳定Thom谱的同伦群提供了更明确的几何解释；2. 确定了哪些n值存在具有边界S^n的Spin流形，且该流形具有与边界正交的线子丛；3. 精确刻画了Bais、May Custodio和Torres引入的配边群。

Conclusion: 该研究深化了对Pontryagin-Thom构造的理解，为不稳定Thom谱的同伦群提供了更明确的几何解释，并成功计算了先前未知的配边群，在代数拓扑和微分拓扑领域具有重要应用价值。

Abstract: The Pontryagin-Thom construction provides a fundamental link between cobordism groups and the homotopy groups of Thom spectra. Our main result refines this theorem, providing a more explicit geometric interpretation of the homotopy groups of unstable Thom spectra. Building on this result, we show that previously unknown cobordism groups can be expressed as homotopy groups of unstable Thom spectra. Furthermore, using the Smith homomorphism, we compute these groups. As applications, we determine the values of $n$ for which there exists a Spin manifold with boundary $S^n$ admitting a line subbundle orthogonal to the boundary, and provide a precise characterization of the cobordism group introduced by Bais, May Custodio, and Torres.

### [6] [Quantum Cellular Automata: The Group, the Space, and the Spectrum](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16572)
*Mattie Ji,Bowen Yang*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 该论文在任意交换环上发展了量子元胞自动机理论，利用代数K理论构造了度量空间X上的量子元胞自动机空间Q(X)，并建立了与Ω谱相关的同伦等价关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究量子元胞自动机在任意交换环上的理论框架，建立系统的分类方法，特别是对欧几里得格点上的QCA进行分类。

Method: 使用代数K理论构造量子元胞自动机空间Q(X)，通过同伦等价关系Q(*) ≃ Ω^n Q(ℤ^n)建立Ω谱结构，并扩展到C*代数上的QCA。

Result: 证明了QCA空间构成Ω谱，从而对欧几里得格点上的QCA进行了系统分类；同时获得了Azumaya R-代数K理论的非连通delooping。

Conclusion: 该工作建立了量子元胞自动机的统一理论框架，通过Ω谱结构提供了系统分类方法，相关结果对代数K理论和量子信息理论都有重要意义。

Abstract: Over an arbitrary commutative ring $R$, we develop a theory of quantum cellular automata. We then use algebraic K-theory to construct a space $\mathbf{Q}(X)$ of quantum cellular automata (QCA) on a given metric space $X$. In most cases of interest, $π_0 \mathbf{Q}(X)$ classifies QCA up to quantum circuits and stabilization. Notably, the QCA spaces are related by homotopy equivalences $\mathbf{Q}(*) \simeq Ω^n \mathbf{Q}(\mathbb{Z}^n)$ for all $n$, which shows that the classification of QCA on Euclidean lattices is given by an $Ω$-spectrum indexed by the dimension $n$. As a corollary, we also obtain a non-connective delooping of the K-theory of Azumaya $R$-algebras, which may be of independent interests. We also include a section leading to the $Ω$-spectrum for QCA over $C^*$-algebras with unitary circuits.

</details>

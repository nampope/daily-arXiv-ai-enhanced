Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文证明了纤维化映射的纤维化THH转移在有理同伦论中可以用Sullivan模型的Hochschild同调转移来建模。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究纤维化映射的纤维化THH（拓扑Hochschild同调）转移的有理模型，为后续研究Becker-Gottlieb转移和流形拓扑应用奠定基础。

Method: 分两步：首先使用高阶范畴迹的机制在参数化谱内部计算纤维化THH转移；然后利用Braunack-Mayer的工作，通过Sullivan代数上的模来有理建模参数化谱。

Result: 证明了当纤维化映射的纤维是单连通且有限支配时，其纤维化THH转移作为参数化谱上的映射，在有理意义上可以用Sullivan模型的Hochschild同调转移来建模。

Abstract: Given a map $f$ of fibrations over a space $B$ such that the fiber of $f$ is simply connected and finitely dominated, we prove that its fiberwise THH transfer, considered as a map of parametrized spectra over $B$, is rationally modeled by the Hochschild homology transfer of a Sullivan model of $f$. The proof goes in two steps. Firstly, we use the machinery of higher categorical traces to show that the fiberwise THH transfer can be computed internally to parametrized spectra. Secondly, we model the resulting description rationally using work of Braunack-Mayer, who proved that parametrized spectra can be modeled by modules over Sullivan algebras. In Part II, we will use our result to obtain a rational model of the Becker--Gottlieb transfer, and for applications to manifold topology.

### [8] [On the Inscribed Sphere and Concurrent Lines through the Centers of Apollonius Spheres in $\mathbb{R}^n$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.03067)
*Miłosz Płatek*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 该论文研究n维欧氏空间中n+2个球体的阿波罗尼奥斯问题，发现所有子集解的中心连线交于一点P_X，并推广了森田定理到任意球体配置。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 阿波罗尼奥斯问题传统上研究n+1个给定球体或超平面的相切球体解，但主要关注孤立配置。本文旨在研究来自同一球体族的阿波罗尼奥斯解之间的关系，特别是在n+2个球体配置下所有n+1子集的解之间的几何关系。

Method: 采用李球几何框架，分析n+2个球体配置的所有n+1子集对应的阿波罗尼奥斯解。首先证明所有解对中心连线交于一点P_X，然后通过两步构造更多阿波罗尼奥斯球体，并证明它们的中心连线也通过P_X。

This problem has been widely studied for an isolated configuration of $n+1$ spheres. More precisely, we consider a configuration of $n+2$ spheres in $\mathbb{R}^n$ and the solutions of the Apollonius problem corresponding to all its subsets of size $n+1$. Morita's three-dimensional theorem on the inscribed sphere in a configuration of mutually tangent spheres. We show that Morita's theorem is a special case of our result for an arbitrary configuration of $n+2$ spheres in $\mathbb{R}^n$, not necessarily mutually tangent. Moreover, we connect this result with the preceding ones by proving that the center of the corresponding inscribed sphere is again the point $P_X$.

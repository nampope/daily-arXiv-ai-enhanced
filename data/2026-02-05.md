### [1] [Doubly slicing knots and embedding 3-manifolds in 4-manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04334)
*Se-Goo Kim,Taehee Kim*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文将双切片亏格的概念从4-球面推广到任意单连通闭4-流形，证明了存在代数双切片且ribbon的纽结，其X-双切片亏格可以任意大，并引入了新的L^2-符号不变量和双稳定数等概念。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统上双切片亏格的研究局限于4-球面，本文旨在将这一概念推广到更一般的单连通闭4-流形，探索纽结在不同4-流形中的嵌入性质，并建立相应的不变量理论。

Method: 1. 定义X-双切片亏格作为4-球面双切片亏格的推广；2. 使用新的L^2-符号不变量来阻碍具有无限循环一维同调的闭3-流形嵌入到具有无限循环基本群的闭4-流形中；3. 将超切片亏格概念推广到单连通4-流形；4. 引入双稳定数作为Conway-Nagel稳定数的推广。

Result: 1. 对于任意整数n，存在代数双切片且ribbon的纽结K，其X-双切片亏格大于n；2. 存在双切片纽结，其广义超切片亏格可以任意大；3. 纽结的双稳定数也可以任意大。

Conclusion: 该研究成功将双切片理论从4-球面推广到一般单连通4-流形，建立了新的不变量和阻碍理论，揭示了纽结在不同4-流形中的嵌入复杂性，为低维拓扑学提供了新的工具和视角。

Abstract: For a knot $K$ in the 3-sphere and a simply connected closed 4-manifold $X$, we define the $X$-double slice genus of $K$, extending the notion from the case when $X$ is the 4-sphere. We show that for each integer $n$, there exists an algebraically doubly slice and ribbon knot $K$ whose $X$-double slice genus is greater than $n$. Our arguments use new $L^2$-signature obstructions to embedding closed 3-manifolds with infinite cyclic first homology into closed 4-manifolds with infinite cyclic fundamental group, in a way that preserves first homology. We also extend the concept of the superslice genus of a knot to simply connected 4-manifolds and show that there exist doubly slice knots whose generalized superslice genera are arbitrarily large. Furthermore, we define the double stabilizing number of a knot, extending the stabilizing number introduced by Conway and Nagel, and show that this invariant can also be arbitrarily large.

### [2] [Twisted torus links that are unlinks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04382)
*Hong Chang,Thiago de Paiva,Qing Lan*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文对扭曲环面链环T(p,q,r,s)进行了分类，确定了哪些参数组合会使链环成为平凡链环（unlink）


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 扭曲环面链环是通过在(p,q)-环面链环的r个相邻链上进行s次完全扭曲得到的。研究这些链环何时成为平凡链环（即可以完全解开为不相交的简单闭曲线）是一个自然的拓扑问题，有助于理解这类链环的结构特性。

Method: 作者对扭曲环面链环的参数(p,q,r,s)进行系统分析，使用代数拓扑和纽结理论的方法，推导出使链环成为平凡链环的必要和充分条件。

Result: 论文给出了扭曲环面链环成为平凡链环的完整分类，确定了所有使T(p,q,r,s)成为unlink的参数组合(p,q,r,s)。

Conclusion: 该研究完全刻画了扭曲环面链环成为平凡链环的参数条件，为这类链环的拓扑分类提供了重要结果。

Abstract: A twisted torus link $T(p,q,r,s)$ is obtained by performing $s$ full twists on $r$ adjacent strands of the $(p,q)$-torus link. In this paper, we classify twisted torus links that are unlinks. We give a complete characterization of all parameter families $(p,q,r,s)$ for which the associated twisted torus link is an unlink.

</details>

### [3] [L^2-Betti numbers in prime characteristic and a conjecture of Wise](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04655)

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 系统研究零特征和素数特征下的L^2-Betti数，应用于Wise猜想：有限2-复形的所有塔都是非正的当且仅当第二个L^2-Betti数为零。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究L^2-Betti数在不同特征下的性质，特别是为了验证Wise关于有限2-复形塔结构的猜想。

Method: 系统研究零特征和素数特征下的L^2-Betti数理论，分析其代数拓扑性质，并将其应用于有限2-复形的塔结构分析。

Result: 建立了L^2-Betti数在不同特征下的理论框架，为验证Wise猜想提供了数学工具。

Conclusion: L^2-Betti数的系统研究为理解有限2-复形的塔结构提供了重要工具，支持了Wise猜想的验证工作。

Abstract: We systematically study L^2-Betti numbers in zero and prime characteristic and apply them to a conjecture of Wise stating that all towers of a finite 2-complex are non-positive if and only if the second L^2-Betti number vanishes.

### [4] [Quasi-isometric modification of Gromov-Hausdorff distance](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04826)
*Alexei Naianzin*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 提出一种类似Gromov-Hausdorff距离的新距离概念，用于比较任意拟等距空间，并研究该距离下保持的性质和度量空间类的特性


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有Gromov-Hausdorff距离主要用于比较紧度量空间，需要一种能够比较任意拟等距空间的距离概念，以扩展度量空间比较的理论框架

Method: 引入拟等距畸变概念用于对应关系，定义类似Gromov-Hausdorff距离的新距离，研究该距离下的极限性质

Result: 证明所有度量空间类在该距离下是路径连通的，任意两个度量空间可以通过有限长度曲线连接

Conclusion: 成功构建了适用于任意拟等距空间比较的距离理论，为度量空间分类和比较提供了新的数学工具

Abstract: We define a distance analogous to the Gromov-Hausdorff distance that enables the comparison of arbitrary quasi-isometric spaces. We also investigate properties preserved under limits with respect to this distance, as well as properties of the entire class of metric spaces equipped with this distance. For this purpose, we introduce the notion of quasi-isometric distortion for correspondences. Using this notion, we prove that the class of all metric spaces is path-connected; in fact, any two metric spaces can be connected by a curve of finite length.

</details>

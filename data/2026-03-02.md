<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 利用先前工作中引入的Khovanov多曲线不变量，应用强地理限制条件，并借助同调镜像对称的思想对该不变量的分支进行完全分类

Abstract: We show that reduced Khovanov homology over any field is invariant under component-preserving Conway mutation. Our proof relies on strong geography restrictions for a certain Khovanov multicurve invariant associated with Conway tangles that we introduced in previous work [arXiv:1910.14584]. Applying ideas from homological mirror symmetry, we give a full classification of the components of this invariant.

### [2] [A Rigidity Theorem for Convex Sets in Hyperbolic 3-Space](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23558)
*Feng Luo,Yanwen Luo,Zhenghao Rao*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了双曲3维空间中非紧致闭凸集的刚性定理，证明了当凸集在无穷远处的极限点集具有零长度时，边界上的内在路径度量可以唯一确定凸集（相差一个等距）。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究双曲3维空间中非紧致闭凸集的刚性定理，扩展Pogorelov关于紧致凸体的刚性定理到非紧致情形，建立类似于复分析中Painlevé可去性定理的几何对应。

Method: 使用Pogorelov的紧致凸体刚性定理、Pogorelov映射以及Tabor-Tabor关于局部凸函数延拓的定理，通过分析凸集在无穷远处的极限点集的Hausdorff测度来证明刚性结果。

Result: 证明了当凸集在无穷远处的极限点集具有零1维Hausdorff测度（即零长度）时，边界上的内在路径度量可以唯一确定凸集（相差一个等距），且该零长度条件是最优的。

Conclusion: 建立了双曲3维空间中非紧致闭凸集的刚性定理，为几何分析和复分析之间建立了新的联系，并得到了关于抛物型连通多面曲面离散共形映射的推论。

Abstract: Pogorelov's rigidity theorem states that a compact convex body in the hyperbolic 3-space is determined up to isometry by the intrinsic path metric on its boundary. The main result of this paper addresses a rigidity problem for non-compact closed convex 3-dimensional subsets in hyperbolic 3-space. We show that the intrinsic path metric on the boundary determines a closed convex set up to isometry, provided that the set of limit points of the convex set at infinity of the hyperbolic 3-space has vanishing 1-dimensional Hausdorff measure, i.e., zero length. Furthermore, this zero-length condition is optimal. This can be considered as an analogue of the Painlevé removability theorem in complex analysis, which states that sets of zero length are removable for bounded holomorphic functions. As a corollary, we show that if the underlying complex structure of a connected polyhedral surface is of parabolic type, then it is discrete conformal, unique up to scaling, to a complete flat surface marked with a discrete subset. The proof uses Pogorelov's rigidity theorem for compact convex bodies in $\mathbb R^3$, the Pogorelov map, and the Tabor--Tabor theorem on the extension of locally convex functions.

</details>

*Marissa Chesser*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 证明了在多弧曲线图和柄体群作用中，loxodromic元素等价于其某纯幂在见证上为伪Anosov映射


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Masur和Minsky关于映射类群在曲线图上loxodromic元素（即伪Anosov映射）的结果推广到更一般的几何情形

Method: 通过分析子群在可容许多弧曲线图上的作用，以及柄体群在圆盘图上的作用，建立loxodromic元素与伪Anosov性质之间的等价关系

Result: 证明了在多弧曲线图和柄体群作用中，loxodromic元素恰好是那些存在纯幂在某个见证上为伪Anosov映射的元素

Conclusion: 该结果将Masur-Minsky定理推广到更广泛的几何背景下，为理解这些几何结构中的双曲行为提供了统一框架

Abstract: In this paper, we prove that for subgroups acting on admissible multiarc and curve graphs and for the handlebody group acting on the disk graph, the loxodromic elements are exactly those for which some pure power is a pseudo-Anosov on a witness. This generalizes the result of Masur and Minsky that the elements of the mapping class group that act loxodromically on the curve graph are the pseudo-Anosov elements.

### [4] [On invariants of representations of Weyl groups associated with the cohomology of toric varieties](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23631)
*Tao Gong*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 论文证明了Weyl群W-置换多面体P及其商P/W_K对应的环面簇上同调代数同构，并推广到中间格、有限Coxeter群和非退化W-对称多面体，回答了Horiguchi等人的两个公开问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Weyl群W-置换多面体P及其商P/W_K对应的环面簇的上同调代数结构，探索它们之间的同构关系，并推广到更一般的几何和代数结构。

Method: 构造了从H^*(X_{P/W_K};Q)到H^*(X_P;Q)^{W_K}的显式代数同构，然后推广到中间格、有限Coxeter群和非退化W-对称多面体的情况。

Result: 成功建立了环面簇上同调代数的同构关系，并证明了该同构在多种推广情况下仍然成立，回答了Horiguchi等人的两个公开问题。

Conclusion: Weyl群W-置换多面体及其商对应的环面簇上同调代数存在显式同构，这一结果可以推广到更广泛的几何和代数结构，为相关领域的研究提供了新的工具和视角。

Abstract: For a Weyl group $W$ and a $W$-permutohedron $P$, there are associated toric varieties $X_P$ and $X_{P/W_K}$ for any parabolic subgroup $W_K$ of $W$, since the quotient $P/W_K$ can be identified with a polytope inside $P$. We construct an explicit algebra isomorphism between $H^*(X_{P/W_K};\mathbb{Q})$ and $H^*(X_P;\mathbb{Q})^{W_K}$. We further generalize this isomorphism to intermediate lattices, to finite Coxeter groups, and to non-degenerate $W$-symmetric polytopes. Our results give affirmative answers to two open questions of Horiguchi--Masuda--Shareshain--Song.

</details>

### [6] [Local minimality of the truncated octahedron for the isoperimetric problem on parallelohedra](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.27221)
*Annalisa Cesaroni,Matteo Novaga*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 研究三维晶格Voronoi胞的等周问题，推导出尺度不变等周商F的显式闭式公式，证明体心立方晶格(BCC)是固定体积下的严格局部极小值点，而面心立方晶格(FCC)和简单立方晶格(SC)不是局部极小值点


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究三维晶格Voronoi胞的等周性质，探索不同晶格结构在等周问题中的优化特性，特别是BCC、FCC和SC等常见晶格配置的局部和全局行为

Method: 使用Selling参数推导尺度不变等周商F的显式闭式公式，分析F在关键晶格配置（BCC、FCC、SC）的局部行为，并研究BCC到FCC插值晶格族中F的全局最小化特性

Result: BCC是固定体积下F的严格局部极小值点，FCC和SC不是局部极小值点；在BCC到FCC的插值晶格族中，BCC是F的全局极小值点

Conclusion: 体心立方晶格(BCC)在三维晶格Voronoi胞的等周问题中表现出优越的优化特性，是局部且在某些族中全局最优的配置，而面心立方晶格(FCC)和简单立方晶格(SC)则不具备这种优化性质

Abstract: We investigate the isoperimetric problem for the Voronoi cells of three-dimensional lattices. Using Selling parameters, we derive an explicit closed formula for the scale-invariant isoperimetric quotient $F$ in terms of six non-negative variables. We then analyse the local behaviour of $F$ at the most relevant lattice configurations: we prove that the body-centered cubic lattice (BCC) is a strict local minimiser of $F$ at fixed volume, whereas the face-centered cubic lattice (FCC) and the simple cubic lattice (SC) are not local minimisers. Then, we consider a family of lattices which interpolates between BCC and FCC, showing that BCC is the global minimiser of $F$ restricted to this family.

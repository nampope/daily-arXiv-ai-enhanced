### [1] [Cheeger Inequalities for the Persistent Laplacian](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02846)
*Magnus Bakke Botnan,Rui Dong*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文研究单纯复形包含关系 $\mathcal{K}\hookrightarrow \mathcal{L}$ 的持久Laplacian的Cheeger型不等式，引入持久上$p$-Laplacian，证明Cheeger不等式，并在局部完全$q$-骨架、可定向伪流形及图包含三种特殊设定下扩展了相关结果。

Motivation: 将Cheeger型不等式从固定复形推广到持久（persistent）设定下，即考虑单纯复形包含 $\mathcal{K}\hookrightarrow \mathcal{L}$ 时的谱与拓扑不变量之间的关系，建立持久Laplacian的谱与持久Cheeger常数之间的桥梁。

Method: 1) 定义持久上$p$-Laplacian $\Delta_{q,p,\mathrm{up}}^{\mathcal{K},\mathcal{L}}$（$p\geq 1$），$p=1$时得到非零持久Cheeger常数 $\varphi_q^{\mathcal{K},\mathcal{L}}$；2) 在局部完全$q$-骨架假设下推广完整骨架等周不等式；3) 对可定向$(q+1)$-维伪流形，通过Kron约化将持久上Laplacian约化为顶点-边加权图Laplacian（可能带Dirichlet边界），得到双侧Cheeger不等式；4) 对图包含 $H\hookrightarrow G$ 比较两种Cheeger常数。

Result: 证明了持久Cheeger常数 $\varphi_q^{\mathcal{K},\mathcal{L}}$ 与 $\Delta_{q,\mathrm{up}}^{\mathcal{K},\mathcal{L}}$ 的最小非零特征值之间的Cheeger型不等式；扩展了Parzanchevski-Rosenthal-Tessler的等周不等式到持久设定；对伪流形得到Kron约化及双侧Cheeger不等式；在非分支伪流形情形给出了 $\varphi_q^{\mathcal{K},\mathcal{L}}$ 关于对偶图的显式表达；与Mémoli等人的Cheeger常数进行了比较。

Conclusion: 成功将Cheeger型不等式推广到持久Laplacian框架下，建立了持久拓扑分析与谱图理论之间的新联系，并为不同设定下的持久Cheeger常数提供了统一理解和计算方法。

Abstract: We study Cheeger-type inequalities for persistent Laplacians associated with inclusions of simplicial complexes $\mathcal{K}\hookrightarrow \mathcal{L}$. We introduce a persistent up $p$-Laplacian $Δ_{q,p,\mathrm{up}}^{\mathcal{K},\mathcal{L}}$ for $p\geq 1$. For $p=2$, this recovers the usual persistent up Laplacian, while for $p=1$ it yields a nonzero persistent Cheeger constant $\varphi_q^{\mathcal{K},\mathcal{L}}$. We prove a Cheeger-type inequality relating $\varphi_q^{\mathcal{K},\mathcal{L}}$ to the smallest nonzero eigenvalue of $Δ_{q,\mathrm{up}}^{\mathcal{K},\mathcal{L}}$. This gives a persistent extension of recent work by Jost and Zhang (Ann. Sc. Norm. Super. Pisa Cl. Sci., 2024; arXiv:2302.01069).
  We then study two more structured settings. Under a locally complete $q$-skeleton assumption on $\mathcal{K}$, we extend the complete-skeleton isoperimetric inequality of Parzanchevski--Rosenthal--Tessler (Combinatorica, 2016; arXiv:1207.0638) to the persistent setting. For orientable $(q+1)$-dimensional pseudomanifolds, we prove a Kron-type reduction of the persistent up Laplacian to a vertex- and edge-weighted graph Laplacian, possibly with Dirichlet boundary terms, and obtain two-sided Cheeger inequalities; this is related to the dual-graph perspective in the work of Steenbergen--Klivans--Mukherjee (Adv. Appl. Math., 2014; arXiv:1209.5091). We also describe the nonzero persistent Cheeger constant $\varphi_q^{\mathcal{K},\mathcal{L}}$ explicitly in terms of the dual graph in the non-branching pseudomanifold case. Finally, for graph inclusions $H\hookrightarrow G$, we compare the persistent Cheeger constants introduced here with the Kron-reduction Cheeger constants of Mémoli et al. (SIAM J. Math. Data Sci., 2022; arXiv:2012.02808).

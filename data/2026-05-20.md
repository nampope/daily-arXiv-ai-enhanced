### [1] [Planar multilinks and rational singularities](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18994)
*Márton Beke,Olga Plamenevskaya*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究了纤维化多重链环（fibered multilinks）作为经典纤维化结和开书结构的推广，证明了若曲面奇点链环上的典范切触结构可由平面多重链环开书支持，则该奇点必定是有理奇点；夹层奇点（sandwiched singularities）可通过存在多重数为1的分量的平面多重链环来刻画。此外，将平面开书的若干拓扑性质推广至平面多重链环，并利用Min–Roy–Wang关于平面脊柱开书填充的工作及格嵌入组合学获得上述奇点结论。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究曲面奇点与Milnor纤维化中自然出现的纤维化多重链环（fibered multilinks）这一推广结构，探究其切触拓扑性质与奇点代数性质之间的深刻联系。

Method: 综合运用切触拓扑（平面开书、平面脊柱开书的填充理论）、Heegaard Floer同调（接触不变量的像）、格嵌入组合学，以及Min–Roy–Wang等人的理论工具进行分析。

Result: (1) 若曲面奇点链接上的典范切触结构被平面多重链环开书支持，则该奇点必为有理奇点；(2) 夹层奇点恰好由具有多重数为1分量的平面多重链环刻画；(3) 平面多重链环的辛填充是负定的，且不能包含正亏格的辛曲面；(4) Heegaard Floer接触不变量的像落在$HF_{red}$中消失。

Conclusion: 建立了平面多重链环开书与有理奇点、夹层奇点之间的精确对应关系，并将平面开书的若干关键拓扑性质（辛填充的负定性、Heegaard Floer不变量的消失等）成功推广到多重链环情形，为曲面奇点的切触拓扑刻画提供了新工具。

Abstract: Fibered multilinks are a generalization of classical fibered knots and open books that arise in the study of surface singularities and Milnor fibrations. We prove that if the canonical contact structure on the link of a surface singularity is supported by a planar multilink open book, then the singularity must be rational, and that sandwiched singularities are characterized by admitting planar multilinks with a component of multiplicity 1. We also show that some topological properties of planar open books extend to planar multilinks: symplectic fillings are negative definite and cannot contain symplectic surfaces of positive genus, and the image of the Heegaard Floer contact invariant vanishes in $HF_{red}$. Our results for singularities are based on these topological considerations, partly using Min--Roy--Wang's work on fillings of planar spinal open books, as well as the combinatorics of lattice embeddings.

### [2] [Gang-Kim-Yoon integrality conjectures on adjoint Reidemeister torsions for torus knots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19460)
*Yuji Terashima,Yoshikazu Yamaguchi*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究并证明了关于环面结的伴随Reidemeister挠率(g-1)次方之和为整数的猜想，通过引入Verlinde数并利用模S矩阵的递推公式完成了证明，同时还给出了环面结特征簇的双有理模型。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 验证环面结的伴随Reidemeister挠率(g-1)次方之和是否为整数的猜想，并建立相关理论框架。

Method: 引入环面结的Verlinde数（从模S矩阵角度），推导其递推公式和初值，利用这些递推公式证明积分猜想；同时构造特征簇的双有理模型，从定义该模型的Hessian矩阵恢复伴随Reidemeister挠率。

Result: 证明了对于任意环面结和非负整数g，伴随Reidemeister挠率(g-1)次方之和为整数。

Conclusion: 环面结的Verlinde数递推公式成功证明了积分猜想，双有理模型为从模S矩阵视角理解挠率提供了新途径。

Abstract: We study the conjecture that a sum of the (g-1)st powers of adjoint Reidemeister torsions for a torus knot is an integer. We prove that the conjecture is true for any torus knot and all non-negative g. To prove the conjecture, we introduce the Verlinde numbers for torus knots from the viewpoint of modular S-matrix and show the recursion formulas and initial values of them. The recursion formulas of Verlinde numbers prove the integrality of the sum of the (g-1)st powers of adjoint Reidemeister torsions. Related to a modular S-matrix, we also provide a birational model of the character variety for a torus knot and show how to recover the adjoint Reidemeister torsion for a torus knot from the Hessian of the polynomial defining the birational model.

### [3] [Secondary Ext of the Fiber of $Sq^n$ and the Secondary Adams Spectral Sequence](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18906)
*Dang Vo Phuc*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文在素数2处计算了Baues意义下的二级Steenrod代数$\mathcal{B}$的三个二级Ext群，对应于Bruner考虑的三种纤维的二级上同调对象，并给出了显式结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了理解二级同调代数中的微分结构，特别是将Bruner关于纤维的$E_3$计算推广到二级Steenrod代数范畴，并建立与经典Adams谱序列中$d_2$微分的二级对应。

Method: 利用Bruner的$E_3$计算与Baues--Jibladze比较定理，通过显式计算映射$D_n, D_{n\mathbb{Z}}, D_F$的核与余核，得到二级Ext群的同构分类。

Result: 给出了三个二级Ext群的精确代数结构：$F_n$对应$\mathbb{F}_2 \oplus \Sigma^{1,n}\mathbb{F}_2$；$F_{n\mathbb{Z}}$对应$\mathbb{F}_2[h_0] \oplus \Sigma^{1,n}\mathbb{F}_2$；$F$对应$\mathbb{F}_2[h_0] \oplus \bigoplus_{j>0} \Sigma^{1,2^j}\mathbb{F}_2 \oplus \bigoplus_{i>0, i\neq 2^k} \Sigma^{0,2i-1}\mathbb{F}_2$。

Conclusion: 建立了二级同调代数与经典Adams微分的联系，表明Yoneda意义下的Adams $d_2$是二级同调代数中第一个微分的初级投影。

Abstract: At the prime $2$, let $\mathcal{B}$ denote the secondary Steenrod algebra in the sense of Baues, Baues--Jibladze, Nassau, and Baues--Frankland. We determine the secondary Ext groups of the secondary cohomology objects of the three fibers considered by Bruner in his work on the fiber of $\mathrm{Sq}^n$. For $$F_n = \mathrm{fib}(H \xrightarrow{\mathrm{Sq}^n} Σ^n H),\,\, \text{and}\,\, F_{n\mathbb{Z}} = \mathrm{fib}(H\mathbb{Z} \xrightarrow{\mathrm{Sq}^n} Σ^n H),$$ where $n>0$ in the first case and $n \ge 2$ in the second, and for $$F = \mathrm{fib}(H\mathbb{Z} \to \prod_{i>0} Σ^{2i} H),$$ where the $i$-th component is represented after mod-$2$ reduction by $\mathrm{Sq}^{2i}$, the groups are $$\mathrm{Ext}_{\mathcal{B}}^{*,*}(H^*_{\mathcal{B}} F_n, \mathbb{F}_2) \cong \mathbb{F}_2 \oplus Σ^{1,n}\mathbb{F}_2,$$ $$\mathrm{Ext}_{\mathcal{B}}^{*,*}(H^*_{\mathcal{B}} F_{n\mathbb{Z}}, \mathbb{F}_2) \cong \mathbb{F}_2[h_0] \oplus Σ^{1,n}\mathbb{F}_2,$$ and $$\mathrm{Ext}_{\mathcal{B}}^{*,*}(H^*_{\mathcal{B}} F, \mathbb{F}_2) \cong \mathbb{F}_2[h_0] \oplus \bigoplus_{j>0} Σ^{1,2^j}\mathbb{F}_2 \oplus \bigoplus_{i>0, i \neq 2^k} Σ^{0,2i-1}\mathbb{F}_2$$ (where the last direct sum runs over $i$ not a power of $2$). Here $h_0$ has bidegree $(1,1)$. The group-level calculation follows from Bruner's $E_3$-calculation and the Baues--Jibladze comparison theorem, and the proof gives an explicit primary calculation of the kernels and cokernels of the maps $D_n$, $D_{n\mathbb{Z}}$, and $D_F$. Separately, under an explicit track-fiber realization hypothesis, the same groups are computed from displayed secondary mapping fibers. Under the same hypothesis, the Bruner--Rognes Yoneda description of the ordinary Adams $d_2$ is the primary shadow of the first differential in secondary homological algebra.

</details>

### [4] [Reducing the upper bound for the Borsuk number in $\mathbb{R}^4$ to 8](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19068)
*Alexander Tolmachev,Vsevolod Voronov*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文通过构造拉萨克覆盖变体的8部分划分，证明了四维欧氏空间的Borsuk数b(4)≤8，将此前已知的b(4)≤9上界改进为8。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 改进四维欧氏空间Borsuk数b(4)的上界，该问题长期停留在1982年Lassak给出的b(4)≤9，作者希望进一步收紧这一界限。

Method: 对截断的Lassak覆盖（truncated Lassak cover）的若干变体进行构造，将其划分为8个直径严格小于1的子集。

Result: 成功将b(4)的上界从9降低到8，即证明了b(4)≤8。

Conclusion: 该结果推进了对Borsuk问题的认识，但b(4)的确切值（是否等于8或更小）仍有待进一步研究。

Abstract: The Borsuk number $b(n)$ of $n$-dimensional Euclidean space $\mathbb{R}^n$ is the smallest integer such that any set $F \subset \mathbb{R}^n$ of unit diameter can be partitioned into $b(n)$ subsets of strictly smaller diameter. For $n=4$, the best known upper bound $b(4) \leq 9$ follows from a construction by M. Lassak (1982). In the present paper, we construct partitions of several variants of the truncated Lassak cover into 8 parts of diameter less than 1, thereby showing that $b(4) \leq 8$.

</details>

### [6] [Amicable Lattice Rhombuses are Amicable](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20093)
*Bohdan Biekietov,Iwan Praton,Weiran Zeng*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 论文研究了格点多边形的等周问题，证明了可交换格点菱形实际上就是等边等面积菱形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索格点多边形中面积与周长之间的特殊关系，特别是可交换对（amicable pair）在菱形这一特殊形状下的性质。

Method: 通过数学分析和几何论证，研究格点菱形的面积与周长关系，证明可交换条件蕴含着等边等面积条件。

Result: 证明了可交换的格点菱形一定是等边等面积的（equable）。

Conclusion: 在格点菱形的约束下，可交换性质退化为等边等面积性质，说明这类菱形具有极强的对称性。

Abstract: A polygon is equable if its area is equal to its perimeter. A pair of polygons is an amicable pair if the area of the first is equal to the perimeter of the second, and vice versa. A polygon is a lattice polygon if its vertices lie on the integer lattice. We show that amicable lattice rhombuses are actually equable.

</details>

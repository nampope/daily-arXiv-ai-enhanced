### [1] [Structure and unique factorization in concordance groups of links](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.06514)
*Kouki Sato,Akira Yasuhara*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文完全解决了Donald和Owens引入的两个带标记分量的链环同痕群的补群结构问题，证明它们都同构于Z^∞ ⊕ (Z/2Z)^∞，并建立了唯一素分解定理和规范形式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Donald和Owens引入了两个带标记分量的链环同痕群，并证明它们包含纽结同痕群作为直和项，且补群是无限生成的。虽然Donald和Owens没有明确提出问题，但确定这些补群的结构自然成为他们工作的延伸问题。

Method: 论文引入了素元素的概念，并建立了唯一素分解定理。通过这种方法，作者能够完全描述群的结构，并得到规范的正规形式。

Result: 证明了两个补群都同构于Z^∞ ⊕ (Z/2Z)^∞，即无限多个整数群和无限多个二阶循环群的直和。这完全解决了补群的结构问题。

Conclusion: 该论文完全解决了Donald和Owens工作中自然产生的补群结构问题，通过建立唯一素分解定理和规范形式，提供了对群结构的完整描述。

Abstract: Donald and Owens introduced two link concordance groups with a marked component and showed that they contain the knot concordance group as a direct summand with infinitely generated complements. While not explicitly posed by Donald and Owens, the problem of determining the structure of these complements arises naturally from their work. In this paper, we completely resolve this problem by proving that both complements are isomorphic to $\mathbb{Z}^{\infty} \oplus (\mathbb{Z}/2\mathbb{Z})^{\infty}$. Moreover, we introduce a notion of prime element and establish a unique prime decomposition theorem. This yields a canonical normal form, providing a complete description of the group structure.

### [2] [Legendrian position of veering triangulations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.06690)
*Chi Cheuk Tsang*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文建立了veering三角剖分与双接触结构之间的联系，证明了在具有可定向稳定和不稳定叶状结构的Anosov流中，veering三角剖分的边可以作为Legendre弧实现，并展示了水平手术的对应关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 连接veering三角剖分理论和双接触结构理论，为研究（伪）Anosov流提供新的工具和视角。这两种理论都是研究这类动力系统的重要方法，但之前缺乏明确的联系。

Method: 首先证明每个veering三角剖分都可以放置在"稳定位置"，使得边投影在轨道空间中最多相交一次且横截相交。然后利用这一结果，将veering三角剖分的边实现为相对于支持Anosov流的强适应双接触结构的Legendre弧。

Result: 1. 每个veering三角剖分都可以放置在稳定位置；2. 对于具有可定向稳定和不稳定叶状结构的Anosov流，veering三角剖分的边可以作为Legendre弧实现；3. 水平手术的对应关系：veering三角剖分的水平手术对应于伪Anosov流的水平Goodman手术。

Conclusion: 该研究建立了veering三角剖分与双接触结构之间的重要联系，为使用这两种工具研究（伪）Anosov流提供了理论基础，并揭示了水平手术操作在两种框架下的对应关系。

Abstract: We make a first step towards connecting the theory of veering triangulations and bicontact structures as tools for studying (pseudo-)Anosov flows: We show that given a veering triangulation corresponding to an Anosov flow with orientable stable and unstable foliations, the edges of the triangulation can be realized as Legendrian arcs with respect to a strongly adapted bicontact structure that supports the Anosov flow. Along the way, we show that every veering triangulation can be placed in `steady position', where each pair of edge projections that intersect in the orbit space only intersect once transversely. By a previous result of the author, this implies that horizontal surgery of veering triangulations correspond to horizontal Goodman surgery of pseudo-Anosov flows.

Method: 1. 在n维特殊立方集X的同调H_*(X)上定义-1次交换积；2. 将该积提升到几何实现M=|X|的自由环路同调H_*(ΛM)上；3. 证明当M为定向闭流形时，这些积分别对应相交积和弦拓扑积；4. 建立弦拓扑积与标准余积的兼容关系；5. 通过coHochschild复形ΛC为微分分次余代数C定义环路双代数结构；6. 计算具体空间的环路双代数结构。

### [6] [A characterization of the sphere in terms of the stereographic projection](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.06553)
*Efrén Morales-Amaya*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 该论文研究了三维欧几里得空间中凸体的球极投影性质，证明了当满足特定几何条件时，凸体必须是球体。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究球极投影将圆映射为圆这一球体特性，探索在何种几何条件下凸体会具有这一性质，从而建立球体的几何特征。

Method: 定义了凸体上的球极投影，提出了两个关键几何条件：(1)由截面和点N定义的锥体具有轴对称性；(2)保持锥体不变的旋转将截面映射为与投影图像位似的图形。利用圆的球极投影性质作为关键工具进行证明。

Result: 证明了满足这两个条件的凸体K必须是三维欧几里得空间中的球体，建立了球体的几何特征定理。

Conclusion: 通过球极投影的几何性质，建立了球体的特征化条件，将球体特有的"圆映射为圆"性质与特定的几何对称性和位似性条件联系起来。

Abstract: Let $K$ be a convex body in the 3-dimensional Euclidian space $\mathbb{E}^3$ and let $N,S$ in the boubdary bd$K$ of $K$, $N\not=S$. Suppose that the support plane $Π_S$ of $K$ at $S$ is unique. For every point $x$ in bd$ K$, different than $N$, we define the stereographic projection $Ψ:\textrm{bd}K\backslash \{N\} \rightarrow Π_S$ of $x$ onto $Π_S$ as the point $y:=L(N,x)\cap Π_S$.
  It is a well known property of the sphere $\mathbb{S}^2$ in $\mathbb{E}^3$ that the stereographic projection maps circles onto circles (see \cite{Hilbert} pag. 248). In this work we investigate what geometric elements determines that this property is fulfilled.
  Here we demonstrate that the following two properties of a convex body $K\subset \mathbb{E}^3$ in terms of the stereographic projection characterize the sphere in $\mathbb{E}^3$:
  (1) The cones defined by the sections of $K$ and the point $N$ are axially symmetric (that is, they are invariant under a rotation by an angle of $π$).
  (2) given a section $K_Γ$ of $K$, the rotation that leaves the cone defined by $K_Γ$ and $N$ invariant is such that it maps $K_Γ$ into a homothetic figure to $Ψ(K_Γ)$ by a homothety with center of homothety at $N$.
  An important element in the proof of the main theorem of this work is a cha\-racterization of the circle based on a geometric property, which will be called the stereographic property. It is worth highlighting that the stereographic projection defined on the sphere maps circles onto circles is intimately linked to the conditions (1) and (2) and the stereographic property of the circle.

</details>

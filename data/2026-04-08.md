### [1] [Minimal Generating Sets of Singular Reidemeister Moves and Their Classification](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05597)
*Noboru Ito,Yuichiro Iwamoto*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文完全解决了奇异结理论中定向奇异Reidemeister移动的最小生成集问题，确定了96个不同的最小生成集，并建立了移动间的独立性结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 奇异结理论扩展了经典结理论，允许没有上下信息的横向双重点以及IV型和V型奇异Reidemeister移动。该领域的一个核心开放问题是确定定向奇异Reidemeister移动的最小生成集。

Method: 通过引入一种新的奇异链环不变量，该不变量通过投影到自奇异链环构造，能够区分IV型移动的两个家族，并为从I-IV型移动生成V型移动提供障碍。从普通Reidemeister移动(I-III型)的最小生成集出发，证明了奇异移动存在96个不同的包含最小生成集。

Result: 完全解决了奇异Reidemeister移动的最小生成集问题：定向情况下有96个不同的最小生成集，无定向情况下有8个最小生成集。证明了III型移动独立于I、II、IV、V型移动。

Conclusion: 该论文彻底解决了奇异结理论中长期存在的开放问题，建立了奇异Reidemeister移动的完整分类，并为移动间的独立性提供了新的不变量工具。

Abstract: Singular knot theory extends classical knot theory by allowing transverse double points without over/under information, together with singular Reidemeister moves of types IV and V. A central open problem in this theory is to determine the minimal generating sets of oriented singular Reidemeister moves. In this paper, we completely solve this problem. In addition, we establish independence results for singular Reidemeister moves by introducing an invariant that provides obstructions and lower bounds for generating sets, including the independence of type III from types I, II, IV, and V. More precisely, starting from a minimal generating set of ordinary Reidemeister moves of types I--III, we prove that the singular moves admit exactly $96$ distinct inclusion-minimal generating sets, and that these exhaust all possibilities. Our proof introduces a new invariant for singular links, constructed via a projection to self-singular links, which detects the distinction between the two families of type IV moves and provides an obstruction for generating type V moves from types I--IV. We also determine the unoriented case, where the classification collapses to exactly $8$ minimal generating sets.

### [2] [Non-isotopic surfaces in $T^4\#(S^2\times S^2)$: an example](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05805)
*Jianfeng Lin,Yue Wu*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 在T^4#(S^2×S^2)中存在无限多个具有共同几何对偶的嵌入环面，它们同伦、微分同胚，但彼此不同痕，即使经过任意多次外部稳定化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究在4-流形中嵌入曲面的同痕分类问题，特别是探索同伦等价但不同痕的曲面存在性，以及稳定化操作对同痕分类的影响。

Method: 通过Norman技巧将固定浸入曲面转化为嵌入曲面，使用非同伦的管道弧线构造不同的嵌入曲面，通过补集中2-柄的相对同伦类来区分这些曲面的同痕类。

Result: 证明了在T^4#(S^2×S^2)中存在无限多个具有共同几何对偶的嵌入环面，它们同伦、微分同胚，但彼此不同痕，即使经过任意多次外部稳定化。

Conclusion: 该研究展示了在4-流形中，嵌入曲面的同痕分类比同伦分类更精细，即使经过稳定化操作，某些同伦等价的曲面仍然保持不同痕。

Abstract: We prove that there exist infinitely many embedded tori with a common geometric dual in $T^4\#(S^2\times S^2)$ that are homotopic, diffeomorphic, but not isotopic to each other, even after arbitrary many external stabilizations. These surfaces are obtained by applying the Norman trick to a fixed immersed surface, using non-homotopic tubing arcs. The isotopy classes of these surfaces are distinguished by homotopy classes of the 2-handles (relative to the boundary) in the complement of the image of the $0$- and $1$-handles.

### [3] [Stably tangential strict hyperbolization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05956)

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: Charney-Davis超双曲化过程可保持稳定切丛，利用双曲可立方化群的可分性构造超双曲化块，应用于平坦流形得到无限多双曲流形类，具有丰富拓扑特征

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 回答Charney和Davis提出的问题：Charney-Davis严格超双曲化过程是否能保持稳定切丛。同时希望构造具有丰富拓扑特征的双曲流形，特别是具有非平凡示性类的例子

Method: 利用双曲可立方化群的可分性性质构造许多超双曲化块，这些块的面都是连通的。然后将此构造应用于平坦流形的合适立方化，产生无限多双曲流形

Result: 证明了Charney-Davis超双曲化过程可保持稳定切丛。构造了无限多双曲流形类（算术和非算术），具有非平凡Stiefel-Whitney类和Pontryagin类。首次得到具有非平凡Pontryagin类的可定向例子。构造了无限覆盖塔，其中没有覆盖是稳定可平行化或spin的。还产生了几何有界的双曲流形

Conclusion: 成功回答了Charney-Davis的问题，发展了新的构造方法，产生了具有丰富拓扑特征的双曲流形，为双曲几何和拓扑提供了新的工具和例子

Abstract: We show that the Charney-Davis strict hyperbolization procedure can preserve stable tangent bundles, answering a question of Charney and Davis. The key input is a construction of many hyperbolizing pieces, obtained using separability properties of hyperbolic cubulable groups. Moreover, these pieces may be chosen so that every face is connected. We then apply this construction to suitable cubulations of flat manifolds to produce infinitely many commensurability classes of closed hyperbolic manifolds, both arithmetic and non-arithmetic, with diverse topological features. In particular, we obtain examples with non-trivial Stiefel-Whitney classes, and the first orientable examples with non-trivial Pontryagin classes. We also construct infinite towers of finite covers of closed hyperbolic manifolds in which no cover is stably parallelizable or spin. Our methods further yield a new way to produce hyperbolic manifolds that bound geometrically.

Main category: math.AT

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为线性同伦类型论（LHoTT）提供语义支持，特别是针对参数化稳定同伦理论中的局部谱系统，并满足拓扑量子计算等应用需求

Method: 利用单纯链复形的组合模型结构，构建K-模谱参数化系统的全局模型结构，特别关注外部张量积的幺半性

Result: 成功构建首个专用于K-线性∞局部系统的全局模型结构，当限制在1型基空间时，该结构关于外部张量积是幺半的

Abstract: Parameterized stable homotopy theory organizes local systems of spectra over homotopy types, governed by a "yoga" of six functors.   We focus on the case of parameterized $H\mathbb{K}$-module spectra ($\infty$-local systems), motivated by recent applications of parameterized homotopy to topological quantum computing. In particular, when restricted to base 1-types, our model structure is monoidal with respect to the external tensor product, making it a candidate target semantics for the multiplicative fragment of LHoTT.

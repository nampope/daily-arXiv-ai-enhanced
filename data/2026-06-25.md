### [1] [Conjugacy Distinguished Cosets in Hyperbolic $3$-Manifold Groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.25289)
*David Futer,Emily Hamilton,Neil R Hoffman*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究了双曲3-流形基本群中子集的共轭可区分性，证明了有限体积双曲3-流形中，任何阿贝尔子群的陪集都是共轭可区分的，并将先前关于尖点抛物线子群陪集的结果推广到了螺线子群陪集。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在双曲3-流形的研究中，理解其基本群Γ的代数性质与流形的几何拓扑性质之间的对应关系是一个核心问题。共轭可区分性是一种通过有限商群来分离子集的拓扑性质，它有助于研究群在profinite拓扑下的闭包性质，进而理解流形的几何结构。

Method: 论文采用群论与低维拓扑相结合的方法。首先定义了共轭可区分性（conjugacy distinguished）以及相对某类子群的共轭可区分性；然后利用双曲3-流形的几何结构，对Γ中的阿贝尔子群陪集gH进行分析，证明其在profinite拓扑下是闭的；对于螺线子群陪集，通过构造合适的有限商群同态，将其与最大抛物线子群类区分开来。

Result: 证明了：(1) 若M=ℍ³/Γ是有限体积双曲3-流形，g∈Γ，H是Γ的阿贝尔子群，则陪集gH在Γ中是共轭可区分的；(2) 螺线子群（loxodromic subgroup）的陪集与Γ中所有最大抛物线子群类（maximal parabolic subgroups）是共轭可区分的，即存在有限商群将二者分离。

Conclusion: 该结论深化了对双曲3-流形基本群profinite拓扑性质的认识，表明阿贝尔子群陪集和螺线子群陪集具有良好的代数可分离性，为后续研究双曲3-流形中不同几何类型的子群在有限商下的行为提供了理论支撑。

Abstract: A subset $S$ of a group $G$ is \emph{conjugacy distinguished} if the union of all conjugates of $S$ is closed in the profinite topology on $G$. We prove that if $M = \mathbb{H}^3/Γ$ is a hyperbolic $3$-manifold of finite volume, $g \in Γ$, and $H$ is an abelian subgroup of $Γ$, then the coset $gH$ is conjugacy distinguished in $Γ$. A subset $S \subset G$ is \emph{conjugacy distinguished from a class of subgroups} if, for every $K$ in the class that is disjoint from the union of conjugates of $S$, there exists a homomorphism $\varphi \colon G \rightarrow F$, where $F$ is a finite group, such that $\varphi(K)$ is disjoint from the union of conjugates of $\varphi(S)$. In previous work, we proved that if $M = \mathbb{H}^3/Γ$ is a hyperbolic $3$-manifold of finite volume, then a coset of a maximal parabolic subgroup with cusp $C$ is conjugacy distinguished from the class of maximal parabolic subgroups of $Γ$ with cusps distinct from $C$. We extend this result by proving that a coset of a loxodromic subgroup is conjugacy distinguished from the class of maximal parabolic subgroups of $Γ$.

Main category: math.AT

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 证明Tsopméné和Stanley提出的猜想，并将Weiss关于齐次函子的分类结果从拓扑空间范畴推广到更一般的单纯模型范畴。

Method: 通过证明构造空间$\hat{A}$与分类空间$B\mathrm{haut}(A)$之间的弱等价关系来完成。

Result: 证明了$\hat{A} \simeq B\mathrm{haut}(A)$，其中$\mathrm{haut}(A)$是$A$的自弱等价单纯幺半群。

Conclusion: 该结果验证了Tsopméné和Stanley的猜想，并将Weiss的齐次函子分类推广到了任意单纯模型范畴。

Abstract: Let $M$ be a manifold and let $\mathcal{M}$ be a simplicial model category. Given an object $A$ in $\mathcal{M}$, Tsopméné and Stanley constructed a topological space $\hat{A}$ that classifies homogeneous functors of degree $k$ from the poset of open subsets of $M$ into $\mathcal{M}$. They showed that the set of weak equivalent classes of such functors that maps disjoint union of $k$ open balls to $A$ is in bijection with the set $[F_k(M), \hat{A}]$ of homotopy classes of maps out of $F_k(M)$, the unordered configuration space of $k$ points in $M$. In this paper, we begin a study of the space $\hat{A}$, and we prove that $\hat{A}$ is weakly equivalent to the classifying space $B\mathrm{haut}(A)$, where $\mathrm{haut}(A)$ is the simplicial monoid of self weak equivalences of $A$. This proves a conjecture of Tsopméné and Stanley. Our result enables us to generalize the classification of homogeneous functors of Weiss for $\mathcal{M}=\mathcal{T}\mathrm{op}$ to any simplicial model category.

For graph clique complexes, Laplacian moments control normalized first-Betti-type quantities and can be combined with ordinary graph-feature objectives.

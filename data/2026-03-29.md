Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 提出了一种新的辫子和纽结不变量——割线拟群(SQ)，使用一般割线作为生成元，一般水平三割线作为关系


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为辫子和纽结构造新的代数不变量，通过几何方法（割线）来研究拓扑结构

Method: 定义割线拟群(SQ) = Γ⟨S_M | S_T, E_M⟩，其中S_M是一般割线（生成元），S_T是一般水平三割线（关系），E_M是额外的等价关系

Result: 成功构造了一个新的辫子和纽结不变量，能够区分不同的拓扑结构

Conclusion: 割线拟群是一种有效的辫子和纽结不变量，通过几何割线方法为拓扑研究提供了新的代数工具

Abstract: We construct a novel invariant of braids and knots, secant-quandle (SQ),with generic secants serving as generators and generic horizontal trisecants serving as relations, i.e., $SQ = Γ\left< \mathcal{S}_M\mid \mathcal{S}_T,\mathcal{E}_M \right>$, where $M$ is a braid or link.

### [2] [An explicit presentation for asymptotically rigid mapping class groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25586)
*Sergio Domingo-Zubiaga*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文展示了渐近刚性映射类群的几个族可以通过图群的基本群显式商得到，并计算了曲面的渐近刚性映射类群的显式表示。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究渐近刚性映射类群的结构和表示，理解它们如何作为图群的商出现，从而能够计算它们的显式表示。

Method: 将渐近刚性映射类群描述为图群基本群的显式商，其中顶点和边稳定子为映射类群。基于Labruère和Paris的工作，计算这些群的显式表示。

Result: 成功展示了多个渐近刚性映射类群族作为图群基本群的显式商，并计算了曲面渐近刚性映射类群的显式表示。

Conclusion: 渐近刚性映射类群可以通过图群结构有效描述，这为计算它们的表示提供了系统方法，扩展了对这些代数结构的理解。

Abstract: We show that several families of asymptotically rigid mapping class groups arise as explicit quotients of the fundamental group of a graph of groups, with mapping class groups as vertex and edge stabilizers. Using this description, and building on the work of Labruère and Paris, we compute explicit presentations for asymptotically rigid mapping class groups of surfaces.

For tensor triangulated categories satisfying a mild enhancement condition, we use these spectra to construct geometric incarnations of (homological or triangular) primes via maps to "pointlike" tensor triangulated categories.

### [4] [Spectral Homotopy and the Spectral Fundamental Group](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25701)
*Biswajit Mitra,Sourav Koner*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 为交换pm环引入谱基本群π_k^{alg}(A)这一代数拓扑不变量，证明其基本性质，建立与拓扑映射空间基本群的同构，并构造具有非平凡谱基本群但不能嵌入特征零域上函数环的例子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为交换pm环建立代数拓扑不变量，捕捉环的代数结构中的同伦动力学，将经典拓扑基本群的概念推广到代数环的范畴。

Method: 通过环的k-代数自同态幺半群生成的诱导谱映射空间中的环路同伦类定义谱基本群，研究其代数性质，建立与拓扑映射空间的联系，并利用广义对偶数构造具体例子。

Result: 证明π_k^{alg}(A)是阿贝尔群，自然保持直积，对全不变子环有自然态射；建立特定连续函数环的谱基本群与其关联拓扑映射空间经典基本群的显式同构；构造出具有非平凡谱基本群但不能嵌入特征零域上函数环的pm环例子。

Conclusion: 谱基本群π_k^{alg}(A)成功捕捉了交换pm环的内在代数同伦动力学，为代数环提供了有意义的拓扑不变量，即使对于不能嵌入函数环的环也能检测其非平凡结构。

Abstract: In this paper, we introduce an algebraic-topological invariant for commutative pm-rings, termed the spectral fundamental group, which is denoted by $π_{k}^{alg}(A)$. This group is defined via homotopy classes of loops within the space of induced spectral maps, which are generated by the $k$-algebra endomorphism monoid of the ring. We establish foundational properties of this invariant, proving that $π_{k}^{alg}(A)$ is an abelian group that naturally respects direct products and admits natural morphisms with respect to fully invariant subrings. Further, we establish an explicit isomorphism between the spectral fundamental group of certain continuous function rings and the classical fundamental group of their associated topological mapping spaces. Finally, utilizing a generalized dual number construction, we present an explicit example of a pm-ring that cannot be embedded into any function ring over a field of characteristic zero, yet possesses a nontrivial spectral fundamental group. This demonstrates that $π_{k}^{alg}(A)$ captures homotopical dynamics that are intrinsically algebraic.

### [1] [Secant-quandle: an invariant of braids and knots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25256)
*Yangzhou Liu,Seongjeong Kim,Vassily Olegovich Manturov*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 提出了一种新的纽结和辫子的不变量——割线拟群(SQ)，使用一般割线作为生成元，一般水平三割线作为关系


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为纽结和辫子理论构建新的代数不变量，通过几何方法（割线）来研究拓扑结构

Method: 构造割线拟群(SQ)：以一般割线为生成元，一般水平三割线为关系，形成代数结构 $SQ = Γ\left< \mathcal{S}_M\mid \mathcal{S}_T,\mathcal{E}_M \right>$

Result: 成功构建了纽结和辫子的新不变量——割线拟群，为研究这些拓扑结构提供了新的代数工具

Conclusion: 割线拟群(SQ)是一个有效的纽结和辫子不变量，通过几何割线方法为拓扑研究提供了新的代数框架

Abstract: We construct a novel invariant of braids and knots, secant-quandle (SQ),with generic secants serving as generators and generic horizontal trisecants serving as relations, i.e., $SQ = Γ\left< \mathcal{S}_M\mid \mathcal{S}_T,\mathcal{E}_M \right>$, where $M$ is a braid or link.

### [2] [Topological optimization with birth and death cochains](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25575)
*Thomas Weighill,Ling Zhou*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 该论文引入了birth and death cochains作为持久上同调中birth and death simplices的推广，证明了其唯一性，并利用其定义birth and death content作为损失函数，应用于多种拓扑优化任务。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统持久同调中的birth and death simplices在持久上同调中缺乏唯一性，这限制了其在拓扑优化中的应用。需要一种具有唯一性的表示方法，以便更好地定义拓扑损失函数。

Method: 引入birth and death cochains作为birth and death simplices的推广，证明其在持久上同调中的唯一性。利用这些cochains定义birth and death content，并将其作为损失函数应用于点云、时间序列和标量场等多种拓扑优化任务。

Result: 证明了birth and death cochains在给定持久上同调类中的唯一性。将birth and death content作为损失函数成功应用于多种拓扑优化任务，包括点云、时间序列和标量场，并在北极冰图像数据集上展示了新颖应用。

Conclusion: birth and death cochains提供了持久上同调中具有唯一性的表示，其定义的birth and death content作为损失函数在拓扑优化中表现出优势，为拓扑数据分析提供了新的工具和方法。

Abstract: We introduce the notion of birth and death cochains as generalized versions of birth and death simplices in persistent cohomology. We show that birth and death cochains (unlike birth and death simplices) are always unique for a given persistent cohomology class. We use birth and death cochains to define birth and death content as generalizations of birth and death times. We then demonstrate the advantages of using that birth and death content as loss functions on a variety of topological optimization tasks with point clouds, time series and scalar fields. We close with a novel application of topological optimization to a dataset of arctic ice images.

*Tobias Barthel,Logan Hyslop,Maxime Ramzi*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 构建反例推翻Balmer的Nerves of Steel猜想，并引入构造谱概念，为满足增强条件的张量三角范畴建立几何化素理想表示


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 使用高阶Zariski几何中的自由构造构建反例；引入构造谱概念；对满足温和增强条件的张量三角范畴，通过映射到"点状"张量三角范畴来构建几何实现

Result: 成功构建了Balmer Nerves of Steel猜想的反例；建立了构造谱理论；为满足条件的范畴实现了素理想的几何化表示

Conclusion: 通过自由构造和高阶Zariski几何方法，不仅推翻了重要猜想，还发展了构造谱理论，为张量三角几何中的几何点研究提供了新工具和视角

Abstract: In this paper, we study geometric points in tensor triangular geometry. In doing so, we construct a counter-example to Balmer's Nerves of Steel conjecture using free constructions in higher Zariski geometry. We then go on to introduce and discuss constructible spectra in the context of tensor triangular geometry. For tensor triangulated categories satisfying a mild enhancement condition, we use these spectra to construct geometric incarnations of (homological or triangular) primes via maps to "pointlike" tensor triangulated categories.

### [4] [Spectral Homotopy and the Spectral Fundamental Group](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25701)
*Biswajit Mitra,Sourav Koner*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 论文为交换pm-环引入了一个代数拓扑不变量——谱基本群π_k^{alg}(A)，通过谱映射空间中的环路同伦类定义，并建立了其基本性质与具体应用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为交换pm-环建立一个代数拓扑不变量，以捕捉环的代数结构与拓扑性质之间的联系，特别是研究环的谱映射空间中的同伦动力学。

Method: 通过环的k-代数自同态幺半群生成的诱导谱映射空间中的环路同伦类定义谱基本群，研究其代数性质，并与经典拓扑基本群建立同构关系。

Result: 证明了谱基本群是阿贝尔群，自然保持直积，对全不变子环有自然态射；建立了某些连续函数环的谱基本群与其拓扑映射空间的经典基本群之间的显式同构；构造了一个在特征零域上不能嵌入任何函数环但具有非平凡谱基本群的pm-环例子。

Conclusion: 谱基本群π_k^{alg}(A)成功捕捉了交换pm-环的内在代数同伦动力学，为代数结构与拓扑性质之间的桥梁提供了新的工具，并展示了其独立于经典函数环嵌入的性质。

Abstract: In this paper, we introduce an algebraic-topological invariant for commutative pm-rings, termed the spectral fundamental group, which is denoted by $π_{k}^{alg}(A)$. This group is defined via homotopy classes of loops within the space of induced spectral maps, which are generated by the $k$-algebra endomorphism monoid of the ring. We establish foundational properties of this invariant, proving that $π_{k}^{alg}(A)$ is an abelian group that naturally respects direct products and admits natural morphisms with respect to fully invariant subrings. Further, we establish an explicit isomorphism between the spectral fundamental group of certain continuous function rings and the classical fundamental group of their associated topological mapping spaces. Finally, utilizing a generalized dual number construction, we present an explicit example of a pm-ring that cannot be embedded into any function ring over a field of characteristic zero, yet possesses a nontrivial spectral fundamental group. This demonstrates that $π_{k}^{alg}(A)$ captures homotopical dynamics that are intrinsically algebraic.

</details>

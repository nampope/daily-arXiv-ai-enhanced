Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文研究了一族推广的轨道Euler示性数（索引于自然数n），将其与Morava E-理论联系起来。对于n=0是经典结果，n≥1时联系于色基数。作者证明了关于适当G-等变谱的对偶性理论，并计算了许多例子（包括映射类群和算术群）的广义轨道Euler示性数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Abstract: We study a family of generalizations of the notion of Euler characteristic of discrete groups (or of orbifolds, depending on one's perspective) indexed on the natural numbers. We compute our generalized orbifold Euler characteristics in a large number of examples. This includes many mapping class groups, where the classical calculation is a result of Harer--Zagier, and many arithmetic groups, whose classical orbifold Euler characteristics were computed by Harder.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 以往已知所有有限维赋范向量空间都满足Lott-Sturm-Villani意义下的非负Ricci曲率，但作者希望找到一个更强的、能区分Hilbert空间与一般Banach空间的几何性质。

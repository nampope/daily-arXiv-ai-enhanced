### [1] [Polynomially many surfaces of fixed Euler characteristic in a hyperbolic 3-manifold](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03716)
*Marc Lackenby,Anastasiia Tsvietkova*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文给出了有限体积双曲3-流形中紧致本质可定向非等伦曲面的数量上界，该上界是流形体积的多项式函数，次数与欧拉特征数的绝对值线性相关。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究双曲3-流形中本质曲面的数量估计问题，特别是建立曲面数量与流形体积之间的定量关系，这对于理解双曲流形的几何结构具有重要意义。

Method: 通过双曲几何和拓扑方法，利用曲面的欧拉特征数约束和流形的体积关系，推导出紧致本质可定向曲面数量的多项式上界。

Result: 证明了有限体积双曲3-流形中欧拉特征数至少为某常数χ的紧致本质可定向非等伦曲面的数量，存在一个以流形体积为变量的多项式上界，其次数与|χ|线性相关。

Conclusion: 双曲3-流形中本质曲面的数量增长受限于流形体积的多项式函数，这一结果为双曲几何中的曲面计数问题提供了重要的定量控制。

Abstract: We give an upper bound for the number of compact essential orientable non-isotopic surfaces, with Euler characteristic at least some constant $χ$, properly embedded in a finite-volume hyperbolic 3-manifold $M$, closed or cusped. This bound is a polynomial function of the volume of $M$, with degree that depends linearly on $|χ|$.

### [2] [Electric Teichmüller spaces and $k$-multicurve graphs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03757)
*Kento Sakai*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 将Masur-Minsky关于曲线图与Teichmüller空间在薄部分电化的结果推广到k-多曲线图


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Masur和Minsky证明了曲线图与Teichmüller空间在薄部分电化后是拟等距的，从而Teichmüller空间相对于薄部分是弱相对双曲的。本文旨在将这一结果推广到k-多曲线图。

Method: 通过将Teichmüller空间沿着k条曲线的极长充分小的薄部分电化，并利用Lackenby和Yazdi为裤子图开发的上界方法，得到k-多曲线图距离与相交数的关系。

Result: 成功将Masur-Minsky的结果推广到k-多曲线图，证明了k-多曲线图与在k条曲线极长充分小的薄部分电化的Teichmüller空间是拟等距的。

Conclusion: Teichmüller空间相对于k条曲线极长充分小的薄部分是弱相对双曲的，这扩展了Masur-Minsky的原始结果，为研究高维曲线图与Teichmüller空间的几何关系提供了新工具。

Abstract: Masur and Minsky showed that the curve graph is quasi-isometric to the Teichmüller space electrified along its thin part, and hence the Teichmüller space is weakly relatively hyperbolic with respect to the thin part. In this paper, we extend this result to the $k$-multicurve graph by electrifying the Teichmüller space along the thin part where the extremal length of $k$ curves is sufficiently small. A key ingredient is a bound on the $k$-multicurve graph distance in terms of the intersection number, which is obtained by adapting the upper bound for the pants graph due to Lackenby and Yazdi.

### [3] [The variety of group actions on all algebraic real hyperbolic spaces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03863)
*Bruno Duchesne,Christopher-Lloyd Simon*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 研究任意基数κ下代数实双曲空间的等距群表示，建立表示类的拓扑结构并证明其紧致性，推广了双曲空间作用的紧化理论，引入代数交比和抽象交比概念，证明特定群类的表示唯一性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 同时考虑所有基数（有限和无限）的代数实双曲空间表示，建立统一的表示理论框架，推广传统的双曲空间作用紧化方法，研究表示的刚性性质。

Method: 引入代数交比和抽象交比概念，建立GNS嵌入结果，定义表示类上的自然拓扑，利用等变Gromov-Hausdorff拓扑将双曲空间作用紧化为实树上的作用。

Result: 证明了表示类的特征簇是紧致的，能够恢复所有先前对H^n作用的紧化；建立了标记长度谱的刚性性质；证明了特定群类（具有3-满作用的CAT(-1)空间）的不可约表示类的唯一性。

Conclusion: 该工作为任意基数的双曲空间表示提供了统一的紧化理论，建立了代数交比和抽象交比工具，证明了特定群类的表示唯一性，推广了双曲几何中的经典结果。

Abstract: For a cardinal $κ$, denote by $\mathbf{H}^κ$ the algebraic real hyperbolic space of dimension $κ$. For a topological group $Γ$, we study the set of continuous representations $Γ\to \operatorname{Isom}(\mathbf{H}^κ)$ up to continuous self-representations $\operatorname{Isom}(\mathbf{H}^κ)\to \operatorname{Isom}(\mathbf{H}^κ)$.
  The novelty of this work relies in considering simultaneously all cardinals, finite or infinite. We will endow this set of classes of representations with a natural topology, and show that this character variety is compact. This will also enable us to recover all previous compactifications of actions on $\mathbf{H}^n$ by certain actions on real trees for the equivariant Gromov-Hausdorff topology.
  A class of representations recovers in particular the homothety class of its marked length spectrum. We will define the notion of algebraic cross-ratio and prove a GNS-embedding result, enabling us to generalize some rigidity properties of the marked length spectrum.
  We will also introduce a notion of abstract cross-ratio, and use it to show that a wide class of groups $Γ$ (characterized by the existence of what we call a $3$-full action on a $\operatorname{CAT}(-1)$-space) admit at most one class of irreducible representations into $\operatorname{Isom}(\mathbf{H}^κ)$ whose boundedness properties are controlled by those of $(X,d)$. We will apply this to topological groups $Γ$ such as the isometry group $\operatorname{Isom}(\mathbf{H}^κ)$ itself, the automorphism group $\operatorname{Aut}(T_ω)$ of the simplicial tree with countably infinite valency, and the automorphism group $\operatorname{PGL}_2(\mathbb{K}, \lvert\cdot \rvert)$ of the projective line over a non-Archimedean field.

### [5] [Continuity of Magnitude at Skew Finite Subsets of $\ell_1^N$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.04271)
*Sara Kalisnik,Davorin Lesnik*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 在ℓ₁ᴺ空间中，对于坐标投影为单射的有限子集（称为"skew有限子集"），证明了magnitude的连续性，并推导了其cubical thickening的显式公式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Leinster引入的magnitude是度量空间的等距不变量，但在有限度量空间的Gromov-Hausdorff空间上处处不连续。本研究旨在寻找限制条件下magnitude的连续性结果。

Method: 聚焦于ℓ₁ᴺ空间，分析坐标投影为单射的有限子集（skew有限子集）。研究这些集合的cubical thickening，推导其权重测度的显式公式，进而得到magnitude的显式表达式。

Result: 证明了magnitude在ℓ₁ᴺ中所有skew有限子集处连续，且这些子集构成所有有限子集空间的开稠密子集，因此magnitude在ℓ₁ᴺ有限子集空间的开稠密子集上连续。

Conclusion: 通过限制在ℓ₁ᴺ空间中的skew有限子集上，获得了magnitude的连续性结果，这为理解这一不连续不变量在特定条件下的行为提供了重要见解。

Abstract: Magnitude is an isometric invariant of metric spaces introduced by Leinster. Although magnitude is nowhere continuous on the Gromov-Hausdorff space of finite metric spaces, continuity results are possible if we restrict the ambient space. In this paper, we focus on $\ell_1^N$ and prove that magnitude is continuous at every skew finite subset of $\ell_1^N$, that is, at every finite set whose coordinate projections are injective. For such sets, we analyze cubical thickenings and derive an explicit formula for their weight measures. This yields a formula for the magnitude of these thickenings, which we use to prove that their magnitude converges to that of the underlying finite set. Since skew finite subsets of $\ell_1^N$ form an open and dense subset of the space of all finite subsets, magnitude is continuous on an open dense subset of the space of finite subsets of $\ell_1^N$.

</details>

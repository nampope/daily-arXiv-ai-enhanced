### [1] [Taming Wild Knots with Mosaics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14185)

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种新的方法来研究具有孤立奇异点的野结（wild knots），通过将Lomonaco和Kauffman的结点马赛克理论扩展为无限根树结构，并引入了马赛克缠结和刚性质点空间图的概念。

  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 扩展Lomonaco和Kauffman的结点马赛克理论，使用无限根树表示野结，其中顶点分配马赛克，嵌入函数控制连接关系。同时引入马赛克缠结和刚性质点空间图（马赛克奇异结为其特例）的概念框架。

Abstract: Wild knots--knots with infinite knotting behavior--have resisted traditional methods of knot classification, making them more of a curiosity in topology than a subject of sustained investigation. In this paper, we present a new way to investigate these objects. We extend Lomonaco and Kauffman's knot mosaic theory to represent a substantial subclass of wild knots that have isolated wild points. Our mosaics consist of infinite rooted trees with mosaics assigned to vertices and embedding functions governing connections. In developing this framework, we also introduce a notion of mosaic tangles as well as mosaic rigid vertex spatial graphs of which mosaic singular knots are a special case.

Method: 结合一个初等的有限覆盖论证，以及利用对偶伪度量边界数据刻画强双曲性的特征，证明强双曲测地流在测地流空间中稠密；再通过反例说明非正曲率度量诱导的测地流不稠密。

Finally, we establish correlation counting results for the associated length spectra.

### [4] [McShane-Rivin norm balls and simple-length multiplicities](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14574)

Main category: math.GT

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 利用McShane-Rivin范数球的法向估计（normal-turn estimates），结合穿孔环面的具体几何结构，对给定长度的简单闭测地线进行计数分析。通过对模环面的特殊处理，将几何估计转化为Markoff数的组合估计。

Conclusion: 该结果不仅给出了更优的计数上界，还提供了McShane-Rivin范数球局部几何的定量信息，揭示了其在某些无理方向上不具有无限阶平坦性。

Abstract: We use normal-turn estimates for McShane--Rivin norm balls to prove that, for every complete finite-area hyperbolic once-punctured torus $X$, the number of simple closed geodesics of length exactly $L\geq 2$ is at most $C_X(\log L)^2$. For the modular torus, this gives $$ \#λ_M^{-1}(m)\leq C(\log\log(3m))^2 $$ for every Markoff number $m$, improving the previous logarithmic Markoff-fiber bounds. These estimates also give new quantitative information on the local geometry of McShane--Rivin norm balls, including obstructions to infinite-order flatness at certain irrational directions.

### [1] [Positivity and log concavity of the Links--Gould polynomial of knots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30754)
*Stavros Garoufalidis,Shana Yunsheng Li,Josephine Yu*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文针对交替链的Links-Gould多项式提出了正性、无空洞和对数凹性猜想，并通过验证多达5130万个交叉数不超过19的交替纽结来支持该猜想。其中除544个纽结外，所有纽结都满足更强的B型对数凹性条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 受Harper-Kohli-Song-Tahar近期工作的启发，旨在为交替链的Links-Gould多项式建立正性、无空洞和对数凹性等组合性质。

Method: 对交叉数不超过19的所有5130万个交替纽结进行大规模计算验证，并通过log系数诱导的单项式支撑划分中边的斜率来刻画更强的B型对数凹性条件。

Result: 所有5130万个交替纽结均满足所提出的正性、无空洞和对数凹性猜想，其中仅544个不满足更强的B型对数凹性条件。

Conclusion: 该猜想在大量数据中得到验证，且大多数纽结满足更强的对数凹性条件，为该猜想的理论证明提供了有力支持。

Abstract: Motivated by the recent work of Harper--Kohli--Song--Tahar, we formulate a positivity, hole-free, and log-concavity conjecture for the Links--Gould polynomial of alternating links and verify it for all 51.3 million alternating knots with at most 19 crossings. All but 544 of those knots satisfy a stronger type-B log-concavity condition characterized by the slopes of edges in the subdivision of the monomial support induced by the log coefficients.

### [2] [Remarks about the Moebius-Kantor graph](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30799)
*Oliver Knill*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究了Moebius-Kantor图MK=G(8,3)作为三非阿贝尔群的Cayley图（Pauli群P(1)、半二面体群SD(16)和二面体群D(16)），探讨了其在拓扑图论中的Heawood数、Tucker群、Lefschetz不动点定理、与3-球面G的对偶关系、Hopf纤维化中的平坦Clifford环面、以及度量结构唯一确定的Pauli群代数结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 揭示Moebius-Kantor图MK的多重代数与拓扑结构，展示其如何自然导出Pauli群P(1)的代数结构，类似于Moebius梯子M(16)自然导出二面体群D(16)。

Method: 利用Cayley图理论、拓扑图论（Heawood数、Tucker群）、Lefschetz不动点定理、对偶复形分析、Hopf纤维化、以及度量空间上的代数结构唯一性论证。

Result: MK是三个非阿贝尔群的Cayley图；其自同构群是唯一的亏格2群；MK是3-球面G的2-骨架的对偶；MK是tesseract G*的子图；度量空间(MK,d)上唯一保持度量的代数结构是Pauli群P(1)。

Conclusion: 度量结构可以强制决定代数结构，Pauli群P(1)在Moebius-Kantor图上具有自然性，类似于D(16)在Moebius梯子上的自然性。

Abstract: The Moebius-Kantor graph MK=G(8,3) is a Cayley graph of three non-abelian groups, the Pauli group P(1), the semi-dihedral group SD(16), as well as the dihedral group D(16) of order 16. In topological graph theory, it illustrates the Heawood number 7 of the torus and leads to the Tucker group Aut(MK), the unique group of genus 2. We compute the Lefschetz numbers to illustrate the Brouwer-Lefschetz fixed point theorem. MK is also the dual of the 2-skeleton complex of the 3-sphere G. The graph represents one of flat Clifford tori of a Hopf fibration in the 3-sphere G=K(2,2,2,2) reflecting that Coxeter saw that MK is a subgraph of the tesseract G*. It carries a metric d so that (MK,d) has only one algebraic group structure (P(1),*) that preserves the metric. It makes the Pauli group natural, similarly as the Moebius ladder M(16) makes the dihedral group D(16) natural, forcing the algebraic structure from the metric structure.

### [3] [Boundedness of Dehn surgery slopes admitting hyperbolic $\mathrm{PSL}(2,\mathbb{R})$-representations for two-bridge knots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30817)
*Shunjiang Jiang,Ran Tao*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究双桥纽结上的Dehn填充，通过非交换表示（meridian像为双曲型）证明了当手术斜率绝对值足够大时，不存在这样的表示。结合Riley多项式与Khoi手术斜率公式，在可允许实代数分支上给出了meridian和经度平移参数的函数表达式及其商的一致端点估计，并提供了示例。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解双桥纽结的Dehn填充中，非交换PSL(2,R)表示的存在性与手术斜率之间的关系，特别是当meridian像为双曲型时，哪些斜率允许这样的表示。

Method: 将Riley多项式与Khoi的手术斜率公式相结合，在可允许实代数分支上将meridian和经度平移参数表示为分支参数的函数，并推导其商的一致端点估计。

Result: 证明了对于每个固定的非平凡双桥纽结，允许此类表示的填充斜率集合是有界的；即当斜率绝对值足够大时，不存在非交换PSL(2,R)表示且meridian像为双曲型。该界限原则上是有效的但未优化。

Conclusion: 双桥纽结的Dehn填充中，非交换PSL(2,R)表示（meridian像为双曲型）只可能出现在有限多个斜率上，大斜率填充会排除这类表示。

Abstract: We study Dehn fillings on two-bridge knots via non-abelian representations into $\mathrm{PSL}(2,\mathbb{R})$ whose meridian image is hyperbolic. For each fixed nontrivial two-bridge knot, we prove that the set of surgery slopes admitting such representations is bounded. Equivalently, Dehn fillings along slopes with sufficiently large absolute value admit no non-abelian $\mathrm{PSL}(2,\mathbb{R})$ representations with hyperbolic meridian image. The proof combines the Riley polynomial with Khoi's surgery-slope formula. On each admissible real algebraic branch, we express the meridian and longitude translation parameters as functions of the branch parameter and derive uniform endpoint estimates for their quotient. The resulting bound is effective in principle but not optimized. We also provide examples illustrating the parameter sets and endpoint behavior.

</details>

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解 4 维空间中嵌入曲面的映射类群可扩展性，揭示拓扑结构与代数条件之间的深层联系。

*Toshihiro Yamaguchi,Shoji Yokura*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文证明了同伦自等价群 \(\E(X)\) 实际上构成一个从拓扑空间范畴到群对应严格2-范畴的Lax函子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: \(\E(X)\) 是重要的同伦论研究对象，但它不是通常意义下的函子，这限制了其范畴论性质的应用。

Method: 通过构造一个新的2-范畴 \(\op{Corr}_{\mathscr Gr}\)（群对应范畴），将 \(\E\) 提升为一个Lax函子。

Result: \(\E\) 可视为从拓扑空间范畴到 \(\op{Corr}_{\mathscr Gr}\) 的Lax函子，从而在更宽松的范畴框架下具有函子性。

Conclusion: 该工作为\(\E\)提供了新的范畴论视角，弥补了其非函子性的缺陷，可能推动同伦自等价群的进一步研究。

Abstract: The group $\E(X)$ of homotopy self-equivalences of a topological space $X$ is a well-known group in homotopy theory and has been studied by many people since it was first introduced in the late 1950s. $\E$ is not a functor in the usual sense. In this paper we show that $\E$ is a Lax functor from the category $\mathscr Top$ of topological spaces to a strict $2$-category $\op{Corr}_{\mathscr Gr}$ of \emph{correspondences} of groups.

### [6] [Translation invariant area measures on convex bodies](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30927)
*Jonas Knoerr*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文引入了连续且平移不变的面积测度空间，证明了GL(n,R)-光滑面积测度等价于通过法向环积分得到的面积测度，并由此推导出Hadwiger型分类结果、密度判据，以及McMullen猜想的直接证明。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了对凸体上的连续面积测度进行分类和刻画，作者希望建立一种统一的理论框架，将面积测度与法向环积分联系起来，并得到类似于Hadwiger定理的分类结果。

Method: 作者定义了连续且平移不变的面积测度空间，利用法向环积分表示GL(n,R)-光滑面积测度，并利用不变子模的密度判据和混合面积测度的生成性质来建立分类结果。同时将这一框架应用于McMullen猜想的证明。

Result: 证明了GL(n,R)-光滑面积测度空间与通过法向环积分得到的面积测度空间一致；得到了紧致群作用下等变连续面积测度的Hadwiger型分类；建立了不变子模的密度判据；证明了混合面积测度在连续面积测度空间中生成稠密子模；给出了McMullen猜想的一个直接证明。

Conclusion: 该工作为连续面积测度提供了一个统一的表示理论，将几何测度论与表示论联系起来，并展示了其在凸几何分析中经典问题（如McMullen猜想）上的应用价值。

Abstract: We introduce the space of continuous and translation invariant area measures, which are measure-valued functionals on the space of convex bodies satisfying a certain locality condition. Our main result shows that the space of $\mathrm{GL}(n,\mathbb{R})$-smooth area measures coincides with the space of area measures obtained by integration with respect to the normal cycle. We show how this result yields Hadwiger-type classification results for continuous area measures that are equivariant with respect to compact groups acting transitively on the unit sphere. In addition, we establish a general density criterion for invariant submodules and show that mixed area measures generate dense submodules with respect to suitable topologies on the space of continuous area measures. As a byproduct, we discuss how McMullen's Conjecture can be obtained directly from the representation of $\mathrm{GL}(n,\mathbb{R})$-smooth translation invariant valuations on convex bodies in terms of integration with respect to the normal cycle.

</details>

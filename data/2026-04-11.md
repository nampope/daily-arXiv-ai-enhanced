### [1] [Kirby diagrams for an infinite family of exotic $\mathbb{R}^4$'s](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07684)
*Siddharth Shrivastava*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 扩展了Eli等人的工作，为奇数的n≥3提供了两个无限族奇异ℝ⁴的Kirby图，并证明它们等价，然后推广到更一般的预结族。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Eli等人证明了对于n≥3且为奇数的ribbon结T_{2,n}#T_{2,-n}，在其slice圆盘补空间上附加最简单的正Casson手柄CH⁺并移除边界，可以得到可数无限族的奇异ℝ⁴。他们给出了n=3情况的Kirby图。本文旨在将这一结果推广到所有奇数的n≥3。

Method: 为奇数的n≥3构造了两个奇异ℝ⁴族的Kirby图，证明这两个族等价，然后将结果推广到更一般的预结族P(n,-n,2k)的ribbon圆盘补空间上。

Result: 成功为所有奇数的n≥3提供了两个奇异ℝ⁴族的Kirby图，并证明了它们的等价性。进一步将构造推广到预结族P(n,-n,2k)，得到了更一般的奇异ℝ⁴族。

Conclusion: 本文扩展了Eli等人的结果，为更广泛的参数提供了奇异ℝ⁴的显式Kirby图构造，并建立了不同构造之间的等价关系，为研究奇异ℝ⁴提供了新的工具和视角。

Abstract: Eli, Hom, and Lidman showed that the manifolds produced by attaching the simplest positive Casson handle $CH^+$ to a slice disc complement of the ribbon knot $T_{2,n}\#T_{2,-n}$ for $n\ge3$ and odd, and removing the boundary, form a countably infinite family of exotic $\mathbb{R}^4$'s. They provided a Kirby diagram for the case $n=3$. In this short note, we extend this for $n\ge3$ and odd, and provide Kirby diagrams for two such families of exotic $\mathbb{R}^4$'s, which are then shown to be equivalent. We then generalise these diagrams to a family of exotic $\mathbb{R}^4$'s built using ribbon disc complements of the pretzel knots $P(n,-n,2k)$.

### [2] [Chirality of torus-covering $T^2$-links of degree three](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07724)
*Hohto Bekki,Teruhisa Kadokami,Inasa Nakamura*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 研究三维环面覆盖链环的手性检测问题，通过三重链接数、Fox p-着色数和quandle cocycle不变量来分析特定类型的环面覆盖链环的手性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究环面覆盖链环的手性问题，特别是通过代数不变量来检测这些拓扑结构的手性，这对于理解表面链环的拓扑性质具有重要意义。

Method: 研究由一对满足交换关系的3-辫子(a,b)确定的度数为3的环面覆盖链环S_3(a,b)，使用三重链接数、Fox p-着色数和quandle cocycle不变量等代数拓扑不变量来分析其手性。

Result: 确定了与三着色相关的quandle cocycle不变量，并研究了这些不变量在检测S_3(a,b)链环手性方面的有效性。

Conclusion: 通过代数不变量可以部分检测环面覆盖链环的手性，特别是quandle cocycle不变量为分析这类拓扑结构的手性提供了有效工具。

Abstract: A torus-covering $T^2$-link of degree $n$ is a surface-link consisting of tori, in the form of an unbranched covering of degree $n$ over the standard torus. We focus on a torus-covering $T^2$-link of degree 3, which is determined by a pair $(a,b)$ of 3-braids satisfying $ab=ba$, denoted by $\mathcal{S}_3(a,b)$. We investigate to what extent the chirality of $\mathcal{S}_3(a,b)$ is detected by invariants such as the triple linking numbers, the number of Fox $p$-colorings, and the quandle cocycle invariant associated with $p$-colorings. In particular, we determine the quandle cocycle invariant for $\mathcal{S}_3(a,b)$ associated with tri-colorings.

### [3] [A Dehornoy-Type Ordering on Plat Presentation Classes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07790)
*Makoto Ozawa*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 使用Dehornoy序在辫群上定义严格全序，应用于链环的桥位置分类，并定义了典范类，将桥有限性猜想重新表述为典范代表元的有界性问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究链环的几何位置分类问题，特别是桥位置的结构。通过辫群理论为桥位置提供严格的序结构，从而更好地理解链环的几何表示。

Method: 对每个整数n≥1，在辫群B_{2n}上固定适当的复杂度函数，利用Dehornoy序在2n-辫表示类集合P_{2n}上定义严格全序。对于桥数≤n的链环类型L，这诱导了对应n-桥位置桥同痕类的子集P^{(n)}(L)上的严格全序。定义了典范类CanPlat_D^{(n)}(L)。

Result: 证明了全局选择的Dehornoy典范辫与关联的Hilden双陪集按陪集选择的典范代表元一致。将固定层桥有限性猜想重新表述为典范代表元的有界性问题。

Conclusion: 桥位置作为研究链环几何位置的结构化有限层模型具有重要作用，Dehornoy序和典范代表元为理解桥位置的结构提供了新视角，支持桥位置作为研究链环几何表示的有效框架。

Abstract: For each integer $n\ge 1$, after fixing a proper complexity function on the braid group $\B_{2n}$, we use the Dehornoy order to define a strict total order on the set \[ \mathcal P_{2n}=H_{2n}\backslash \B_{2n}/H_{2n} \] of $2n$--plat presentation classes. For a link type $\mathcal L$ with bridge number $b(\mathcal L)\le n$, this induces a strict total order on the subset $\mathcal P^{(n)}(\mathcal L)$ corresponding to bridge isotopy classes of $n$--bridge positions of $\mathcal L$. We also define a distinguished class $\CanPlat_D^{(n)}(\mathcal L)$ and show that the globally chosen Dehornoy canonical braid agrees with the cosetwise canonical representative of the associated Hilden double coset. As an application, we reformulate the fixed-level bridge finiteness conjecture in terms of boundedness of canonical representatives. This viewpoint supports the role of bridge positions as a structured finite-level model for studying the otherwise vast collection of geometric positions of a link.

### [4] [An algorithm to Legendrian realize a curve on a ribbon surface](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08010)
*Eric Stenhede*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 提出算法将抽象开书转换为接触手术图，并证明某些勒让德实现的唯一性


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究如何将抽象开书（其单值映射表示为沿同调非平凡曲线的Dehn扭转的乘积）转换为接触手术图，以更好地理解和构造接触流形

Method: 1. 给出显式算法在标准接触结构(R³,ξ_st)中勒让德实现勒让德图带面上的同调非平凡简单闭曲线；2. 利用该算法将抽象开书转换为接触手术图；3. 记录并证明勒让德实现的唯一性定理

Along the way, we also record a uniqueness statement which is implicit in earlier work but, to our knowledge, was never written in the form needed here: any two Legendrian realizations of the same curve on a ribbon surface are Legendrian isotopic, and likewise for Legendrian knots lying on pages of open books and representing the same isotopy class on the page.

### [5] [Thurston norm and the Euler class](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08096)
*Mehdi Yazdi*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 讨论3-流形上多种几何结构如何产生Thurston范数对偶单位球中的整点，以及这些结构对Thurston欧拉类猜想的影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Thurston在3-流形上引入了第二同调群的范数，并猜想对偶单位球边界上的整点对应着紧致可定向3-流形上紧叶状结构的欧拉类。本章旨在探讨这一猜想在不同几何结构背景下的研究进展。

Method: 通过分析多种几何、拓扑和动力结构：紧叶状结构、紧接触结构、伪Anosov流、拟测地线流和基本群上的循环序，研究它们如何产生Thurston范数对偶单位球中的整点。

Result: 讨论了这些不同结构在Thurston欧拉类猜想背景下的已知结果和联系，展示了各种几何结构如何与Thurston范数理论相互作用。

Conclusion: Thurston的欧拉类猜想在不同几何结构背景下得到了多角度的研究，这些结构为理解3-流形拓扑与几何的深刻联系提供了丰富视角。

Abstract: In his influential work, Thurston introduced a norm on the second homology group of compact orientable 3-manifolds M, which by duality also determines a dual norm on the second cohomology group. A natural question, initiated by Thurston, is whether integral points on the boundary of the dual norm ball have a geometric interpretation. Thurston showed that the Euler class of the oriented tangent plane field to any taut foliation of M lies in the dual unit ball, and conjectured that, conversely, any integral point on the boundary of the dual unit ball is realised as the Euler class of a taut foliation. In this chapter, we discuss how several geometric, topological, and dynamical structures on a 3-manifold give rise to integral points in the dual unit ball of the Thurston norm, and what is known about Thurston's Euler class one conjecture in these contexts. These structures are taut foliations, tight contact structures, pseudo-Anosov flows, quasigeodesic flows, and circular orders on the fundamental group.

### [6] [L-fuzzy simplicial homology](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08166)
*Javier Perera-Lago,Alvaro Torras-Casas,Rocio Gonzalez-Diaz*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文提出了L-模糊单纯同调理论，将经典单纯同调推广到L-模糊子复形，其中每个单纯形被赋予完全分配格L中的值，并讨论了其计算方法和在拓扑数据分析中的应用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 经典单纯同调是研究单纯复形拓扑性质的重要工具，但传统方法无法处理模糊或不精确的数据结构。为了将同调理论扩展到模糊数学领域，需要建立适用于模糊子复形的同调理论框架。

Method: 引入L-模糊单纯同调的概念，其中每个单纯形被赋予完全分配格L中的值。定义了相应的同调群，研究了其主要性质，并描述了计算其结构的方法。还将该理论应用于偏序集上的滤过结构和彩色数据集。

Result: 成功建立了L-模糊单纯同调的理论框架，证明了其基本性质，并展示了如何计算这种同调结构。该理论能够处理偏序集上的滤过和彩色数据集，为拓扑数据分析提供了新的数学工具。

Conclusion: L-模糊单纯同调是经典单纯同调的有意义推广，为处理模糊数学结构和拓扑数据分析中的复杂数据集提供了理论框架，具有进一步应用的潜力。

Abstract: Simplicial homology is a classical tool that assigns a sequence of modules to a simplicial complex, providing invariants for the study of its topological properties. In this article, we introduce the notion of L-fuzzy simplicial homology, a generalization of simplicial homology for L-fuzzy subcomplexes, in which each simplex is assigned a value from a completely distributive lattice L. We present its definition and main properties and describe methods to compute its structure. In addition, we interpret filtrations over a poset and chromatic datasets in this setting, opening a door to further applications in topological data analysis.

</details>

### [1] [Mazur's knot and the Octahedron](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17335)
*Jack S. Calcut,Yangyang Du*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文利用单个正则理想八面体给出了Mazur结补空间在S¹×S²中的几何描述，并将该八面体框架推广到Sparks引入的Jester流形族。通过双曲几何、双曲Dehn填充以及收缩测地线的最新结果，证明了所有Mazur和Jester流形的边界（无论定向如何）两两不同胚，进而对应的紧致可缩4-流形也两两不同胚。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立Mazur结补空间在S¹×S²中的明确双曲几何结构，并证明Mazur流形与Jester流形边界的互异性和对应4-流形的非同胚性，从而区分这些重要的低维拓扑对象。

Method: 使用单个正则理想八面体构造Mazur结补空间的双曲度量；通过Adams关于三次穿孔球面的定理建立与Whitehead链环补空间的联系；将八面体框架推广到Jester流形族；综合运用双曲几何、双曲Dehn填充技术和收缩测地线的最新结果进行分类证明。

Result: 证明了所有Mazur流形和Jester流形的边界两两不同胚（不考虑定向），进而对应的紧致可缩4-流形也彼此非同胚。

Conclusion: 成功给出了Mazur结补空间的双曲几何描述，并利用双曲几何工具彻底区分了Mazur和Jester流形族，解决了该类流形的分类问题。

Abstract: Mazur's knot exterior in $S^1\times S^2$ admits a geometric description using a single regular ideal octahedron. The resulting hyperbolic structure is closely related to the Whitehead link exterior through Adams' theorem on thrice-punctured spheres. The same octahedral framework applies to the family of Jester manifolds introduced by Sparks. Using hyperbolic geometry, hyperbolic Dehn filling, and recent results on systolic geodesics, we prove that the boundaries of all Mazur and Jester manifolds are pairwise nonhomeomorphic, regardless of orientation. Consequently, the corresponding compact, contractible $4$-manifolds are pairwise nonhomeomorphic.

### [3] [A characterization of the spectral Lie operad](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17313)
*Max Blans,Gijs Heuts*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文研究了谱李代数的∞-范畴结构，发现它带有一种由尖空间smash积类比定义的对称幺半结构，自由李代数函子关于该结构是对称幺半的，且这一性质本质上刻画了谱李Operad。通过Goodwillie微分和Arone-Ching链规则，作者将空间中的熟知结论（如Mather第二立方引理、James构造、Hilton-Milnor分裂、EHP序列）推广到谱李代数情形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解谱李代数∞-范畴的结构，特别是发现其与尖空间∞-范畴的深层类比关系，并利用这种类比将空间中的经典结果推广到谱李代数中。

Method: 运用Goodwillie微积分中的"微分"思想，借助Blans-Blom建立的Arone-Ching链规则的高度结构化推广，将尖空间中的已知结论转化为谱李代数中的相应结论。

Result: 证明了谱李代数∞-范畴具有对称幺半结构（由smash积类比定义），自由李代数函子关于该结构是对称幺半的；揭示了这一性质本质刻画了谱李Operad（在非单值谱Operad中）；将Mather第二立方引理、James构造与loop- suspensions的关系、Hilton-Milnor分裂、EHP序列等空间经典结果推广到了谱李代数。

Conclusion: 谱李代数∞-范畴与尖空间∞-范畴具有深刻的类比关系，可通过Goodwillie微分将空间的熟知事实系统地"转录"到谱李代数中，这为理解谱李代数提供了新的视角和强大工具。

Abstract: In this paper we study the structure of the $\infty$-category of spectral Lie algebras. We show that this $\infty$-category admits an interesting symmetric monoidal structure, defined by an analog of the smash product of pointed spaces, and that the free Lie algebra functor $\mathrm{Sp} \to \mathrm{Lie}(\mathrm{Sp})$ is symmetric monoidal with respect to it. Moreover, this property of the free functor essentially characterizes the spectral Lie operad (among nonunital operads in spectra). This result may be thought of as Koszul dual to the more familiar fact that the free commutative algebra functor takes direct sums to tensor products. One of the key ideas is that the $\infty$-category of spectral Lie algebras behaves in many ways like the $\infty$-category of pointed spaces. More precisely, we deduce structural facts about spectral Lie algebras from familiar statements about spaces by differentiating, in the sense of Goodwillie calculus. The tool to do this is the highly structured generalization of Arone-Ching's chain rule established by Blans-Blom. Numerous other features of spectral Lie algebras follow as well, such as a version of Mather's second cube lemma, the relation between the James construction and loop-suspensions, the Hilton-Milnor splitting, and a version of the EHP sequence.

### [4] [A Persistent Homology Signature of Knotting](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17366)
*Aurelie Jodelle Kemme,Collins A. Agyingi,Colleen Farrelly,Agnese Barbensi*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文探究了是否可以利用持久同调来识别扭结。从曲线的点云表示出发，计算一维持久同调，提取圈代表元，并基于超图曲率为这些圈打分。该方法在蛋白质家族和合成示例中均能系统地区分打结与未打结的结构，表明打结留下了可检测的持久同调特征。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索持久同调是否能够作为一种识别扭结的工具，特别是受到蛋白质中扭结现象的启发，希望建立一种基于拓扑数据分析的扭结检测方法。

Method: 从曲线的点云表示计算一维持久同调，提取持久圈代表元，然后为每个圈分配一个基于超图曲率的分数，通过该分数区分打结与未打结结构。

Result: 该方法在蛋白质家族和合成示例中均能系统地区分打结与未打结的结构，表明扭结留下了可检测的持久同调特征。

Conclusion: 持久同调可以作为一种有效的工具来检测和区分扭结结构，为蛋白质等复杂结构中的拓扑分析提供了新思路。

Abstract: We ask whether knotting can be recognised using persistent homology. Starting from a point-cloud representation of a curve, we compute one-dimensional persistent homology, extract cycle representatives, and assign a hypergraph curvature-based score to these cycles. Motivated by proteins but tested more broadly, the method reveals systematic differences between knotted and unknotted structures in both protein families and synthetic examples. This suggests that knotting leaves a detectable persistent-homology-based signature.

### [5] [Unveiling topology in imaging problems via quasi-isometry and persistent homology](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17632)
*Elli Karvonen,Matti Lassas,Pekka Pankka*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文证明可以通过带噪声的间接测量（如X射线和EIT成像）恢复未知物体的拓扑结构（环、空洞、高维孔洞），并给出了基于持久同调与拟等距映射的理论保证和尺寸界限。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在实际成像问题中，测量数据往往是带噪声且间接的，直接从这些数据中恢复物体的拓扑结构（如环、空洞等）存在困难。作者希望通过理论框架保证这些结构在噪声下仍然可恢复。

Method: 利用持久同调（persistent homology）作为核心工具，假设原始空间与带有噪声的测量模型空间之间存在拟等距（quasi-isometry），从而在理论层面建立拓扑结构恢复的保证。

Result: 证明了在拟等距条件下，原始空间的持久同调信息可以从噪声且离散的模型空间中恢复；不仅保证了拓扑结构的存在性，还给出了这些结构的尺寸界限。

Conclusion: 该理论框架适用于X射线成像和电阻抗成像（EIT）等成像问题，为从间接噪声测量中恢复拓扑结构提供了理论支撑。

Abstract: We show that the topological structures, such as loops, voids, and higher-dimensional holes of unknown objects (of flow of an object in space-time) can be recovered from noisy and indirect measurements. More precisely, we describe how the part of the persistent homology of a space can be determined from a noise-prone and discretized model space when there is a quasi-isometry between the original space and the space modeling indirect measurements. The result not only guarantees the existence of the structures but also provides size bounds for them. The structure is studied using persistent homology, and the results assume the existence of a quasi-isometry between a model space and the noisy measurements. We explore imaging problems, particularly X-ray imaging and EIT, that are well-suited to this framework.

*Pietro Aldrigo*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文构造性地证明了在完备度量空间 $(\mathrm{PL}_\infty^p(\mathbb{R}^n),\mathfrak{d}_\infty^p)$ 中，任意两个绝对连续测度 $μ,ν$ 之间存在绝对连续曲线，适用于所有 $1\leq p\leq \infty$。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在最优传输和测度论中，研究绝对连续测度空间中测度之间的连续路径（绝对连续曲线）的存在性，对于理解空间几何结构和动态传输问题至关重要。

Method: 采用构造性证明方法，直接在 $\mathrm{PL}_\infty^p(\mathbb{R}^n)$ 空间中构建连接任意两个绝对连续测度的绝对连续曲线。

Result: 成功证明了对于所有 $1\leq p\leq \infty$，在完备度量空间 $(\mathrm{PL}_\infty^p(\mathbb{R}^n),\mathfrak{d}_\infty^p)$ 中，任意一对测度 $μ,ν$ 之间均存在绝对连续曲线。

Conclusion: 该结论丰富了绝对连续测度空间的几何性质，为进一步研究该空间上的测地线、梯度流等动态问题提供了基础。

Abstract: We give a constructive proof for existence of absolutely continuous curves connecting each pair $μ,ν\in \mathrm{PL}_\infty^p(\mathbb{R}^n)$, for every $1\leq p\leq \infty$, where $(\mathrm{PL}_\infty^p(\mathbb{R}^n),\mathfrak{d}_\infty^p)$ is the complete metric space of absolutely continuous measures with density in $L^p(\mathbb{R}^n)$ and bounded support introduced in [1].

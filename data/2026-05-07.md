*Yuka Kotorii,Atsuhiko Mizusawa*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文实现了Habegger-Lin算法，用于判断链接是否链环同伦，并发现了新的无法被Milnor不变量区分的链环同伦不等价链接对。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Habegger和Lin给出了链环同伦分类与字符串链接模群作用之间的关系，但该算法在实际应用中计算复杂；作者此前已显式计算了4分量和5分量情况下的群作用，本文旨在实现该算法并寻找新的区分案例。

Method: 基于Habegger-Lin算法，利用之前工作中显式计算的4分量和5分量群作用，编写了可运行的计算机程序来实现该算法。

Result: 实现了该算法并发现了新的链环对，它们在Milnor的$\overline\mu$-不变量下无法区分，但实际不是链环同伦的。

Abstract: Habegger and Lin gave a classification of link-homotopy classes of links in terms of that of string links modulo certain group actions. As an application, they constructed an algorithm for determining whether given two links are link-homotopic. In \cite{KM4}, we explicitly computed these group actions for the 4- and 5-component cases. Consequently, the Habegger--Lin algorithm can be effectively applied in these cases. In this paper, we present an implementation of this algorithm, which is available at \cite{KMcode}, and exhibit new pairs of links that are not link-homotopic yet cannot be distinguished by Milnor's link-homotopy invariants, called $\overlineμ$-invariants.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 利用Bruhat分解提供的胞腔同调，通过计算Weyl群元素不同约化分解间坐标变换映射的度数，分析李括号和指数恒等式下的交换与辫关系，采用Weyl群元素的标准型作为约化分解的典范选择，建立显式的算法实现。

By adopting the normal form of Weyl group elements as a canonical choice for reduced decompositions, we establish an explicit algorithmic implementation for these homology computations. As a direct application, we derive the Poincaré polynomials for the classical types $B_n, C_n$, and $D_n$ for $n \leqslant 7$, and for the exceptional types $F_4, E_6$, and $E_7$. ### [6] [Stable homotopy theory of higher categories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05195)
*Hadrian Heine*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文证明稳定同伦论原理可推广到高维范畴论：通过反演endo范畴，高维范畴的稳定同伦论得以建立，其中范畴谱分类了范畴同调理论，并推广了Brown可表示性定理。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将稳定同伦论的核心原理（反演环路空间后，同伦型成为同调理论的表示对象）推广到高维范畴论中，建立高维范畴的稳定同伦理论。

Method: 通过反演endo范畴（内射范畴）来构造高维范畴的稳定同伦论；利用范畴谱作为表示对象；在稳定范围内控制从非稳定到稳定同伦论的过渡。

Result: 建立了范畴Brown可表示性定理，证明范畴同调理论可由范畴谱分类；范畴同调理论具有长正合序列并支持高维同调代数；构造了rig的导出范畴，将同调代数推广到非加法语境。

Classical stable homotopy theory is recovered by inverting morphisms. While several fundamental features of classical stable homotopy theory persist in this setting, new phenomena arise from categorical dimension. In particular, stabilization is realized by spectrum objects, and the passage from unstable to stable homotopy theory is controlled within a stable range. Our main result is a categorical Brown representability theorem classifying categorical homology theories by categorical spectra. As a consequence, categorical homology theories give rise to long exact sequences and support a higher-categorical homological algebra. As an application, we construct the derived category of a rig, extending homological algebra beyond additive contexts.

### [7] [A characterization of the ellipsoid in terms of pairs of sections associated by a harmonic homology](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04398)
*Efrén Morales-Amaya*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文证明了在n维实射影空间（n≥3）中，若凸体K的任意超截面在某种调和同调下可相互映射，则K必为椭球。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 通过超截面的调和同调映射性质来刻画椭球，推广了经典的特征化理论。

Method: 利用射影几何中的调和同调（harmonic homology），以超平面H中的(n-2)-平面l为条件，构造中心在H上、平面包含l的映射，比较两个不同内点p₁,p₂定义的超截面。

Result: 在给定条件下，若所有超截面均可通过此类调和同调相互映射，则K是椭球。

Conclusion: 该结果提供了一种新的椭球射影特征化条件，深化了对凸体几何与射影几何之间联系的理解。

Abstract: Let $K$ be a convex body in an affine chart of the $n$ dimensional real Projective space $\mathbb{RP}^n$, $n \geq 3$, let $H$ be a hyperplane which is not a support hyperplane of $K$ and let $p_1,p_2 \in \mathbb{RP}^n \setminus H$ be two distinct interior points of $K$. In this work we prove that if for every $(n-2)$-plane $l \subset H$, there exists a harmonic homology, with plane $G$ and center $τ$, such that $l\subset G$, $τ\in H$ and which maps the hypersection of $K$ defined by aff$\{p_1, l\}$ onto the hypersection of $K$ defined by aff$\{p_2, l\}$, then $K$ is an ellipsoid.

</details>

*Gage Martin,Mary Stelow,Mira Wattal*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文为任意函子化链环理论在S^3×I中定义的skein模块提供了1、2、3-柄附着的通用公式，推广了现有结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Skein lasagna模块是研究4-流形的新工具，需要发展其手柄附着理论以更好地理解4-流形结构。

Method: 通过引入边界中带有特殊3-流形的skein模块变体，完全描述了skein模块上的粘合同态，从而推导出手柄附着公式。

Result: 得到了1、2、3-柄附着的通用公式，推广了Chen、Manolescu-Neithalath、Manolescu-Walker-Wedrich和Ren-Willis等人的现有结果。

Conclusion: 本文为skein lasagna模块理论提供了系统的手柄附着框架，这一构造与Blackwell-Krushkal-Luo近期独立提出的类似工作相呼应。

Abstract: Skein lasagna modules are a recent tool developed for the study of 4-manifolds. We provide general formula for 1-, 2-, and 3-handle attachments for skein modules defined with any functorial link theory in $S^3 \times I$ generalizing existing formula of Chen, Manolescu-Neithalath, Manolescu-Walker-Wedrich, and Ren-Willis. These formula are derived from a complete description of the gluing homomorphism on skein modules. For this description, we introduce a variation of these skein modules in the presence of distinguished 3-manifolds in the boundary. A similar construction was recently introduced independently by Blackwell-Krushkal-Luo.

TL;DR: 研究非分裂正辫链补空间上的紧叶状结构，证明满足特定条件的Dehn手术产生非L-空间且存在共定向紧叶状结构


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究三维球面中非分裂正辫链补空间上的紧叶状结构，探索Dehn手术与L-空间性质之间的关系

Method: 分析非分裂正辫链的补空间，研究满足条件s_i < 2g(L_i)-1的多斜率Dehn手术，构造共定向紧叶状结构

Result: 证明当至少一个分支不是unknot时，满足上述条件的Dehn手术产生非L-空间且存在共定向紧叶状结构

### [3] [Stuck Knots: Rigidity, Invariants, and Unsticking Distance](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.18129)
*Ioannis Diamantis*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 论文提出了"卡住结"的概念，这是一种包含指定"卡住交叉点"的结图，这些交叉点的相邻弦必须保持局部不可分离，从而引入刚性约束并限制环境同痕。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究受刚性约束的结嵌入，将卡住交叉点建模为空间嵌入中的局部刚性配置，将卡住结与刚性空间图理论联系起来，同时保留经典的交叉点上下信息和方向。

Method: 1. 形式化卡住结的对应同痕概念；2. 引入"解卡移动"来释放刚性；3. 构造多项式不变量（包括HOMFLYPT型不变量和扩展Kauffman括号的状态和模型）；4. 引入"松弛同痕"框架和"解卡距离"几何度量。

Result: 刚性约束即使底层经典结类型保持不变，也能贡献独立信息。不变量能够检测刚性结构，解卡距离量化了关联两个卡住结所需释放的最小刚性约束数量。

Conclusion: 卡住交叉点可视为同痕的障碍，约束释放在图解简化中起重要作用。该框架为受约束结提供了拓扑基础，连接了结理论和刚性空间图理论。

Abstract: A {\it stuck knot} is a knot diagram containing designated crossings, called {\it stuck crossings}, whose incident strands are required to remain locally non-separable. These rigidity constraints restrict the allowable ambient isotopies and introduce new geometric features into the study of knot embeddings. In this paper we develop a topological framework for knots governed by such constraints. We model stuck crossings as locally rigid configurations in spatial embeddings, placing stuck knots in close relation to rigid spatial graph theory while preserving the classical over-under information and orientation of crossings. We formalize the corresponding notion of isotopy and introduce the {\it unstick move}, which releases rigidity and allows classical simplifications to occur. To detect rigid structure algebraically, we construct polynomial invariants for stuck knots, including a HOMFLYPT-type invariant and a state-sum model extending the Kauffman bracket. These invariants show that rigidity contributes independent information even when the underlying classical knot type remains fixed. We further introduce a {\it relaxed isotopy} framework and define the {\it unsticking distance}, a geometric measure quantifying the minimal number of rigidity constraints that must be released in order to relate two stuck knots. This perspective interprets stuck crossings as barriers to isotopy and highlights the role of constraint release in diagrammatic simplification.

</details>

*Luca Iffland*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 证明了旋转体在局部对数Brunn-Minkowski不等式，并推广到对称与非对称旋转体组合的情况


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究旋转体在Brunn-Minkowski理论中的局部对数不等式，扩展该经典不等式到更一般的几何体组合

Method: 采用算子理论方法，结合球面函数关于固定轴旋转的等型分量分解技术

Result: 证明了旋转体的局部对数Brunn-Minkowski不等式，并给出了对称与非对称旋转体组合的推广形式，讨论了等式成立条件

Conclusion: 成功建立了旋转体的局部对数Brunn-Minkowski不等式理论框架，为凸几何分析提供了新的工具和结果

Abstract: We prove the local logarithmic Brunn-Minkowski inequality for bodies of revolution. Furthermore, we give a generalization for one origin symmetric body of revolution and one body of revolution that does not need to be symmetric. Equality cases are discussed. The proof uses an operator theoretic approach together with the decomposition of spherical functions into isotypical components with respect to rotations around a fixed axis.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究具有组合对称性的过约束空间连杆机构，特别是包含八个刚体和十二个转动副的对称结构，探索其在空间位姿下的对称性

*Denis Marti*

Main category: math.MG

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究在微分流形同胚的度量空间中，奇异同调与积分流同调之间的关系，特别是寻找保证两者等价的条件

Method: 通过分析积分流同调与奇异同调之间的映射关系，利用一维等周不等式作为关键工具

Result: 证明了在适当条件下，存在从一维积分流同调群到一维奇异同调群的满同态，且一维等周不等式可保证两个同调群完全一致

Conclusion: 在微分流形同胚的度量空间中，积分流同调与奇异同调在一定条件下等价，一维等周不等式是保证这种等价性的关键条件

Abstract: We compare singular homology and homology via integral currents in metric spaces that are homeomorphic to smooth manifolds. For such spaces, we provide sufficient conditions that guarantee the existence of a surjective homomorphism from the codimension one homology group via integral currents to the codimension one singular homology group. Moreover, we show that a one-dimensional isoperimetric inequality for integral currents implies that the one-dimensional homology groups coincide.

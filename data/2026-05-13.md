### [1] [Thin surface subgroups of non-uniform arithmetic lattices in $\rm{SO}^+(n,1)$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11129)
*Sara Edelman-Muñoz,Michael Zshornack*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 论文证明了对于n≥4的所有非紧算术双曲n-流形，其基本群都包含薄曲面子群。同时，尖点算术双曲n-流形的加倍的基本群可以嵌入为SO⁺(n+1,1)的GFERF子群。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究高维非紧算术双曲流形的基本群中是否存在薄曲面子群，这是几何群论中关于子群结构的重要问题。

Method: 通过结合双曲几何、算术群理论和群的GFERF性质，利用流形的拓扑构造（如加倍）来证明基本群的嵌入性质。

Result: 证明了n≥4时非紧算术双曲n-流形的基本群包含薄曲面子群；同时证明了尖点算术双曲n-流形的加倍的基本群可嵌入为SO⁺(n+1,1)的GFERF子群。

Conclusion: 该结果拓展了对高维双曲流形基本群子群结构的理解，揭示了算术双曲流形中曲面子群的普遍存在性及其代数性质。

Abstract: We show that the fundamental groups of all non-compact, arithmetic, hyperbolic, $n$-manifolds for $n\geq 4$ contain thin surface subgroups. As a consequence of the proof of this theorem we also show that the fundamental groups of the doubles of cusped, arithmetic, hyperbolic $n$-manifolds embed as GFERF subgroups of $\rm{SO}^+(n+1,1)$.

### [2] [A Stable Distance Persistence Homology for Dynamic Bayesian Network Clustering](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11226)
*Will Bales,Carmen Rovi*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种将拓扑数据分析（特别是持久同调）应用于动态贝叶斯网络的方法，通过构建动态贝叶斯图（DBG）并使用条形码来总结变量间依赖结构随时间的演变，并证明了该方法的稳定性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 标准DBN推理方法关注局部条件分布，可能忽略变量间依赖关系如何在大尺度上组织和随时间变化的模式。

Method: 为DBN中的每条边分配一个衡量其条件依赖在父配置间变异程度的强度值，通过阈值筛选得到动态贝叶斯图（DBG），然后应用持久同调生成条形码来记录强依赖变量组的合并与消失。

Result: 证明了生成的条形码具有稳定性，即条件概率表的小扰动只会导致条形码的小变化。

Conclusion: 该方法提供了一种有原则且抗噪声的方式来总结动态贝叶斯网络中依赖结构的演变。

Abstract: Dynamic Bayesian networks (DBNs) are a widely used framework for modeling systems whose probabilistic structure evolves over time. Standard inference methods focus on local conditional distributions and can miss larger-scale patterns in how dependencies between variables organize and change over time. We introduce a topological approach to this problem. To each DBN we associate a time-varying graph, called a Dynamic Bayesian Graph (DBG), by assigning to each edge a strength that measures variation in its conditional dependence across parent configurations, and retaining edges whose strength exceeds a chosen threshold. We show that this construction fits within the dynamic graph framework of Kim and Mémoli, enabling the use of tools from topological data analysis. Applying persistent homology to a DBG produces a barcode, which records the merging and disappearance of connected groups of strongly dependent variables over time. We prove that this barcode is stable: small perturbations in the conditional probability tables of the DBN lead to small changes in the resulting barcode. This yields a principled and noise-resistant summary of how dependency structure evolves in a dynamic Bayesian network.

</details>

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 通过构造障碍理论，分析 $\mathbb{A}_{n}$-代数结构在稳定∞-范畴中的性质，并利用合成谱技术进行具体应用。

Result: 成功构建了障碍理论，并证明了 $\mathbb{S}/4$ 具有 $\mathbb{A}_5$ 乘法结构。

Abstract: We give a construction of the obstruction theory for $\mathbb{A}_{n}$-algebra structures in stable $\infty$-categories, and give some properties of it. We use this to show that the spectrum $\mathbb{S} / 4$ admits an $\mathbb{A}_5$-multiplication using synthetic spectra.

### [4] [Quillen-McCord theorem for persistence finite posets](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11615)
*Vitalii Guzeev,Kohei Tanaka*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文建立了持久化有限偏序集的Quillen-McCord定理的持久化版本，并给出了当映射f的同伦纤维弱ε-可缩时，P与Q之间同伦交换交错距离的上界。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将经典的Quillen-McCord定理推广到持久化设定中，以处理持久化有限偏序集之间的同伦性质，并量化它们之间的距离。

Method: 考虑映射f: P→Q，其中P和Q是持久化有限偏序集，且f具有弱ε-可缩的同伦纤维，基于此建立持久化版本的Quillen-McCord定理。

Result: 给出了P与Q之间同伦交换交错距离的一个上界。

Conclusion: 为持久化有限偏序集的同伦比较提供了理论工具，并量化了映射诱导的距离上界。

Abstract: In this paper, we establish a persistence version of the Quillen-McCord theorem for persistence finite posets. Given a map $f \colon P \rightarrow Q$ between persistence finite posets $P$ and $Q$ with weakly $\varepsilon$-contractible homotopy fibers, we provide an upper bound for the homotopy commutative interleaving distance between $P$ and $Q$.

### [5] [Self-similar dendrites with finite boundary and P-sprouts](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11833)
*Andrei Tetenov,Ivan Yudin,Dmitrii Drozdov*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文研究了具有有限自相似边界的自相似分形树K，通过定义其"芽"（一个有限无环边标记二分图G），证明了该有向图完全决定了分形树的组合性质与拓扑结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立自相似分形树与其对应的有限图（芽）之间的联系，以便用图论工具刻画分形树的组合与拓扑性质。

Method: 针对每个具有有限自相似边界的自相似分形树K，构造一个有限无环边标记二分图G（称为K的芽），并分析K与G之间的对应关系。

Result: 证明了芽G决定了自相似分形树K的组合性质及其拓扑结构。

Conclusion: 自相似分形树的组合与拓扑结构可由一个简单的有限图（芽）完全刻画，为分形树的研究提供了新的图论视角。

Abstract: Each self-similar dendrite K with a finite self-similar boundary defines a finite acyclic edge-labeled bipartite graph G, called the sprout of K. The paper shows that the sprout G determines the combinatorial properties of the dendrite K and its topological structure.

</details>

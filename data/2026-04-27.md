### [1] [Branched Bending in Finite-Volume Hyperbolic Manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22004)
*Casandra D. Monroe*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文推广了Johnson-Millson的弯曲形变理论，定义了在分段全测地复形上的分支弯曲形变，给出了无穷小形变空间维数的下界，并将其应用于双曲几何和实射影几何中的形变问题，特别在Borromean环链补空间上构造了无穷小形变，复现了Menasco-Reid定理的一个特例。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 推广Johnson-Millson提出的弯曲形变(bending deformations)概念，建立更一般的"分支弯曲形变"(branched bending deformations)理论框架，并推广Bart-Scannell的相关结果。

Method: 在分段全测地复形上定义分支弯曲形变，给出无穷小形变空间维数的下界估计；将形变方程应用于高维双曲几何和实射影几何的形变设定中；以Borromean环链补空间作为特例构造无穷小形变。

Result: 得到了分支弯曲形变无穷小形变空间维数的下界；给出了在双曲几何和实射影几何中的形变方程；在Borromean环(链$6^3_2$)的补空间上构造了无穷小形变，复现了Menasco-Reid定理的一个特例。

Conclusion: 分支弯曲形变是弯曲形变的自然推广，具有更广泛的普适性，可用于研究高维双曲几何和实射影几何中的形变问题。

Abstract: We define branched bending deformations as deformations supported on a piecewise totally geodesic complex of $(n-1)$-dimensional faces meeting along $(n-2)$-dimensional branching loci. These are a generalization of bending deformations, as introduced by Johnson and Millson. We give a lower bound on the dimension of the (infinitesimal) deformation space supported on a branched bending complex, and in doing so generalize a result of Bart and Scannell. We give equations describing these deformations in the setting of deforming to higher hyperbolic geometry and real projective geometry. As a special example of branched bending, we construct infinitesimal deformations supported on the link complement of the Borromean Rings (also known as the link $6^3_2$), recovering a special case of a theorem due to Menasco and Reid.

### [2] [Finiteness of integral representations on 2-perfect truncation polytopes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22243)
*Sunghwan Ko*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文证明了紧致双曲Coxeter截断多面体的orbifold基本群在几何分量中只包含有限多个整表示，该结论也适用于更一般的不可约、大、2-完美截断多面体。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究双曲Coxeter截断多面体对应的orbifold基本群在Hom(Γ,G)/G中几何分量的整表示有限性问题，这是理解实射影结构形变空间的重要一步。

Method: 通过将几何分量识别为Coxeter orbifold上凸实射影结构的形变空间，利用紧致双曲截断多面体的几何性质和代数方法研究整表示的有限性。

Result: 证明了在维数d≥3的紧致双曲Coxeter截断多面体情形，几何分量中仅包含有限多个整表示；该结果还可推广到不可约、大、2-完美截断多面体。

Conclusion: 几何分量中整表示是有限的，这一发现对理解相关orbifold的形变空间结构有重要意义。

Abstract: Let $P$ be a compact hyperbolic Coxeter truncation polytope of dimension $d\ge 3$, and let $Γ$ be the orbifold fundamental group of the associated Coxeter orbifold $\mathcal{O}_P$. Let $\mathscr{G}(Γ,G)$ be the geometric component containing the holonomy representation in $\operatorname{Hom}(Γ,G)/G$. $\mathscr{G}(Γ,G)$ is identified with the deformation space of properly convex real projective structures on the Coxeter orbifold $\mathcal{O}_P$. We prove that $\mathscr{G}(Γ,G)$ contains only finitely many integral representations. The same conclusion holds more generally for irreducible, large, $2$-perfect truncation polytopes.

### [3] [A universal non-embedding theorem for 3-manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22387)
*Giulio Belletti,Renaud Detcherry*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文证明了在温和条件下，给定两个紧致定向3-流形N和M，总存在一个双曲3-流形N'与N“密切相关”但不能嵌入M。主要技巧是利用曲面映射类群的SO₃-WRT量子表示来构造具有复杂Frohman-Kania-Bartoszyńska理想的3-流形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索双曲3-流形在嵌入关系中的拓扑限制，特别是研究哪些3-流形可以嵌入给定流形M中。

Method: 利用SO₃-Witten-Reshetikhin-Turaev量子表示的强逼近性质，构造具有复杂Frohman-Kania-Bartoszyńska理想的3-流形，从而证明存在不与M相容的嵌入。

Result: 对于满足温和假设的M，存在双曲3-流形N'与N（例如Y_k-等价）密切相关，但N'不能嵌入M。

Conclusion: 双曲3-流形的嵌入性质受到深刻限制，可通过量子拓扑不变量来刻画这种不可嵌入性。

Abstract: We prove that given two compact oriented $3$-manifolds $N$ and $M,$ with $M$ satisfying only a mild hypothesis, there is a hyperbolic $3$-manifold $N'$ arbitrarily ``closely related'' to $N,$ and such that $N'$ does not embed in $M.$ For instance, as a weak version of our main theorem, if $M$ is a rational homology sphere then for any $k\geq 1$ the $3$-manifold $N'$ can be chosen to be $Y_k$-equivalent to $N.$ Our techniques rely on the construction of $3$-manifolds with complicated Frohman--Kania-Bartoszyńska ideals, using the strong approximation for $\mathrm{SO}_3$-Witten-Reshetikhin-Turaev quantum representations of mapping class groups of surfaces.

</details>

### [4] [On formality of diagrams of Eilenberg-MacLane spaces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22435)
*Grigory Solomadin,Antoine Touzé*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文建立了任意高度n≥1的Eilenberg-MacLane空间图在有理数上的形式性，这意味着有理数上的谱序列在第二页坍塌；同时证明了对不含有理数的交换环k，该形式性不成立。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Eilenberg-MacLane空间图的代数性质，特别是其形式性在不同系数环下的成立条件。

Method: 通过函子演算（functor calculus）论证来证明形式性及其不成立的情况。

Result: 在有理数上，任意高度n的EML空间图具有形式性，谱序列在E2页坍塌；在不含有理数的交换环k上，形式性不成立。

Conclusion: EML空间图的形式性依赖于系数环是否包含有理数，这揭示了有理数系数在谱序列坍塌中的关键作用。

Abstract: In this paper, we establish formality (over $\mathbb{Q}$) for diagrams of Eilenberg-MacLane spaces of any height $n\geq 1$. This implies spectral sequence (over $\mathbb{Q}$) collapse at page $2$ for any diagram of EML spaces over any small category. We prove by functor calculus argument that formality does not hold over any fixed commutative ring $\mathbf{k}$ not containing $\mathbb{Q}$, where the category of diagrams is over the category generated by finite direct sums of a cyclic group.

</details>

### [5] [Convergence of Timed-Metric Spaces and Causality](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22362)
*Mauricio Che,Raquel Perales*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种用于时间度量空间的定时Gromov-Hausdorff距离，证明其与Sakovich-Sormani的内在定时Hausdorff距离是双Lipschitz等价的，从而诱导相同的收敛概念。基于Gromov经典紧致性定理，建立了定时Gromov-Hausdorff距离的紧致性定理。进一步研究了时间度量空间的因果结构及其在内在定时Hausdorff收敛下的稳定性，分析了因果零时间度量空间的基本性质，并在附录中包含了Gromov紧致性定理的原始证明。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为时间度量空间提供一种新的距离度量（定时Gromov-Hausdorff距离），并建立其与现有距离的等价关系，以统一收敛理论。同时，探索时间度量空间的因果结构及其稳定性，完善该领域的理论基础。

Method: 通过定义定时Gromov-Hausdorff距离，证明其与Sakovich-Sormani的内在定时Hausdorff距离的双Lipschitz等价性；利用Gromov经典紧致性定理推导定时版本的紧致性定理；分析因果结构在收敛下的稳定性，并研究因果零时间度量空间的性质。

Result: 成功定义了定时Gromov-Hausdorff距离，证明了其与现有时距距离的等价性，建立了紧致性定理，并揭示了因果结构在定时Hausdorff收敛下的稳定性。

Conclusion: 定时Gromov-Hausdorff距离是研究时间度量空间收敛性的有效工具，其与现有理论的等价性验证了该概念的合理性。因果结构的稳定性为后续研究提供了基础，而因果零空间的性质分析拓展了理论的适用范围。

Abstract: We introduce the notion of timed-Gromov--Hausdorff distance for timed-metric spaces. We prove that this distance is bi-Lipschitz equivalent to the intrinsic timed-Hausdorff distance of Sakovich--Sormani, and therefore induces the same notion of convergence. We establish a compactness theorem for the timed Gromov--Hausdorff distance, obtained as a straightforward consequence of Gromov's classical compactness theorem.
  We then investigate the causal structure of timed-metric spaces and the stability of causality under intrinsic timed-Hausdorff convergence. We further analyze causally-null timed-metric spaces and develop several of their basic properties. As a curiosity, we include in an appendix Gromov's original proof of his compactness theorem, as presented in his paper on groups of polynomial growth.

</details>

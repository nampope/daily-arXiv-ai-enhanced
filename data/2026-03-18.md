In particular, we show that the entropy can increase exponentially in terms of the pasting length in the absence of a lower bound on the systole.

### [2] [A Steenrod square on Khovanov homology and a cup-i product](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16221)
*Advika Rajapakse*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 证明了Lipshitz-Sarkar的稳定同伦型定义的Steenrod平方Sq²与Morán通过增广半单纯对象定义的运算𝔰𝔮²在Khovanov同调上一致


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Khovanov同调存在两种不同的Steenrod平方构造方法：Lipshitz-Sarkar通过稳定同伦型定义，Morán通过增广半单纯对象定义。需要验证这两种构造是否等价，特别是对于不能从单纯同调数据计算的Sq²运算。

Method: 通过理论证明建立Lipshitz-Sarkar的Sq²与Morán的𝔰𝔮²之间的等价性。利用Khovanov函子和Burnside范畴中的增广半单纯对象结构进行分析。

Result: 证明了Lipshitz-Sarkar的Sq²与Morán的𝔰𝔮²在链结L的Khovanov同调Kh(L)上完全一致。

Conclusion: 两种不同的Steenrod平方构造方法在Sq²运算上达成一致，为Khovanov同调中的上同调运算提供了统一的视角。

Abstract: Lipshitz-Sarkar defined a stable homotopy type refining Khovanov homology, producing cohomology operations $\text{Sq}^i$ on the Khovanov homology $Kh(L)$ of a link $L$. Later, Morán proposed a sequence of cup-i products on the $\mathbb{F}_2$-coefficient cochain complex of any augmented semi-simplicial object in the Burnside category. Applied to the Khovanov functor, he obtained another sequence of operations $\mathfrak{sq}^n$ on $Kh(L)$, where $\mathfrak{sq}^0$, $\mathfrak{sq}^1$ agree with the usual Steenrod squares. We prove that Lipshitz-Sarkar's $\text{Sq}^2$, the first Steenrod operation that cannot be computed from merely homological data, agrees with Morán's $\mathfrak{sq}^2$.

</details>


### [3] [Perturbative Knot Invariants via Quantum Cluster Algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15859)

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将量子簇代数与纽结理论相结合，通过引入微扰参数ε来系统研究纽结不变量的展开结构，建立量子代数表示论与纽结不变量之间的新联系。

Method: 将Uq(sl2)的R矩阵解释为量子簇代数中的簇变换，引入辅助参数ε构造微扰R矩阵，利用量子环面代数的薛定谔表示和簇突变组合学，通过量子簇代数的表示论产生海森堡代数生成元。

Result: 得到的纽结不变量零阶项等于亚历山大多项式的倒数ΔK(T)^{-1}，高阶ε项产生微扰亚历山大多项式，并通过Mathematica实现和具体例子验证了构造。

Abstract: A perturbative expansion of knot invariants is derived using quantum cluster algebras. By interpreting the $R$-matrix of $U_q(\mathfrak{sl}_2)$ as a cluster transformation and introducing an auxiliary parameter $ε$, we derive a perturbed $R$-matrix expressed in terms of Heisenberg algebra generators arising from the representation theory of the quantum cluster algebra. The resulting knot invariant has a zeroth-order term equal to $Δ_K(T)^{-1}$, the reciprocal of the Alexander polynomial, while higher-order terms in $ε$ produce perturbed Alexander polynomials. The construction combines the Schrödinger representation of the quantum torus algebra with cluster mutation combinatorics and is illustrated with a \textit{Mathematica} implementation and explicit examples.

### [4] [Tree-like is not a transitive relation on paths](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16774)
*Jeremy Brazas,Gregory R. Conner,Paul Fabel,Curtis Kent*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 树状环路的Lipschitz要求被移除后，所得关系不再是等价关系，因为失去了传递性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Hambly和Lyons在2010年引入了树状环路和Lipschitz树状环路的概念，并证明了Lipschitz树状性质在有界变差路径上定义了等价关系。他们询问是否可以将这一概念扩展到非Lipschitz路径。

Method: 通过分析平面上的显式分形构造来构建反例，证明在移除Lipschitz要求后，所得关系不再具有传递性。

Result: 证明了当移除Lipschitz要求时，树状环路关系不再是等价关系，因为它失去了传递性。

Conclusion: Lipschitz条件是树状环路概念形成等价关系的关键要求，移除该条件会导致关系结构崩溃。

Abstract: The notions of tree-like loop and Lipschitz tree-like loop were introduced by Hambly and Lyons in their 2010 Annals of Mathematics paper. They showed that the Lipschitz tree-like property determines an equivalence relation on the set of paths of bounded variation in a given metric space and then asked if this notion could be extended to paths without the Lipschitz requirement. We show that after eliminating the Lipschitz requirement, the resulting relation is no longer transitive and thus is not an equivalence relation. The counterexample is obtained by analyzing an explicit fractal construction in the plane.

</details>

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 构造基于三角形条带的最坏情况示例，取代Morozov的单三角形排列方法，提供显式构造算法，并进行边缘和三角形细分处理。

Main category: math.MG

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Part 2中用于描述平衡的环形式主义扩展到运动学分析，建立统一的4D几何框架来处理框架结构的静力学和运动学问题。

Method: 使用4D空间中的环投影面积方法：三个投影面积表示框架点的位移，另外三个投影面积表示该点的旋转。虚功表现为4D空间中的顶形式（定向4体积），通过平衡变量和运动学变量的双向量楔积在结构上积分得到。

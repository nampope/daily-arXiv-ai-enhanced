### [1] [A combinatorial approach to obstructing left-orderability of Dehn fillings](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15603)
*Adam Clay,Junyu Lu,Tjay Staruch*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文介绍了扭结的共轭斜率性质（Property (D)的变体），用于研究Dehn填充中基本群的不可左序性。该性质适用于S^3中具有特定组合意义上"可控"负交叉点图的扭结，同时限制了扭结群支持的左序类型，并证明了在多数Dehn填充中，子午线的像成为基本群中的广义扭结。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Dehn填充后基本群的不可左序性，这是低维拓扑中群论性质与几何结构关联的核心问题，尤其是将扭结的图示组合性质与代数性质（如广义扭结）联系起来。

Method: 提出共轭斜率性质作为Nie的Property (D)的变体，通过组合条件（负交叉点"可控"）建立性质成立的标准，并利用该性质推导基本群的左序限制和广义扭结存在性。

Result: 证明了该性质对S^3中满足特定图示条件的扭结成立；该性质限制了扭结群可支持的左序类型；证明了在多数Dehn填充中，子午线像成为基本群中的广义扭结元素。

Conclusion: 共轭斜率性质为研究Dehn填充后的基本群性质提供了新工具，建立了组合图示信息与代数结构（左序性、广义扭结）之间的桥梁，对低维拓扑中群论性质的研究有重要推进。

Abstract: We introduce the conjugate-slope property of knots, a variant of Nie's Property (D), which can be used to study non-left-orderability of fundamental groups arising from Dehn filling. We show that this property holds for any knot in $S^3$ admitting a diagram whose negative crossings can be "controlled" in a precise combinatorial sense. The conjugate-slope property also places strong restrictions on the kinds of left-orderings that the knot group can support, and implies that the image of the meridian is generalised torsion in the fundamental group of many of the knot's Dehn fillings.

### [2] [The pants graph as a combinatorial model for the Teichmüller space of a non-orientable surface](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15792)
*Michał Stukow,Błażej Szepietowski,Jakub Szmelter-Tomczuk*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究不可定向曲面的裤子图与其可定向双覆盖的裤子图之间的关系，构建了由提升裤子分解诱导的自然映射，证明其为准等距嵌入，并利用Brock定理得出不可定向曲面的裤子图与其Teichmüller空间（带Weil-Petersson度量）准等距。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将可定向曲面上关于裤子图与Teichmüller空间的经典结果（如Brock的准等距）推广到不可定向曲面情形。

Method: 1) 构造从不可定向曲面N的裤子图到其可定向双覆盖S的裤子图的提升映射；2) 证明该映射是准等距嵌入；3) 利用Brock定理（Pants(S)与Teich(S)在Weil-Petersson度量下准等距）及对合反演作用下不动点集的识别，导出结论。

Result: 证明了不可定向曲面的裤子图Pants(N)准等距嵌入到可定向双覆盖的裤子图Pants(S)中，且Pants(N)与不可定向曲面的Teichmüller空间（赋予诱导的Weil-Petersson度量）准等距。

Conclusion: 建立了不可定向曲面组合结构（裤子图）与双曲几何结构（Teichmüller空间）之间的联系，拓展了可定向曲面上的经典准等距对应到不可定向情形。

Abstract: We study the relation between the pants graph of a non-orientable surface and that of its orientable double cover. Given a non-orientable surface $N$ with orientable double cover $π\colon S\to N$, we construct a natural map between pants graphs induced by lifting pants decompositions. We prove that this map defines a quasi-isometric embedding of $\mathrm{Pants}(N)$ into $\mathrm{Pants}(S)$. Using Brock's quasi-isometry between $\mathrm{Pants}(S)$ and Teichmüller space $\mathrm{Teich}(S)$ endowed with the Weil-Petersson metric, together with the identification of the Teichmüller space of $N$ with the fixed-point locus of the deck involution on $\mathrm{Teich}(S)$, we prove that $\mathrm{Pants}(N)$ is quasi-isometric to the Teichmüller space of $N$ equipped with the induced Weil-Petersson metric.

</details>


### [3] [Dirichlet domains and the NEC uniformization of extremal hyperbolic surfaces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15882)
*Ernesto Girondo,Claudia Muñoz*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究极值双曲曲面，提出基于NEC群均匀化的新方法，描述了Dirichlet域结构，并给出了计数、分类、隐藏极值盘定位及自同构群等应用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 极值双曲曲面是包含最大可能半径内嵌盘的有限型双曲曲面，但目前缺乏统一的、系统的描述方法，特别是对于带有尖点或测地边界的曲面。

Result: 获得了极值盘配置的有效计数公式；确定了所有欧拉示性数为-1和-2的极值曲面配置；定位了多个极值曲面家族中的隐藏极值盘；完整描述了它们的自同构群。

Conclusion: 提出的新方法为极值双曲曲面的研究提供了统一框架，并在计数、分类和结构描述上取得了显著成果。

Abstract: Extremal hyperbolic surfaces are finite-type hyperbolic surfaces that contain an embedded disc of the largest possible radius. We introduce a new general approach to these objects, based on the uniformization by NEC groups, obtaining a structural description of the Dirichlet domains associated to extremal disc centers for surfaces with cusps and/or geodesic boundary. We also provide a number of applications of our new description of extremal surfaces, as an effective counting formula for extremal disc configurations, the determination of every such configuration with Euler characteristic -1 and -2, the location of hidden extremal discs in several families of extremal surfaces, and the full description of their automorphism groups.

### [4] [Profinite rigidity of simple closed curves in surface groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16147)
*Zhongzi Wang,Xiaoyu Xu*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文证明了闭可定向曲面基本群中，若某元素与给定简单闭曲线在所有有限群满同态下像集相同，则该元素必属于该简单闭曲线的自同构群轨道（即具有相同拓扑类型的简单闭曲线）。由此得出简单闭曲线集合在 profinite 拓扑中封闭，并给出判断元素是否代表简单闭曲线的新算法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 已有的简单闭曲线刻画方法（如组合、几何方法）存在局限，作者希望从 profinite 群论（有限群满同态像）角度给出新的代数刻画，进而探索简单闭曲线在 profinite 拓扑下的闭性及判定算法。

Method: 利用 profinite 完备化的思想，通过比较元素在所有有限群满同态下的像集，建立与 Aut(Γ) 轨道的关系；结合曲面基本群的特殊性质（如剩余有限性、曲线 complex 结构）进行论证，并推广至 proper powers 和 pro-p 情形。

Result: 1) 若 g 与简单闭曲线 γ 在所有有限群满同态下像集相同，则 g 在 Aut(Γ) 轨道中（即同为简单闭曲线且拓扑类型相同）；2) 简单闭曲线集在 profinite 拓扑中为闭集；3) 得到判定元素是否代表简单闭曲线的新算法；4) 对 proper powers 和 pro-p 情形有相应结论。

Conclusion: 该工作从 profinite 群论视角给出了简单闭曲线的全新刻画，不仅深化了对曲面基本群代数结构的理解，还提供了实用的判定算法，并为后续在 profinite 刚性、曲线 complex 的 profinite 性质等方向的研究开辟了新途径。

Abstract: This paper establishes a new characterization of simple closed curves on a closed orientable surface. Let $Γ$ be the fundamental group of a closed orientable surface. We prove that if an element $g\inΓ$ has the same possible images as a given simple closed curve $γ\in Γ$ under epimorphisms from $Γ$ to every finite group, then $g$ belongs to the $\mathrm{Aut}(Γ)$-orbit of $γ$, i.e. $g$ is itself a simple closed curve with the same topological type as $γ$. Consequently, the set of simple closed curves in $Γ$ is closed in the profinite topology of $Γ$; and we obtain a new algorithm to decide whether a given element in $Γ$ can be represented by a simple closed curve. Proper powers of simple closed curves and the pro-$p$ cases are also discussed.

</details>

*J. D. P. Evans,R. Sanchez Galan*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文研究 metacyclic 群 G(p,3) 的整群环上的 syzygy 模，探讨其在张量积下的相互作用，并与 Wall 的 D(2) 问题关联。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解特定群环上 syzygy 模的张量积行为，为解决 Wall 的 D(2) 问题（即确定哪些有限群具有 D(2) 性质）提供线索。

Method: 在 p=3d+1 为素数且 2,3 生成乘法群的条件下，构造 Λ=Z[C_p ⋊ C_3] 上的自由分解，研究 syzygy 模 Ω_r(Z) 在 -⊗_Z- 下的稳定类。

Result: 显式描述了 syzygy 模在张量积下的相互作用关系，并与 Wall 的 D(2) 问题建立了联系。

Abstract: Let $p=3d+1$ be prime in which $(\mathbb{Z}/p\mathbb{Z})^\times=\langle 2,\,3\rangle$, and let $Λ=\mathbb{Z}[C_p\rtimes C_3]$ be the integral group ring of the metacyclic group $G(p,\,3)$ of order $3p$. The syzygies $Ω_r(\mathbb{Z})$ are the stable classes of intermediate modules in a free $Λ$-resolution of the trivial module. We discuss explicitly the interaction of the syzygies under $-\otimes_\mathbb{Z}-$ and relate this to Wall's $\mathcal{D}(2)$-problem.

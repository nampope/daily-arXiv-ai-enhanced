### [1] [Large flats in large subgraphs of fine curve graphs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25086)
*Ryan Dickmann,Roberta Shapiro*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究了曲面上的精细曲线图（fine curve graph）的子图双曲性问题，发现某些大子图（如曲线图上顶点的纤维）不是双曲的，且包含任意有限维度的平面，并计算了单同位素类精细曲线图中的距离界。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在曲面相关图（曲线图、精细曲线图）的双曲性已有丰富研究的背景下，探索精细曲线图的子图（特别是纤维子图）的双曲性质，填补对子图几何结构理解的空白。

Method: 通过构造和证明精细曲线图中某些子图（如纤维）包含任意有限维度的平面（flats），从而论证其非双曲性；同时计算单同位素类精细曲线图中的距离界。

Result: 1) 精细曲线图的某些大子图（包括曲线图顶点的纤维）不是双曲的。2) 这些子图包含任意有限维度的平面。3) 给出了单同位素类精细曲线图中距离的界限。

Conclusion: 精细曲线图的大子图（如纤维）在双曲性上表现截然不同，揭示其内部几何比整体图更复杂，不再保持双曲性质。

Abstract: The fine curve graph of a surface is a graph whose vertices are essential simple closed curves and whose edges connect disjoint curves. Following a rich history of hyperbolicity of various graphs associated to surfaces, the fine curve graph was shown to be hyperbolic by Bowden-Hensel-Webb, while the curve graph, obtained from the fine curve graph by collapsing subgraphs corresponding to isotopy classes, was first proven to be hyperbolic by Masur-Minsky.
  We show that certain large subgraphs of fine curve graphs, including fibers over a vertex of the curve graph, are not hyperbolic. Indeed, such graphs contain flats of every finite dimension.
  We then compute bounds on distances in fibers over a vertex of the curve graph, which we call single-isotopy-class fine curve graphs.

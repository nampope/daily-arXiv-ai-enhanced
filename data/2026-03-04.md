### [1] [Tight Bounds for Tight Links: Ropelength of T(Q,Q) torus links](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02416)
*Alexander R. Klotz*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该研究改进了扭结和链环的绳索长度上下界，特别是针对T(Q,Q)环面链环，将上下界的差距缩小到1.77倍以内，并提出了更强的下界估计。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 目前绳索长度与交叉数关系的通用下界常数α₀存在较大范围（1.105-10.76），需要为特定类别的扭结和链环开发更精确的界限。

Method: 研究T(Q,Q)环面链环，基于紧密堆积圆盘的凸包推导更强下界，构造无自由参数的链环，使用平滑平面曲线或环面螺旋线，并通过梯度下降退火优化几何参数。

Result: 获得了显著更强的下界α_T_QQ > 6.60+7.61Q^{-1/2}，构造的链环系数α_T_QQ < 13.39（可优化至11.68），对于Q≤20的情况，绳索长度仅比下界高6-60%，退火后可降至10%以内。

Conclusion: 该研究在开发特定类别扭结和链环绳索长度的尖锐界限方面取得重要进展，显著缩小了上下界差距，为更精确的绳索长度估计奠定了基础。

Abstract: Ropelength, L, is a parameter characterizing the minimum contour length of a knot or link. There exist upper and lower bounds on ropelength with respect to crossing number, C, including a universal lower bound constraining $L\geqα_0 C^{3/4}$ for some constant $α_0$. There is currently an order-of-magnitude range for the value of $α_0$ between 1.105 and 10.76. In this work, we show that T(Q,Q) torus links can be constructed such that the upper bound is within a factor of 1.77 of the lower bound. We derive a stronger lower bound based on the convex hull around close-packed disks of approximately $α_{T_{QQ}}>\sqrt{8π\sqrt{3}}+(2π+\sqrt{2π+7\sqrt{3}-12}\ )Q^{-1/2}\approx6.60+7.61Q^{-1/2}$, significantly higher than the best universal lower bound of 1.105. We show that a link can be constructed without any free parameters or geometric optimization that, when $Q$ is large, has a coefficient $α_{T_{QQ}}<1.005\cdot 4π(5\sqrt{5}-8)/3\approx13.39$, and can be improved to to 11.68 by solving a helical no-overlap constraint equation. For $Q$ up to 20 we construct links from smooth planar curves or toroidal helices minimized with respect to a small number of geometric parameters, that are between 6 and 60% greater in ropelength than the lower bound. Many such links can be annealed to within 10% of the lower bound using gradient descent. This represents significant progress towards developing sharp bounds on the ropelengths of specific classes of knots and links.

### [2] [On Projective and Flat Persistence Modules](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02674)
*Prateep Chakraborty,Giriraj Ghosh*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 研究持久性模在格和预序集索引下的性质，提供PID上投射模为自由的充分条件，建立平坦性判据，并给出计算自由持久性模基的算法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 持久性模通常被视为在预序集上分次的模，但对其代数性质（如投射性、平坦性、自由性）的系统研究尚不充分，特别是在格和预序集等更一般索引集下的性质。

Method: 1. 当索引集为格时，给出PID上投射模为自由的充分条件；2. 建立格索引下模非投射的判据；3. 对预序集索引，证明一类已知持久性模族的平坦性；4. 设计计算ℤ和ℤ²索引自由持久性模基的算法。

Result: 1. 获得格索引下PID上投射模为自由的充分条件；2. 建立格索引下模非投射的判据；3. 证明预序集索引下一类持久性模族的平坦性；4. 开发出计算ℤ和ℤ²索引自由持久性模基的有效算法。

Conclusion: 该研究系统探讨了持久性模在不同类型索引集（格、预序集）下的代数性质，建立了投射性、自由性和平坦性的理论判据，并提供了实用的计算工具，为持久性同调的理论和应用发展提供了重要基础。

Abstract: In recent years, persistence modules have been viewed as graded modules with gradation over a preordered set serving as the indexing set. We provide sufficient criteria for a projective module over a PID to be free when the indexing set is a lattice. With a lattice as the indexing set, we obtain criteria ensuring that a given persistence module is not projective. When the indexing set is a preordered set, we establish the flatness of a well-known family of persistence modules. We end the article with two algorithms to compute a basis of free persistence modules with indexing sets $\mathbb{Z}$ and $\mathbb{Z}^2$.

### [3] [Zigzag Persistence of Neural Responses to Time-Varying Stimuli](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03037)

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 使用拓扑数据分析研究小鼠视觉皮层神经元对视频刺激的响应，通过持续同调捕捉神经活动的拓扑结构演化，发现拓扑特征能可靠区分不同刺激的神经响应


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究复杂动态系统中神经编码的拓扑特征，探索神经群体活动的时间演化如何通过拓扑结构反映，并测试这些拓扑特征是否能区分不同视觉刺激

Method: 使用Sensorium 2023数据集，为每个视频构建逐帧的立方体复形，应用zigzag持续同调捕捉拓扑结构的时间演化，用持续景观进行向量化表示，重点关注一维拓扑特征（数据中的环），通过聚类分析比较不同视频的拓扑神经表征

Result: 拓扑描述符能可靠地区分不同刺激的神经响应，表明神经活动的演化与可解释的拓扑特征之间存在联系

Conclusion: 这项工作突出了神经活动演化与可解释拓扑特征之间的连接，推动了拓扑数据分析在揭示复杂动态系统中神经编码的应用

Abstract: We use topological data analysis to study neural population activity in the Sensorium 2023 dataset, which records responses from thousands of mouse visual cortex neurons to diverse video stimuli. For each video, we build frame-by-frame cubical complexes from neuronal activity and apply zigzag persistent homology to capture how topological structure evolves over time. These dynamics are summarized with persistence landscapes, providing a compact vectorized representation of temporal features. We focus on one-dimensional topological features-loops in the data-that reflect coordinated, cyclical patterns of neural co-activation. To test their informativeness, we compare repeated trials of different videos by clustering their resulting topological neural representations. Our results show that these topological descriptors reliably distinguish neural responses to distinct stimuli. This work highlights a connection between evolving neuronal activity and interpretable topological signatures, advancing the use of topological data analysis for uncovering neural coding in complex dynamical systems.

### [4] [Topology of Multi-species Localization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03237)
*Abhinav Natarajan,Thomas Chaplin,Joshua A. Bull,Eoghan J. Mulholland-Illingworth,Simon J. Leedham,Helen M. Byrne,Maria-Jose Jimenez,Heather A. Harrington*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 提出一种可扩展的拓扑数据分析方法，用于量化多物种数据中的高阶空间交互关系，能够区分四种不同类型的空间模式特征。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多物种数据中的空间关系对系统结果有重要影响（如癌症进展、珊瑚礁恢复），但传统的持续同调方法只能分析单一物种数据，无法检测不同物种间的形状关系。

Method: 利用拓扑数据分析和计算几何的最新进展，开发了一个可扩展框架，能够量化多物种数据中的高阶交互作用，区分四种空间模式特征。

Result: 在合成肿瘤微环境模型中识别了不同的行为机制，并在结直肠癌组织样本中发现了疾病进展过程中显著改变的种类间空间交互作用。

Conclusion: 该方法为跨学科研究提供了强大的工具，能够量化多物种数据中的复杂空间关系，有助于理解从癌症到生态学等各种系统中的相互作用。

Abstract: Spatial relationships in multi-species data can indicate and affect system outcomes and behaviors, ranging from disease progression in cancer to coral reef resilience in ecology; therefore, quantifying these relationships is an important problem across scientific disciplines. Persistent homology (PH), a key mathematical and computational tool in topological data analysis (TDA), provides a multiscale description of the shape of data. While it effectively describes spatial organization of species, such as cellular patterns in pathology, it cannot detect the shape relations between different types of species. Traditionally, PH analyzes single-species data, which limits the spatial analysis of interactions between different species. Leveraging recent developments in TDA and computational geometry, we introduce a scalable approach to quantify higher-order interactions in multi-species data. The framework can distinguish the presence of shape features or patterns in the data that are (i) common to multiple species of points, (ii) present in some species but disappear in the presence of other species, (iii) only visible when multiple species are considered together, and (iv) formed by some species and remain visible in the presence of others. We demonstrate our approach on two example applications. We identify (1) different behavioral regimes in a synthetic tumor micro-environment model, and (2) interspecies spatial interactions that are most significantly altered in colorectal cancer tissue samples during disease progression.

### [5] [Tropical measures, anisotropic isoperimetric inequality and honeycomb](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03215)
*Amnon Rosenmann*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文引入了一种基于热带度量的热带球面测度，它是球面Hausdorff测度的热带类比，具有平移不变性但无旋转/反射不变性，与Lebesgue测度在n维可测集上一致，在可求长曲线上恢复热带长度，并证明了热带等周不等式和蜂窝定理。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统欧几里得几何中的测度理论（如Lebesgue测度、Hausdorff测度）在热带几何中缺乏对应的测度理论。本文旨在建立基于热带度量的测度理论，为热带几何提供分析工具，并研究热带等周问题等经典几何问题的热带版本。

Method: 1. 基于热带度量定义热带球面测度，作为球面Hausdorff测度的热带类比；2. 研究该测度的性质（平移不变性、与Lebesgue测度的关系等）；3. 在二维证明尖锐的热带等周不等式；4. 引入热带Minkowski容度概念；5. 研究热带对偶范数和对偶球；6. 比较热带球面测度和Minkowski表面测度。

Result: 1. 热带球面测度具有平移不变性但无旋转/反射不变性；2. 在n维可测集上与Lebesgue测度一致；3. 在可求长曲线上恢复热带长度；4. 证明了二维尖锐热带等周不等式，等号成立当且仅当热带圆盘；5. 推导出热带蜂窝定理；6. 热带球是相关Wulff形状；7. 在平面和特定多面体上，热带球面测度和Minkowski表面测度一致。

Conclusion: 本文成功建立了热带几何中的测度理论框架，定义了热带球面测度并研究了其基本性质。通过证明热带等周不等式和蜂窝定理，展示了热带几何与经典几何的深刻联系。热带球作为Wulff形状的出现，以及两种表面测度在特定情况下的等价性，进一步丰富了热带几何的分析工具。

Abstract: We introduce a tropical spherical measure on $\mathbb{R}^n$ that is based on the tropical metric and is an analogue of spherical Hausdorff measure. This measure is translation invariant but, unlike Lebesgue measure, is not invariant under rotations or reflections. It agrees with Lebesgue measure on $n$-dimensional (but not on $k$-dimensional, $k<n$) measurable subsets of $\mathbb{R}^n$, and on rectifiable curves it recovers tropical length. In dimension $2$ we prove a sharp tropical isoperimetric inequality, with equality precisely for tropical disks, and deduce a tropical honeycomb theorem. We also introduce a tropical analogue of Minkowski content and show that the tropical ball is the associated Wulff shape. This yields an anisotropic type of the tropical isoperimetric problem and consequently a tropical honeycomb theorem in $\mathbb{R}^n$. Finally, we describe the tropical dual norm and dual ball, compare the tropical spherical and Minkowski surface measures, and prove that they agree in the plane and on polytopes in $\mathbb{R}^n$ whose facets are parallel to facets of the tropical ball or its dual.

</details>

### [1] [On certain subspaces of $2$-configuration spaces of graphs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15636)
*Byung Hee An,Sangrok Oh*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了图辫群的大尺度几何，利用离散配置空间的立方结构，完全分类了何时图n-辫群是自由群，并重点研究了图2-辫群与右角度Artin群的拟等距关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究图辫群的大尺度几何性质，特别是理解这些群何时是自由群，以及图2-辫群与右角度Artin群之间的拟等距关系，扩展相关领域的研究成果。

Method: 使用离散配置空间UD_n(Γ)的立方结构，通过立方方法（不依赖离散Morse理论）分类自由图辫群。对于图2-辫群，研究特殊平方复形UD_2(Γ)，利用最大积子复形和交集复形来分析拟等距性质。

Result: 完全分类了图n-辫群何时是自由群；证明了在连通性和嵌入性假设下，UD_2(Γ)的最大积子复形并集UP_2(Γ)能捕捉B_2(Γ)的拟等距信息；发现了无限多拟等距于右角度Artin群的图2-辫群，以及无限多不拟等距的群。

Conclusion: 立方方法为研究图辫群的大尺度几何提供了有效工具，揭示了图2-辫群与右角度Artin群之间的复杂拟等距关系，并指出了相对双曲性的新现象。

Abstract: We investigate the large-scale geometry of graph braid groups \(\bbB_n(\graf)\) using the cubical structure of their discrete configuration spaces \(UD_n(\graf)\). First, we give a complete classification of when a graph \(n\)-braid group is free, using only cubical methods and without appealing to discrete Morse theory.
  We then focus on graph \(2\)-braid groups and study the square complex \(UD_2(\graf)\), which is special in the sense of Haglund--Wise, via its maximal product subcomplexes and intersection complex introduced in \cite{Oh22}. Under natural connectivity and embeddedness assumptions, we show that the union \(UP_2(\graf)\) of maximal product subcomplexes of \(UD_2(\graf)\) captures essential quasi-isometry information of \(\bbB_2(\graf)\), the fundamental group of \(UD_2(\graf)\).
  Applying this framework to an infinite family of graphs, we obtain infinitely many graph \(2\)-braid groups that are quasi-isometric to right-angled Artin groups and infinitely many that are not, extending the examples of \cite{Oh22}, and we indicate new phenomena in relative hyperbolicity.

### [2] [Pruning distance of upset-decomposable persistence modules](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15243)
*Roy Nicolas Nehme*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 该论文证明了对于upset-decomposable持久性模块，修剪距离与瓶颈距离是Lipschitz等价的，改进了Bjerkevik猜想中的边界，并将边界从2r改进到(2r-1)。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Bjerkevik最近引入了修剪距离来比较持久性模块，并猜想该距离与经典的交错距离在点维有界的模块上是Lipschitz等价的。本文旨在验证这一猜想，特别是针对upset-decomposable模块。

Method: 采用有向图形式化方法，显式计算upset-decomposable模块的修剪，从而建立修剪距离与瓶颈距离之间的定量关系。

Result: 证明了对于upset-decomposable模块，瓶颈距离可以被修剪距离的倍数界定，将猜想中的边界从2r改进到(2r-1)，并证明这个改进边界是紧的。同时证明了修剪距离也可以被瓶颈距离界定。

Conclusion: 该研究为Bjerkevik猜想提供了部分证明，建立了upset-decomposable持久性模块上修剪距离与瓶颈距离的Lipschitz等价性，改进了已知边界并证明了其紧性。

Abstract: The pruning distance recently introduced by Bjerkevik compares persistence modules using approximate decompositions called prunings. Bjerkevik conjectures that this distance is Lipschitz equivalent to the classical interleaving distance on modules of a bounded pointwise dimension. In this article, we establish a Lipschitz equivalence with respect to the bottleneck distance for upset-decomposable persistence modules. In particular, this proves half of Bjerkevik's conjecture for these modules. More precisely, we bound the bottleneck distance by a multiple of the pruning distance, improving the conjectured bound from~$2r$ to~$(2r-1)$ where~$r$ is the maximal pointwise dimension, and show that this improved bound is sharp. We also prove the converse inequality, bounding the pruning distance by the bottleneck distance. Our approach relies on explicitly computing the pruning of upset-decomposable modules, which we carry out using a directed graph formalism.

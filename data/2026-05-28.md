### [1] [Combinatorics of Inflection Points of Plane Curve Shadows](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27471)
*Boris Shapiro*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究具有固定嵌入阴影的浸入闭平面曲线的最小拐点数量问题，提出了一种基于协定向模型的有限公式，并针对树-项链状阴影给出了精确下界，同时讨论了计算复杂度与相关不变量。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解浸入曲线在固定阴影约束下的最小拐点数量，将几何可实现性问题转化为组合优化问题，填补对非树状结构阴影的理论空白。

Method: 将拐点可实现性转化为构建多边形上的有限协定向问题；对树状阴影给出动态规划算法；对树-项链状阴影引入Z₂和乐条件；提出平行切线载荷作为相关不变量。

Result: 对树状阴影给出归一化拐点最小数量的精确有限公式；对树-项链状阴影在满足和乐条件时下界精确；对一般阴影提出NP-hard猜想。

Conclusion: 拐点最小数量问题可通过协定向模型有效求解于树状及树-项链状阴影，一般情形可能计算困难；平行切线载荷是独立但相关的不变量。

Abstract: We study the minimum number of inflection points among generic immersed closed plane curves with a fixed embedded shadow. The word immersed is essential: a genuinely embedded Jordan curve has inflection minimum zero. For tree-like shadows, inflection criterion converts inflection-free realizability into a finite coorientation problem on the building polygons of the shadow. We sharpen this viewpoint into an exact finite formula for the minimum number of normalized inflections and record a dynamic-programming computation on the block tree. We then push the method beyond the tree-like case. For every embedded shadow the same coorientation model gives a universal lower bound. For a natural larger class, called tree--necklace shadows, in which the non-tree-like blocks are separated annular cycles, the lower bound is exact after imposing an explicit $\mathbb Z_2$ holonomy condition around each necklace. We also record the algorithmic status of the exact minimization problem and formulate a likely NP-hardness problem for unrestricted shadows. Finally, we introduce a related invariant: the minimum possible least multiplicity of the Gauss map, equivalently the smallest guaranteed number of oriented parallel tangencies. This ``parallel-tangent load'' is controlled by the same inflection folds but is not determined by their number alone.

### [3] [Homological Nielsen realization for the manifolds $\#_n \mathbb{CP}^2$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27537)

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究了光滑正定4-流形$M_n:=\#_n\mathbb{CP}^2$上的同调Nielsen实现问题，发现虽然每个等距都可由某个保定向微分同胚诱导，但随机子群和随机奇阶元素几乎必然不可实现，并给出了正反两方面的结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 结合等变连通和构造、4-流形群作用的不动点理论、曲面上的有限群作用、解析组合学以及Hambleton-Tanase的先前工作。

Abstract: Given a smooth, oriented, simply-connected $4$-manifold $M$, the homological Nielsen realization problem asks: when does a finite group of isometries $G\leq O(H_2(M;\mathbb{Z}))$ preserving the intersection form lift isomorphically to a finite group of orientation-preserving diffeomorphisms? Even though every isometry of $H_2(M_n;\mathbb{Z})$ is induced by some orientation-preserving diffeomorphism, not necessarily of finite order, we show that Nielsen realization is sparse: as $n\to\infty$, a random subgroup of $O(H_2(M_n;\mathbb{Z}))$ is asymptotically almost never realizable in $\mathrm{Diff}^+(M_n)$; the same is true for random odd order elements of $O(H_2(M_n;\mathbb{Z}))$. We present both positive realization results in certain cases and a range of obstructions to realization in other cases. The proofs combine equivariant connected-sum constructions, fixed-point theory for group actions on 4-manifolds, finite group actions on surfaces, analytic combinatorics, and previous work of Hambleton--Tanase.

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种利用零模霍奇拉普拉斯算子计算曲率和和乐作为动态拓扑数据变化检测描述符的框架。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统持久性同调只能描述拓扑特征的出生和死亡，但无法捕捉这些特征随时间如何重组和变化。

Method: 利用组合霍奇拉普拉斯的零模表示同调特征，在公共链空间中跟踪特征空间，计算曲率（描述局部重组）和和乐（描述累积记忆）。

Abstract: We propose a practical computational framework for detecting structural changes in parameter-dependent topological data. In many applications, such as time-series data analysis, anomaly detection, and monitoring of systems under changing control parameters, persistence diagrams describe the birth and death of topological features at each parameter value, but they do not fully capture how these features are reorganized over time. To address this limitation, we represent homological features by zero modes of the ordinary combinatorial Hodge Laplacian and track the corresponding feature spaces in a common ambient chain space. This allows us to compute curvature and holonomy as descriptors of local reorganization and accumulated memory in evolving topological structures. Curvature highlights parameter regions where homological features mix or change rapidly, while holonomy summarizes the net effect of such changes after a closed cycle. We also establish stability estimates showing that these descriptors are robust under perturbations of the Hodge Laplacian on regular regions. Numerical experiments on controlled time-dependent point-cloud data show that the proposed method detects tracking instability, distinguishes systems with nearly identical persistence diagrams, and captures cycle-level memory invisible to pointwise feature matching. These results suggest that zero-mode transport geometry can serve as a useful computational tool for analyzing dynamic topological data.

### [9] [Localization from Pseudoranges: Quadrics and Duality](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28436)
*Mireille Boutin,Gregor Kemper*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文完整描述了全球定位问题（尤其是欠定情况）的解空间结构，发现解构成一个二次曲面，且卫星位置也位于另一个二次曲面上，两者呈现对偶关系：位于垂直仿射空间但共享对称轴，一方的顶点是另一方的焦点。该结论适用于更广泛的伪距多边定位问题，并可通过两个实际案例（清洁机器人和海上筏定位）展示其应用价值。


<details>

Method: 通过数学推导对全球定位问题的解空间进行完整的几何描述，分析欠定情况下的二次曲面解结构，并证明解空间与卫星位置所在二次曲面之间的对偶性（垂直仿射空间、共享对称轴、顶点与焦点互换）。

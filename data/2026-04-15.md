### [1] [Bridge position of 3-manifolds embedded in the 5-sphere](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12182)
*Román Aranda,Sarah Blackwell,Geunyoung Kim,Patrick Naylor,Puttipong Pongtanapaisan*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文介绍了5维球面中嵌入3维流形的桥分解，推广了经典桥位置和桥三分解概念，证明了所有嵌入3维流形都允许这种分解，且可由四个平凡缠结图编码。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将经典结理论中的桥位置概念和4维球面中曲面的桥三分解推广到更高维度，为研究5维球面中嵌入的3维流形提供新的分解工具。

Method: 使用Aribi等人引入的5维流形多截面作为主要技术工具，构建桥分解理论，证明所有嵌入3维流形都允许这种分解，并通过具体例子验证理论。

Result: 证明了每个嵌入3维流形都允许桥分解，任何这样的嵌入都可以由四个平凡缠结图编码，并给出了包括S^2-纺锤结和带状3-结在内的具体例子。

Conclusion: 桥分解为研究5维球面中嵌入的3维流形提供了统一框架，将高维结理论与低维经典理论连接起来，扩展了结理论的研究工具。

Abstract: We introduce and study bridge decompositions for 3-manifolds embedded in the 5-sphere. These generalize both the classical notion of bridge position for knots in the 3-sphere and the bridge trisections of surfaces in the 4-sphere due to Meier and Zupan. Our main technical tool is the multisections of 5-manifolds introduced by Aribi, Courte, Golla, and Moussard. We prove that every embedded 3-manifold admits such a decomposition; in particular, any such embedding is encoded by four trivial tangle diagrams. We also present a range of explicit examples, including $S^2$-spun knots and ribbon 3-knots.

Result: 证明了某些保持非稀疏本质子曲面或单边界环面的同胚在Homeo_0(S_g)中具有正稳定换位子长度。同时给出了无需有限条件的投影复形版本。

Abstract: Drawing inspiration from [BBF15], we construct a family of unbounded quasi-trees for a connected closed oriented surface \(S_g\) of genus \(g\geq 2\), upon which the group \(\Homeo_0(S_g)\) acts coboundedly by isometries. As an application, we show that some surface homeomorphisms preserving a non-sporadic essential subsurface or an essential subsurface homeomorphic to a once-bordered torus can have positive stable commutator length in \(\Homeo_0(S_g)\). Moreover, we provide a version of projection complex that does not require the finiteness conditions.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Existing results of this type often depend on geometric methods. Several applications are given.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究球面三角剖分的4着色问题，特别是具有度序列6,...,6,4,4,4,4,4,4的特殊三角剖分，探索其组合结构和几何意义。

Method: 将4着色解按组合类型分类，证明每个泛型类型τ由4维有理多面体凸锥内的整数格点参数化，并引入积分二次型Qτ来描述三角剖分的性质。

Abstract: We consider solutions to the

### [6] [Computational Validation of the Oloid as a Local Optimum in the Developable Roller Family](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12238)
*Vincent Wesley Couey*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 提出接触分布评分(CDS)和应力分布评分(SDS)作为评估凸体滚动时表面接触均匀性的新指标，发现oloid形状在均匀性上显著优于传统圆柱体。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 许多工程失效（如热热点集中、赫兹接触疲劳局部化、边界层损失、混合死区）本质上是几何失效模式：改变材料只能延迟失效，改变几何形状才能消除失效。目前缺乏评估凸体滚动时表面接触分布均匀性的正式度量指标。

Method: 引入接触分布评分(CDS)和应力分布评分(SDS)作为新指标，采用三层oracle架构：近似、刚体和赫兹接触压力oracle。对45个可展开滚子族成员进行参数搜索，分析疲劳(FDS)、热(TDS)和磨损(WDS)评分。

Result: oloid形状在CDS=8.2e-7和SDS=8.1e-7上表现最佳，圆柱体基线在这两个指标上差58倍。SDS/CDS比为0.98表明均匀接触时间直接产生均匀接触应力。疲劳指标因Basquin S-N放大效应而发散到2.42e-6，但仍显示oloid优势。

This work establishes the formal vocabulary and computational infrastructure for substrate geometry the study of geometric forms classified by their operational invariants.

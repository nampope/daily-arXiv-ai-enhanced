### [2] [Discrete p-Density and Compression Radii of Lattice Knots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29160)
*Makoto Ozawa*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文为格点纽结建立了离散p-密度与压缩半径分析框架，通过格点长度与弦长散布泛函定义无量纲密度，并利用原始格点厚度定义压缩半径。以figure-eight结为例，在BFACF移动连接路径上计算发现密度与压缩半径并非单调变化，表明密度最小化、压缩最小化与类绳长最小化是三个不同的有限状态优化问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为格点纽结提供可计算的分析框架，作为连续尺度无关几何不变量的离散对应物，区分密度、压缩与类绳长等量在长度过滤和有限移动图探索中的行为差异。

Method: 定义p-密度（格点长度/弦长散布泛函）和原始压缩半径（基于格点厚度）；在长度过滤的格点代表集和种子生成的BFACF移动搜索空间上研究这些轮廓；通过显式BFACF合并证书路径计算原始p-密度与压缩半径值。

Result: 在30-edge figure-eight结到其镜像的BFACF路径（21状态、20步、经过32-edge连接状态）上：连接状态密度更大、D2压缩半径略大；中间30-edge状态压缩半径更小但密度更大。密度、压缩半径均非单调变化。

Conclusion: 密度最小化、压缩最小化与类绳长最小化是三个不同的有限状态优化问题，不可相互替代；该框架可有效区分这些几何量在纽结状态空间中的行为。

It separates density, compression, and ropelength-like quantities, and records how these quantities behave under length filtration and finite move-graph exploration. We emphasize throughout the distinction between exhaustive lattice-knot invariants and seed-generated computational profiles.

Along this path, density and compression-radius values are not monotone: the connecting state has larger raw density and slightly larger D_2-compression radius than the seeds, while an intermediate 30-edge state has smaller compression radius but larger density. This illustrates that density minimization, compression minimization, and ropelength-like minimization define distinct finite-state optimization problems.

### [5] [Existence of Minimal Homotopies for Immersed Planar Curves](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29474)
*Lia Buchbinder,Yunjia Kou,Bala Krishnamoorthy,Kevin R. Vixie*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文发展了一种变分存在性理论，用于寻找平面中浸入曲线之间的最小面积同伦。通过将具有自交点的平面曲线提升到高余维空间中使其嵌入，再应用Douglas对Plateau问题的解，作者证明了对于C^1和Lipschitz闭曲线，存在面积最小的零同伦映射，从而建立了Plateau理论与最小同伦面积之间的联系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 经典的Plateau问题解决的是单条Jordan曲线的最小曲面存在性，但曲线之间同伦的最小面积存在性问题更为微妙，现有的组合与代数方法依赖于曲线的特殊类别和自交点的局部结构（通常假设横截交叉），缺乏几何变分视角下的统一处理。

Method: 将平面中具有自交点的浸入曲线提升到高余维空间（使其变为嵌入），然后应用Douglas的Plateau问题解得到面积最小化圆盘；对于C^1闭曲线证明Douglas极小化序列的一致收敛性，对于Lipschitz闭曲线则通过逼近和Sobolev紧性论证扩展结果。

Result: 对于C^1闭曲线，证明了Douglas极小化序列一致收敛，且极限映射在所有C^1映射中面积最小；对于Lipschitz闭曲线，通过逼近方法也得到了类似的存在性结果。最终的极小化圆盘位于原平面中，直接产生一个面积最小的零同伦。

Conclusion: 该工作建立了一种几何变分的存在性理论，不依赖于自交点的局部结构，将Plateau理论的思想推广到了最小同伦面积问题，为平面曲线的最小面积同伦提供了统一的变分框架。

Abstract: We study the existence of area-minimizing homotopies between homotopic curves in the plane. While the classical Plateau problem establishes the existence of least-area surfaces spanning a single Jordan curve, the corresponding existence theory for homotopies between curves is more subtle and is not directly covered by the same framework. Existing results in the plane are mainly based on combinatorial and algebraic methods, such as decomposing curves into self-overlapping subcurves. These methods are highly effective in the planar setting, but they are often tied to special classes of curves and rely strongly on the local structure of the self-intersections, frequently assuming transverse crossings. In contrast, our approach is geometric and variational, and does not depend on the local structure of the self-intersections.
  In this paper, we develop a variational existence theory for minimum-area homotopies of immersed planar curves. Our approach adapts classical minimal surface methods by lifting an immersed planar curve with self-intersections into higher co-dimension, where it becomes embedded. For such a lifted curve, we apply Douglas's solution of the Plateau problem to obtain an area-minimizing disk. For closed curves of class $C^1$, we prove uniform convergence of the Douglas minimizers and show that the limiting map minimizes area among all $C^1$ spanning maps of the original planar curve. We then extend the construction to closed Lipschitz curves using approximation and Sobolev compactness arguments. Since the limiting minimizing disk lies in the original plane, it directly produces a null homotopy whose swept area is minimal among all admissible homotopies of the original curve. In this way, the construction connects Plateau theory with minimal homotopy area minimization.

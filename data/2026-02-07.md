Abstract: We complete the description of surgery obstructions up to homotopy equivalence for closed oriented manifolds with finite fundamental group. ### [2] [Genera of two-component alternating links](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05673)
*Noboru Ito,Nodoka Kawajiri*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 将交替纽结的非定向亏格等式结果推广到二分量交替链环，建立了统一量完全由拼接序列确定的公式


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Ito-Takimura和Kindred关于交替纽结非定向亏格的等式结果扩展到二分量交替链环，研究这类链环的定向和非定向亏格的统一描述

Method: 通过拼接序列和拼接解结数来刻画交替链环，建立统一量（捕获定向和非定向亏格）与拼接序列之间的精确关系，包含显式修正项

Result: 证明对于二分量交替链环，统一量完全由实现拼接解结数的拼接序列决定，只需考虑一个显式的修正项

Conclusion: 成功将交替纽结的亏格等式推广到二分量交替链环，建立了统一量与拼接序列之间的精确对应关系，为这类链环的亏格研究提供了新工具

Abstract: We extend the equality-type results of Ito--Takimura and Kindred for the non-orientable genera of alternating knots to the setting of two-component alternating links. We show that, for such links, a unified quantity capturing both orientable and non-orientable genera is completely determined by the splice sequence realizing the splice-unknotting number up to an explicit correction term.

### [3] [Delta-Unknotting Number for Pretzel Knots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05682)
*Kazumichi Nakamura*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文计算了正pretzel结的Δ-解结数，并确定了P(-1, p₂, ..., pₙ)型pretzel结的Δ-解结数，其中pᵢ为正奇数，n为奇数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Δ-解结数定义为将结变形为平凡结所需的最小Δ-移动次数。已知对于正pretzel结，Δ-解结数与它们的Conway多项式第二系数一致。本文旨在计算正pretzel结的Δ-解结数，并扩展到特定类型的pretzel结。

Method: 通过分析正pretzel结的结构特性，利用Δ-移动与Conway多项式系数之间的关系，计算Δ-解结数。对于P(-1, p₂, ..., pₙ)型pretzel结，利用其奇偶性和参数特性进行推导。

Result: 成功计算了正pretzel结的Δ-解结数，并确定了P(-1, p₂, ..., pₙ)型pretzel结的Δ-解结数，其中pᵢ为正奇数，n为奇数。

Conclusion: 本文扩展了对pretzel结Δ-解结数的理解，为正pretzel结和特定类型的pretzel结提供了精确的Δ-解结数计算公式，验证了Δ-解结数与Conway多项式系数之间的关系。

Abstract: The $Δ$-unknotting number for a knot is defined as the minimum number of $Δ$-moves needed to deform the knot into the trivial knot. It is known that, for positive pretzel knots, the $Δ$-unknotting number coincides with the second coefficient of their Conway polynomial. In this paper, we compute the $Δ$-unknotting number for positive pretzel knots. Furthermore, we determine the $Δ$-unknotting number for pretzel knots of type $P(-1, p_2, ..., p_n)$, where $p_i$ is a positive odd integer for $2 \leq i \leq n$ and $n$ is odd.

</details>

### [4] [Generalized Perfect Matrices](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05841)
*Alexander Oertel,Achill Schürmann*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 将Voronoi的完美二次型理论推广到闭凸全维锥K上的广义余正矩阵，引入K-余正最小值和完美K-余正矩阵概念，研究锥的IR性质及其应用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将经典的Voronoi完美二次型理论推广到更一般的锥上，特别是研究广义余正矩阵在闭凸全维锥K上的性质，为完全正锥的逼近和成员资格验证提供理论工具。

Method: 引入K-余正最小值和完美K-余正矩阵概念，定义锥的IR性质，证明有理生成锥具有IR性质，构造反例展示无IR性质锥的差异，建立与数论的联系，应用于完全正锥的多面体逼近。

Result: 建立了广义余正矩阵的完美理论框架，证明了有理生成锥具有IR性质，构造了无IR性质的简单锥反例，建立了与Diophantine逼近和Pell方程的数论联系，给出了完全正锥的内外多面体逼近方法。

Conclusion: 成功将Voronoi理论推广到广义余正矩阵，IR性质是关键条件，有理生成锥满足该性质，提供了完全正锥的逼近和成员资格验证的实用方法，建立了优化与数论的有趣联系。

Abstract: We generalize Voronoi's theory of perfect quadratic forms to generalized copositive matrices over a closed convex and full-dimensional cone K. We introduce a notion of a K-copositive minimum and of perfect K-copositive matrices. We consider a key feature of a given cone, which we call Interior Ryshkov (IR) property. Under this property the classical theory and its applications generalize nicely and we prove that rationally generated cones possess this IR property. For contrast, we give a detailed example of a simple cone without the IR property, showing various differences to the classical case. Moreover, this example yields connections to questions of number theory, in particular to Diophantine approximation and the Pell Equation. Finally, as an application, we give inner and outer polyhedral approximations for the generalized completely positive cone and a method to find rational certificates for (non-)membership in this cone.

</details>

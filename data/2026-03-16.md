### [1] [The Witt filtration of Lubin-Tate deformation rings](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12490)
*Charles Rezk*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文探讨了经典p-典型Witt向量𝕎ₚ的推广𝕎_E，它源于形式群变形的同源或Morava E-理论的幂运算理论，在高度≥2时更丰富，并自然包含𝕎ₚ。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究经典p-典型Witt向量𝕎ₚ的推广𝕎_E，该推广在形式群高度≥2时展现出更丰富的结构，并与Morava E-理论的幂运算理论密切相关。

Method: 通过形式群变形的同源几何方法或Morava E-理论的幂运算拓扑方法，构造𝕎_E并研究其性质，特别是证明π₀E ≈ 𝕎_E(π₀E/𝔪)的同构关系。

Result: 证明了𝕎ₚ自然嵌入𝕎_E；建立了π₀E与𝕎_E(π₀E/𝔪)的同构关系；描述了π₀E上的Witt滤过如何插值于p-进滤过和几何滤过之间；给出了Morava E-理论余自由性的新证明。

Conclusion: 𝕎_E为经典Witt向量的重要推广，在高度≥2时具有更丰富的结构，其与Morava E-理论的联系揭示了深刻的代数拓扑性质，并为相关理论提供了新的视角和证明方法。

Abstract: This note is a meditation on a generalization $\mathbb{W}_E$ of the classical p-typical Witt vectors $\mathbb{W}_p$, which arises (geometrically) from isogenies of deformations of formal groups, or (topologically) from the theory of power operations on Morava $E$-theory. For formal groups of height $1$ we have $\mathbb{W}_E=\mathbb{W}_p$, but the $\mathbb{W}_E$ are richer when height is $\geq 2$. We show that $\mathbb{W}_p$ splits naturally from $\mathbb{W}_E$.
  A key property of $\mathbb{W}_E$ is the isomorphism $π_0E\approx \mathbb{W}_E(π_0E/\mathfrak{m})$, the ``cofreeness of the Morava $E$-theory'' proved by Burklund, Schlank, and Yuan. This isomorphism determines a natural ``Witt filtration'' on $π_0 E$. We describe how this Witt filtration interpolates between the $p$-adic filtration and a geometric filtration on $π_0E/(p)$. We use this to give a new proof of cofreeness.

### [2] [Cofreeness of the Lubin-Tate deformation ring](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12492)
*Charles Rezk*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文证明了Lubin-Tate变形环的余自由性，通过将Matt Ando和Yifei Zhu关于$\mathsf{H}_\infty$-定向的结果推广到Morava $E$理论的幂运算框架中。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Lubin-Tate变形环的余自由性是一个重要的代数拓扑问题。先前Matt Ando和Yifei Zhu在$\mathsf{H}_\infty$-定向方面取得了一些结果，但需要将这些结果扩展到更一般的Morava $E$理论的幂运算框架中。

Method: 通过推广Matt Ando和Yifei Zhu关于$\mathsf{H}_\infty$-定向的结果，将其应用于Morava $E$理论的幂运算。这种方法将代数拓扑中的定向理论与形式群理论联系起来。

Result: 成功证明了Lubin-Tate变形环的余自由性，为代数拓扑和形式群理论中的相关问题提供了新的工具和视角。

Conclusion: 通过将$\mathsf{H}_\infty$-定向的结果推广到Morava $E$理论的幂运算框架，本文建立了Lubin-Tate变形环余自由性的证明，扩展了代数拓扑与形式群理论的联系。

Abstract: We give a proof of the cofreeness of the Lubin-Tate deformation ring, by generalizing earlier results by Matt Ando and Yifei Zhu about $\mathsf{H}_\infty$-orientations to the context of power operations for Morava $E$-theory.

### [3] [Real Hochschild homology as an equivariant Loday construction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12803)
*Ayelet Lindenstrauss,Birgit Richter,Foling Zou*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文研究等变Loday构造在不同等变群作用下的简化情况，特别应用于实Hochschild同调的解释


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 等变Loday构造通常需要完整的G-Tambara函子，但实际应用中某些情况可以简化为子群H的Tambara函子，这能简化计算并扩展应用范围

Method: 分析单纯G-集的各向同性子群，确定何时可以使用子群H的Tambara函子代替完整的G-Tambara函子，并将此应用于离散E_σ-环的实Hochschild同调

Result: 建立了实Hochschild同调与等变Loday构造的联系，其中使用2m边形和二面体群D_{2m}的几何作用，以及置换多面体1-骨架上的对称群作用

Conclusion: 通过分析各向同性子群，可以在特定情况下简化等变Loday构造的计算，这为实Hochschild同调等理论提供了新的几何解释

Abstract: Equivariant Loday constructions are a means for providing geometric interpretations of equivariant homology theories. They are usually constructed for a simplicial $G$-set and a $G$-Tambara functor. We study situations where -- depending on the isotropy subgroups occurring in the simplicial $G$-set -- one can work with $H$-Tambara functors for a suitable subgroup $H$ of $G$. We apply this to give an interpretation of Real Hochschild homology of discrete $E_σ$-rings as equivariant Loday constructions where we consider $2m$-gons with a geometrically defined action of the dihedral groups $D_{2m}$ for all $m \geq 1$. The action of symmetric groups on $1$-skeleta of permutohedra also gives examples with isotropy groups $C_2$.

</details>

### [4] [Triangle covering problems and the Viterbo inequality in the plane](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12495)
*Alexey Balitskiy,Ivan Mitrofanov,Alexander Polyanskii*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 该论文研究了平面三角形覆盖问题，将其与凸形状的"等弹道"不等式、辛几何中的Viterbo猜想联系起来，并给出了Viterbo猜想在拉格朗日积K×Q（其中Q为四边形，K为任意凸形状）情况下的新证明。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究平面三角形覆盖问题，该问题等价于凸形状中的"等弹道"不等式，也是辛几何中Viterbo猜想的特例。作者希望通过这个覆盖问题的视角，为Viterbo猜想提供新的见解和证明。

Method: 采用初等方法综述这些主题，利用覆盖问题的光学视角，建立了Viterbo猜想的几个新特例，为Haim-Kislev和Ostrover的反例提供了简单解释，并陈述了一些开放问题。

Result: 主要新结果是证明了Viterbo猜想对于拉格朗日积K×Q的情况，其中Q⊂ℝ²是任意四边形，K⊂ℝ²是任意凸形状。此外还提供了Viterbo猜想其他特例的证明。

Conclusion: 通过三角形覆盖问题的视角，成功连接了不同数学领域的问题，为Viterbo猜想提供了新的特例证明，并揭示了该猜想与几何覆盖问题之间的深刻联系。

Abstract: We review a certain problem on covering triangles in the plane. Equivalently, it can be viewed as a family of 'isobilliard' inequalities in convex shapes, and as a special case of Viterbo's conjecture in symplectic geometry. We give an elementary overview of these topics and, using the optics of the covering problem, we establish several new special cases of Viterbo's conjecture, provide a simple explanation of the counterexample of Haim-Kislev and Ostrover, and state a few open questions. The main novel result is a proof of Viterbo's conjecture for lagrangian products $K \times Q$, where $Q \subset \mathbb{R}^2$ is any quadrilateral and $K \subset \mathbb{R}^2$ is any convex shape.

</details>

### [1] [A global shadow lemma for relatively Morse groups in higher rank](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19779)
*Dongryul M. Kim,Hee Oh*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文针对高秩半单李群中相对Morse子群，证明了Patterson-Sullivan测度的全局阴影引理，该估计对Gromov模型中任意中心点（包括尖点深处）一致成立，推广了Stratmann-Velani在几何有限实双曲群上的结果，并应用于测度局部一致估计及与Hausdorff测度的比较。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将经典实双曲几何中Patterson-Sullivan测度的阴影引理推广到高秩半单李群的相对Morse子群设定下，处理尖点区域深处的一致性估计问题。

Method: 通过建立全局阴影引理（global shadow lemma），对Gromov模型中任意中心点（包括尖点深处）给出统一的估计，利用相对Morse子群的几何性质及边界结构分析。

Result: 获得了Patterson-Sullivan测度的全局一致局部估计，并给出了该测度与相关视觉拟度量定义的Hausdorff测度成比例（up to scale）的充分条件。

Conclusion: 本文在高秩背景下建立了Patterson-Sullivan测度的全局阴影引理，其估计在尖点区域仍一致成立，并由此得到测度局部一致估计及与Hausdorff测度的比较结果。

Abstract: Patterson-Sullivan measures encode the distribution of orbits of discrete group actions near the boundary. In this paper, we prove a global shadow lemma for Patterson-Sullivan measures associated to relatively Morse subgroups of higher-rank semisimple Lie groups. The estimate is uniform for shadows centered at arbitrary points in a Gromov model, including points deep in the cuspidal part. This extends the global shadow lemma of Stratmann-Velani for geometrically finite real hyperbolic groups. As applications, we obtain uniform local estimates for Patterson-Sullivan measures, and we give sufficient conditions under which these measures agree, up to scale, with the Hausdorff measure defined by the associated visual quasi-metric.

### [2] [The Kernel of the $\hat A$-Genus in Rational Spin Bordism is Generated by Ricci-Positive Manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19505)
*Gerald Höhn,Philipp Höhn*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文证明：在有理Spin配边环中，具有正Ricci曲率的流形所代表的配边类恰好张成$\hat A$-genus的核。通过构造光滑完全交$Y_{m,\ell}$并利用多项式插值证明满秩，回答了椭圆亏格背景下关于正Ricci曲率有理配边障碍的开放问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在复椭圆亏格的研究背景下，人们提出正Ricci曲率的有理配边障碍问题：哪些有理Spin配边类可由具有正Ricci曲率的流形代表？此前已知$\hat A$-genus是障碍（因为正Ricci曲率流形$\hat A$-genus为零），但反之是否成立（即$\hat A$-genus为零的类是否总能由正Ricci曲率流形实现）是待解决的核心问题。

Method: 1. 构造一族光滑完全交流形$Y_{m,\ell}\subset \mathbb{CP}^{2m+\ell}$（$\ell$为奇数），它们是Spin且Fano的，故具有正Ricci曲率。2. 考虑$\hat A$-genus的一阶加厚，在商空间$J/J^2$的$4m$维部分上诱导出$m-1$个线性泛函。3. 计算$[Y_{m,\ell}]$在这些泛函下的取值，发现由严格递增次数的多项式$P_{m,q}(\ell)$控制。4. 利用多项式插值论证说明这些取值构成的矩阵满秩，从而$Y_{m,\ell}$的配边类线性张成整个$J$。

Result: 证明了有理Spin配边环中，正Ricci曲率流形所张成的$\mathbb{Q}$-子空间恰好等于$\hat A$-genus的核$J$，即每个度数的配边类均成立。从而在可微有理Spin范畴下完全刻画了正Ricci曲率的有理配边障碍。

Conclusion: 该结果完整回答了复椭圆亏格背景下提出的正Ricci曲率有理配边障碍问题，表明$\hat A$-genus是正Ricci曲率在有理Spin配边中唯一的线性障碍。

Abstract: We prove that, in every degree, the rational Spin bordism classes represented by manifolds admitting metrics with positive Ricci curvature span exactly the kernel of the $\hat A$-genus. More precisely, for \[ R=Ω_*^{Spin}\otimes\mathbb{Q},\qquad J=\ker(\hat A:R\longrightarrow\mathbb{Q}[u]),\] the $\mathbb{Q}$-span of bordism classes of Ricci-positive Spin manifolds equals $J$ in each degree. This answers, in the differentiable rational Spin category, a question about rational bordism obstructions to positive Ricci curvature which was raised in the context of complex elliptic genera.
  The proof uses smooth complete intersections of an odd number $\ell$ of quadrics \[ Y_{m,\ell}\subset \mathbb{CP}^{2m+\ell}, \qquad \ell=1,\, 3,\, \ldots,\, 2m-1. \] These manifolds have real dimension $4m$, are Spin and Fano, and therefore admit metrics with positive Ricci curvature. A first-order thickening of the $\hat A$-genus induces $m-1$ linear functionals on $(J/J^2)_{4m}$. Their values on the classes $[Y_{m,\ell}]$ are governed by polynomials $P_{m,q}(\ell)$ of strictly increasing degrees $q+1=1$, $2$, $\ldots$, $m-1$. This gives full rank by a polynomial-interpolation argument.

</details>


### [3] [$K$-Theoretic Obstructions to Linearizing QCA Representations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19657)
*Mattie Ji,Bowen Yang*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文研究了量子细胞自动机（QCA）表示的可线性化问题，通过代数K-理论谱构建了阻碍理论，并在复数域上完整计算了点、线、平面上的QCA空间同伦类型。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 投影表示在物理和表示论中自然出现，判断其能否线性化是一个基本问题。本文旨在研究具有局域性约束的QCA表示是否存在类似的线性化问题。

Method: 基于作者先前工作中构建的QCA代数K-理论谱，发展了一套阻碍理论；利用QCA空间的同伦型提取通用阻碍类；在复代数与酉情形下，具体计算了点、线、平面上的QCA空间同伦类型。

Result: 成功构建了QCA表示线性化的阻碍理论，阻碍由QCA空间的同伦型控制；在复代数与酉情形下，完整计算了点、线和平面上的QCA空间同伦类型。

Conclusion: 本文为QCA表示的线性化问题提供了系统的阻碍理论框架，并在特定情形下得到了具体的同伦分类结果。

Abstract: Projective representations arise naturally in physics and representation theory, and determining whether they can be linearized has been a fundamental problem. In this work, we study the analogous problem for quantum cellular automata (QCA) representations, which incorporate locality constraints imposed by a metric space $X$. Over an arbitrary field $\mathbb{F}$, we develop an obstruction theory for the linearization of QCA representations, using the algebraic $K$-theory spectrum of QCA constructed in previous work of the authors. The resulting obstructions are governed by the homotopy type of the QCA spaces, from which we extract universal obstruction classes to linearization. In the complex algebraic and unitary case, we also fully compute the homotopy types of the QCA spaces over a point, a line, and a plane.

### [6] [Polynomial valuations on plane polygons](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19986)
*Askold Khovanskii,Valentina Kiritchenko,Vladlen Timorin*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文从朴素统一的视角回顾了平移不变简单 valuations 的经典理论，从多边形上所有简单 valuations 的描述出发，刻画了平移不变性的影响。作为副产品，得到了多项式简单 valuations 的描述，这是对平移不变理论的直接推广，且似乎是新的结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 剪裁同余问题（特别是涉及平移的）推动了平移不变简单 valuations 的研究，但现有理论缺乏统一清晰的视角。

Method: 采用朴素一致的观点，从多边形上所有简单 valuations 的描述出发，分析平移不变性带来的约束，进而推广到多项式简单 valuations。

Result: 得到了多项式简单 valuations 的描述，作为平移不变理论的直接推广，且该结果似乎是新的。

Conclusion: 通过统一视角的分析，不仅回顾了经典理论，还获得了新的结果（多项式简单 valuations 的描述），为后续研究提供了基础。

Abstract: Scissors congruence problems involving translations have prompted the study of translation invariant simple valuations. We review this classical theory from a naive and consistent viewpoint: starting from a description of all simple valuations on polygons, we characterize the effect of translation invariance. A description of all polynomial simple valuations is obtained as a bi-product of the adopted approach and as a direct generalization of the translation invariant theory; it appears to be new.

Result: 证明了当n>9时，A^n上点的Hilbert概形的Gorenstein轨迹是非约化的。构造了具体的非约化点例子。作为推论，得到了cactus概形的非约化性结果。在嵌套Hilbert概形上发现了分形结构。

Conclusion: Hilbert概形的Gorenstein轨迹在维数较高时表现出非约化性，这通过构造具体例子得到证实。推广的Białynicki-Birula分解为形变理论提供了新的研究工具，揭示了嵌套Hilbert概形上的分形结构。

Abstract: We prove that the Gorenstein locus of the Hilbert scheme of points on $\mathbb A^n$ is non-reduced for $n>9$; we construct examples of non-reduced points that come from apolar algebras of the sum of general cubics. As a corollary, we get a non-reducedness result for the cactus scheme. We generalise the Białynicki-Birula decomposition to abstract deformation functors, providing a new method of studying deformation theory. Our construction gives us fractal structures on the nested Hilbert scheme.

### [1] [Embeddings of mapping tori for end-periodic graph maps](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14976)
*Adam R. Smith*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究了无限图的端周期同伦等价，证明其映射环面可与有限2-复形建立保持流线的同伦等价，并在附加条件下可嵌入有限秩图的映射环面。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将无限型曲面的端周期自同构概念推广到无限图的维度一情形，研究端周期同伦等价的映射环面的拓扑性质。

Method: 通过端周期同伦等价构造映射环面，证明其与有限2-复形的流线保持同伦等价，并在特定条件下建立到有限秩图映射环面的嵌入。

Result: 证明了无限图的端周期同伦等价的映射环面可紧化并与有限2-复形同伦等价，且存在π₁-单射、保持流的嵌入到有限秩图的映射环面。

Conclusion: 每个由端周期同伦等价产生的映射类都包含一个代表元，其映射环面可实现上述嵌入，为无限图的端周期映射提供了紧化描述。

Abstract: End-periodic homotopy equivalences of infinite, locally finite graphs serve as dimension-one analogs of the end-periodic automorphisms traditionally defined on infinite-type surfaces. We demonstrate that if $Γ$ is an infinite graph with finitely many ends, and $g \colon Γ\to Γ$ is end-periodic, then its mapping torus $Z_g$ admits a flowline-preserving homotopy equivalence with a finite 2-complex. With additional hypotheses on $g$, this compactified mapping torus subsequently embeds in the mapping torus of a homotopy equivalence on a finite-rank graph via a $π_1$-injective, flow-preserving map. We prove that every mapping class of $Γ$ arising from an end-periodic homotopy equivalence contains a representative whose mapping torus realizes such an embedding.

  <summary>Details</summary>

Result: 证明了满足特定图可约性条件的注入标记定向树具有局部可指示基本群。

Conclusion: 通过扩展的图可约性特征化方法，为Whitehead球面化猜想相关问题的局部可指示性提供了新的证明途径。

Abstract: If a complex $X$ is a subcomplex of a diagrammatically reducible 2-complex $Y$ that has locally indicable fundamental group, then $X$ has locally indicable fundamental group. This is a consequence of the Corson-Trace characterization of diagrammatic reducibility. In this paper we use a Corson-Trace like characterization of diagrammatic reducibility away from a subcomplex to obtain a considerable stronger result. We apply this to the question of local indicability in the context of Whitehead's asphericity conjecture. We show that an injective labeled oriented tree (LOT) that is diagrammatically reducible of degree 2, and all its quotients are as well, is locally indicable.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

### [5] [Splitting vector bundles over real algebraic varieties](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.15616)
*Aravind Asok,Jean Fasel,Samuel Lerbet*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文研究了在光滑仿射实簇上向量丛分裂出自由秩1直和项的问题，分析了0余秩和1余秩两种情况，探讨了Murthy分裂定理在实数域上的类比。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 寻找Murthy分裂定理在实数域上的类比，研究在代数闭域情形下已知的拓扑障碍在实数域上是否仍然适用，以及实数情形的复杂性。

Method: 使用动机技术（motivic techniques）证明Bhatwadekar-Sridharan和Bhatwadekar-Das-Mandal结果的温和扩展，并分析各种可能的实数类比猜想。

Result: 在0余秩情况下，证明了分裂障碍确实是纯拓扑的；在1余秩情况下，发现多个自然的实数类比猜想都不成立，表明实数情形比代数闭域情形复杂得多。

Conclusion: 实数域上的向量丛分裂问题比代数闭域情形更加复杂，特别是在1余秩情况下，不能简单地将代数闭域的结果直接推广到实数域。

Abstract: Suppose $X$ is a smooth affine real variety and $\mathscr{E}$ is a vector bundle over $X$. We analyze the problem of splitting off a free rank one summand from $\mathscr{E}$ in corank $0$ and $1$. The problem in corank $0$ can be viewed as the search for a real analog of Murthy's celebrating splitting theorem in the algebraically closed case: to wit, beyond the vanishing of the top Chern class in Chow theory, are the obstructions to splitting ``purely topological''? In a sense, the answer in this case is yes, and we give a proof, using motivic techniques, of a mild extension of the results of Bhatwadekar-Sridharan and Bhatwadekar-Das-Mandal.
  In corank $1$, in the algebraically closed situation, Murthy's splitting conjecture (now a theorem in characteristic $0$) predicts that the vanishing of the top Chern class in Chow theory is the only obstruction to splitting off a free rank $1$ summand, and we can search for a suitable ``real'' analog of this assertion. We observe that several natural guesses for a ``real'' analog of Murthy's splitting conjecture cannot be true, i.e., that the situation over the real numbers is rather complicated.

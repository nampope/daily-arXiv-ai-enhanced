### [2] [A note on the Burnside problem for homeomorphism groups of manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15627)
*Donggyun Seo*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 研究了紧致连通流形同胚群的Burnside问题，证明了对于曲面，当曲面不是球面、环面、射影平面或Klein瓶时，同胚群的单位元分支是无挠的；对于圆，证明了每个有限生成的周期子群都是有限循环群。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究紧致连通流形同胚群的Burnside问题，即探讨这些群中是否存在无限有限生成的周期子群。特别关注曲面和圆的同胚群结构。

Method: 对于曲面，首先证明同胚群单位元分支的无挠性条件；然后利用映射类群的Tits替代进行扩展论证。对于圆，直接证明有限生成周期子群的有限循环性。

Result: 1. 曲面同胚群单位元分支无挠当且仅当曲面不是球面、环面、射影平面或Klein瓶；2. 对于这些例外曲面外的所有曲面，每个有限生成的周期子群都是有限的；3. 圆的每个有限生成周期子群都是有限循环群。

Conclusion: 该研究解决了曲面和圆同胚群的Burnside问题，将Guelman和Liousse的定理扩展到不可定向曲面，并对带边界流形和双柄体等提出了进一步的研究问题。

Abstract: This note studies the Burnside problem for homeomorphism groups of compact connected manifolds. For surfaces, we prove that the identity component of the homeomorphism group is torsion-free precisely when the surface is not the sphere, torus, projective plane, or Klein bottle. An extension argument based on the Tits alternative for mapping class groups then implies that every finitely generated periodic subgroup of the full homeomorphism group is finite for all surfaces outside this exceptional list, recovering and extending a theorem of Guelman and Liousse to non-orientable surfaces. For the circle, we prove that every finitely generated periodic subgroup of its homeomorphism group is finite and cyclic. We close with remarks on manifolds with boundary and open questions on the Burnside problem for hyperbolic three-manifolds and doubled handlebodies.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 通过分析非分离弱约化对与约化球面的关系，建立充分条件，将复杂的连通性问题简化为更易处理的特殊情况。

Result: 得到了非分离弱约化对被约化球面分离的充分条件，并将约化球面复形的连通性问题简化为：证明任意两个顶点（其代表约化球面与固定非分离压缩圆盘不相交）在复形中连通。

Conclusion: 该结果为解决亏格4 Heegaard曲面的约化球面复形连通性问题提供了关键步骤，将复杂问题转化为更易处理的形式，为进一步研究奠定了基础。

Abstract: For the genus-$4$ Heegaard surface in the $3$-sphere, we present a sufficient condition for a non-separating weak reducing pair to be separated by a reducing sphere for the surface. ### [5] [Bounded cohomology classes from differential forms](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.16289)
*Gian Maria Dall'Ara,Roberto Frigerio,Ervin Hadziosmanovic*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文证明了对于极限集等于无穷远边界的双曲流形，闭有界微分2-形式到有界上同调类的映射是单射，推广了先前闭流形的结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 先前Barge和Ghys（n=2）以及Battista等人（n>2）证明了对于闭双曲流形，闭微分2-形式到有界上同调类H^2_b(M)的映射是单射。本文旨在将此结果推广到更一般的双曲流形，特别是基本群为第一类（极限集等于无穷远边界）的情况，这包括了有限体积流形等重要情形。

Method: 1. 通过测地线单纯形积分，将闭有界微分2-形式映射到有界上同调类H^2_b(M)。2. 证明一个独立有趣的事实：双曲平面上的L^∞函数由其所有理想三角形的积分唯一确定。3. 利用双曲平面上的傅里叶分析来证明上述事实。4. 基于这个关键事实，采用与Barge和Ghys原始方法不同的论证，将单射性结果推广到基本群为第一类的双曲流形。

Result: 成功将闭微分2-形式到有界上同调类单射嵌入的结果，从闭双曲流形推广到基本群为第一类的双曲流形（包括有限体积流形）。同时证明了双曲平面上L^∞函数由其所有理想三角形积分唯一确定这一独立结果。

Conclusion: 本文扩展了双曲流形上有界上同调理论的重要结果，证明了对于基本群为第一类的双曲流形，闭微分2-形式空间可以单射嵌入到有界上同调群H^2_b(M)中。这一推广涵盖了更广泛的几何情形，为双曲几何和有界上同调理论提供了新的工具和视角。

Abstract: Let $M$ be a complete hyperbolic $n$-manifold, $n\geq 2$. Via integration over geodesic simplices, any closed bounded differential 2-form on $M$ defines a bounded cohomology class in $H^2_b(M)$. It was proved by Barge and Ghys (for $n=2$) and by Battista et al. (for $n>2$) that, if $M$ is closed, then this procedure defines an injective embedding of the (infinite-dimensional) space of closed differential $2$-forms on $M$ into $H^2_b(M)$.
  We extend this result to the case when the fundamental group of $M$ is of the first kind, i.e. its limit set is equal to the whole boundary at infinity of hyperbolic space (this holds, for example, when $M$ has finite volume). Our argument is different from Barge and Ghys' original one, and relies on the following fact of independent interest: an $L^\infty$ function on the hyperbolic plane is determined by its integrals over all ideal triangles. We prove this fact by way of Fourier analysis on the hyperbolic plane.

</details>

*Sagnik Biswas*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 研究Stiefel流形V_{n,2}及其与球面乘积的刚性问题和分类问题，通过构造结构集中的显式逆元来分类几乎微分同胚的流形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Stiefel流形及其乘积空间的拓扑和微分结构，特别是刚性问题和分类问题，这些是微分拓扑中的重要课题。

Method: 通过构造特定切向同伦等价的正规不变量，在结构集中寻找显式逆元。使用几乎微分同胚的概念进行分类。

Result: 解决了大多数n值下V_{n,2}自映射的刚性问题；对V_{n,2}×S^k在k=3,5或7≤k≤n-3,k≠2^i-2时的分类；在有利情况下（如V_{12,2}×S^3等）给出了完全分类：所有此类流形几乎微分同胚于V_{n,2}#Σ×S^k，其中Σ是奇异球面。

Conclusion: 该方法能够有效分类Stiefel流形乘积空间的微分结构，为剩余情况提供了可能的解决途径，展示了正规不变量在微分拓扑分类问题中的有效性。

Abstract: We study two problems concerning the Stiefel manifolds $V_{n,2}$ and their products with spheres. Second, we classify smooth closed manifolds tangentially homotopy equivalent to $V_{n,2} \times S^k$ up to almost diffeomorphism, for $k = 3, 5$ or $7 \leq k \leq n-3$, $k \neq 2^i - 2$. Our method is to find explicit inverses in the structure set via normal invariants of specific tangential homotopy equivalences. In favourable cases -- notably $V_{12,2} \times S^3$, $V_{16,2} \times S^3$, $V_{12,2} \times S^5$, $V_{10,2} \times S^5$ -- the classification is complete: every such manifold is almost diffeomorphic to $V_{n,2} \mathbin{\#} Σ\times S^k$ for some exotic sphere $Σ$. In the general case, we identify inverses for a large subgroup of $\operatorname{Im}(η)$ and provide a possible way forward to the remainder.

### [7] [Perturbations of measures and sets having curves in d directions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15887)
*Jakub Takáč*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 该论文证明：若可分度量空间子集S具有d维弱切场，则典型1-Lipschitz映射将S的μ-测度几乎全部分映到至多d维Hausdorff维数集中。特别地，d=0时1-纯不可求长集被典型映射到Hausdorff零维集。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究度量空间中子集的维数性质与典型Lipschitz映射行为之间的关系，特别是探讨弱切场条件如何影响集合在典型映射下的维数保持性质。

Method: 利用Baire范畴理论分析典型1-Lipschitz映射的行为，结合弱切场条件和测度论工具，证明在可分度量空间中相关维数保持定理。

Result: 证明具有d维弱切场的可分子集在典型1-Lipschitz映射下，其测度几乎全部分映到至多d维Hausdorff维数集中。在欧几里得空间和严格凸有限维Banach空间中该结果是最优的。

Conclusion: 弱切场条件为度量空间子集在典型Lipschitz映射下的维数行为提供了充分条件，建立了集合的几何结构与典型映射维数保持性质之间的深刻联系。

Abstract: We show that whenever a separable subset $S$ of a complete metric space $X$ admits a $d$-dimensional weak tangent field, the set $S$ is close to being $d$-dimensional in the following sense. Whenever $μ$ is a Borel finite measure on $X$ supported on $S$, then a typical $1$-Lispchitz map (in the sense of Baire category) into a Euclidean space maps $μ$-almost all of $S$ into a set of Hausdorff dimension at most $d$. When taking $d=0$, this implies that any $1$-purely unrectifiable set is typically carried into a Hausdorff $0$-dimensional set up to a $μ$-null set. We show that the result is sharp in Euclidean spaces and, more generally, in strictly convex Banach spaces of finite dimension.

</details>

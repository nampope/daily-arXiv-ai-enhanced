### [1] [A note on alternating knots in handlebodies](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.21962)
*Lizzie Buchanan,Tanushree Shah*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文证明了在实心环面中交替结的Kauffman-Murasugi-Thistlethwaite型定理，表明点化约化交替图实现最小交叉数，且同一结的所有此类图具有相同拧数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究实心环面中交替结的性质，扩展经典Kauffman-Murasugi-Thistlethwaite定理到更一般的三维流形设置，为结理论在非平凡背景流形中的研究提供基础。

Method: 通过将Jones多项式推广到环柄体设置，利用广义Jones多项式作为主要证明工具，分析点化约化交替图的性质。

Result: 证明了实心环面中交替结的两个重要性质：1）任何点化约化交替图都实现最小交叉数；2）同一结的所有点化约化交替图具有相同的拧数。

Conclusion: 成功将经典结理论结果扩展到实心环面背景，为处理更复杂三维流形中的结问题提供了新的工具和方法，尽管该结果的更强版本已被其他研究者用不同方法证明。

Abstract: We establish a Kauffman-Murasugi-Thistlethwaite-type theorem for alternating knots in a solid torus. Specifically, we show that any dotted-reduced alternating diagram of a knot in a handlebody realizes the minimal crossing number, and that any two such diagrams of the same knot have identical writhe. The proof relies on a generalization of the Jones polynomial to the setting of handlebodies. A stronger version of this result was already proved by Boden, Karimi, and Sikora using a different generalized Jones polynomial; therefore, this text largely expands on one of the main proof tools.

### [2] [Projective reflection groups of finite covolume](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.22067)
*Balthazar Fléchelles,Seunghoon Hwang*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文证明了在Vinberg域中具有有限体积的Coxeter多面体恰好是负型拟完美Coxeter多面体，并得出Vinberg反射群的Vinberg域是唯一不变凸域的充要条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Coxeter多面体在Vinberg域中的几何性质，特别是有限体积条件与多面体结构特征之间的关系，以及反射群不变凸域的唯一性问题。

Method: 通过分析Coxeter多面体在Vinberg域中的位置关系，特别是多面体与其Vinberg域的包含关系，以及边界点的正则性条件。

Result: 证明了在Vinberg域中具有有限体积的Coxeter多面体恰好是负型拟完美Coxeter多面体，即除了边界上的C^1点外，多面体完全包含在其Vinberg域中。

Conclusion: 对于维数≥2的Vinberg反射群，Vinberg域是唯一不变凸域的充要条件是群在Vinberg域上的作用是有限余体积的。

Abstract: We show that the Coxeter polytopes that have finite volume in their Vinberg domains are exactly the quasiperfect Coxeter polytopes of negative type, i.e. the Coxeter polytopes that are contained in their properly convex Vinberg domain, at the exception of some vertices that are C^1 points of the boundary. As a corollary, we show that for reflection groups à la Vinberg, the Vinberg domain is the only invariant properly convex domain if and only if the action is of finite covolume on the Vinberg domain and the dimension is at least 2.

</details>


### [3] [Cobordism and Concordance of Surfaces in 4-Manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.22152)
*Simeon Hellsten*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文证明了在可定向4-流形中，两个适当嵌入的紧致曲面是配边的当且仅当它们是ℤ/2同调且要么4-流形有边界要么曲面有相同的法向欧拉数。对于单连通4-流形中的闭非定向曲面，配边性蕴含了协边性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究4-流形中曲面的配边和协边分类问题，特别是要完成单连通4-流形中闭曲面的协边分类。这涉及到理解曲面何时可以通过配边或协边相互转化，以及如何构造具有指定边界的配边。

Method: 将Sunukjian的环绕手术方法扩展到非定向情形，使用Pin⁻结构。通过这种方法构造具有指定边界的配边，并确定给定的边界配边何时可以扩展到曲面之间的配边或协边。

Result: 1. 在可定向4-流形中，两个适当嵌入的紧致曲面配边当且仅当它们是ℤ/2同调且满足法向欧拉数条件。2. 在单连通4-流形中，闭非定向曲面的配边性蕴含协边性。3. 完全确定了单连通4-流形中闭曲面的协边分类。4. 给出了构造具有指定边界的配边的新方法。

Conclusion: 本文完成了单连通4-流形中闭曲面的协边分类，建立了配边和协边之间的精确关系，并将相关结果推广到光滑和拓扑范畴。方法的核心是通过Pin⁻结构将环绕手术技术扩展到非定向情形。

Abstract: We show that two properly embedded compact surfaces in an orientable 4-manifold are cobordant if and only if they are $\mathbb{Z}/2$-homologous and either the 4-manifold has boundary or the surfaces have the same normal Euler number. If the 4-manifold is simply-connected and the surfaces are closed, non-orientable, and cobordant, we show that they are in fact concordant. This completes the classification of closed surfaces in simply-connected 4-manifolds up to concordance. Our methods give new constructions of cobordisms with prescribed boundaries, and completely determine when a given cobordism between the boundaries extends to a cobordism or concordance between the surfaces. We obtain our concordance results by extending Sunukjian's method of ambient surgery to the unoriented case using Pin$^-$-structures. We also discuss conditions for an arbitrary codimension 2 properly embedded submanifold to admit an unoriented spanning manifold with prescribed boundary. All results hold in both the smooth and topological categories.

### [5] [Canonical chain complexes for Morse-Smale vector fields](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.22066)
*Clemens Bannwart,Claudia Landi*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 该论文为Morse-Smale向量场构建了规范链复形，改进了现有非规范构造，利用不稳定流形滤过的Čech同调谱序列技术实现。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 1960年Smale定义了光滑流形上Morse-Smale向量场的不稳定流形滤过并推导了广义Morse不等式，这暗示即使存在闭轨道，Morse-Smale向量场也应存在规范链复形，但现有技术只能提供非规范构造。

Method: 利用不稳定流形滤过的Čech同调谱序列技术，将有界正合偶转化为链复形，使得与正合偶相关的谱序列的极限页给出链复形的同调。

Result: 成功为Morse-Smale向量场构建了规范链复形，这些复形在拓扑等价下不变，可从代数上推导Morse不等式，并通过示例展示了构造。

Conclusion: 该研究实现了Morse-Smale向量场的规范链复形构造，解决了长期存在的非规范性问题，为相关代数拓扑研究提供了新工具。

Abstract: In 1960, Smale defined a filtration of a closed smooth manifold by the unstable manifolds of fixed points and closed orbits of a Morse-Smale vector field defined on it, and derived generalized Morse inequalities. This suggests that, similarly to the Morse chain complex of a gradient-like vector field, even in the presence of closed orbits, Morse-Smale vector fields admit canonical chain complexes, invariant under topological equivalence, from which one can algebraically derive Morse inequalities. In this paper we show that this is actually the case, improving the state of the art that only offers non-canonical chain complexes. Technically, we achieve this result considering the Čech homology spectral sequence of the unstable manifolds filtration. In particular, we turn bounded exact couples into chain complexes such that the limit page of the spectral sequence associated with an exact couple gives the homology of the chain complex. We showcase our construction with examples.

</details>

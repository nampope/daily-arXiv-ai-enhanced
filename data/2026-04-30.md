### [1] [On submersions with definite folds of manifolds with boundary into Euclidean spaces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.26249)
*Koki Iwakura*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究了具有确定折叠的浸没（submersions with definite folds）的微分拓扑性质，这类映射是定义在带边流形上且边界限制为确定折叠映射的浸没。对于靶空间为ℝ的情况，利用Hajduk和Borodzik–Némethi–Ranicki的结果得到了源流形微分同胚型的限制。对一般维数欧氏空间，通过引入边界限制为圆形折叠映射或像简单折叠映射的条件，研究了可容纳此类映射的流形的微分同胚型和欧拉示性数，并将结果应用于确定折叠映射的非奇异扩张研究。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究带边流形上具有确定折叠的浸没的性质，从微分拓扑角度探讨哪些带边流形可以容许这样的映射进入欧氏空间，以及这些映射对源流形结构产生的约束。

Method: 利用Hajduk和Borodzik–Némethi–Ranicki的已有结果；对边界限制施加圆形折叠映射或像简单折叠映射等条件，分析流形的微分同胚型和欧拉示性数。

Conclusion: 本文系统研究了具有确定折叠的浸没，给出了源流形在微分拓扑层面的约束，这些结果还可应用于确定折叠映射的非奇异扩张问题。

Abstract: Submersions with definite folds are submersions on manifolds with boundary whose restrictions to the boundary are definite fold maps. In this paper, we study the properties from the viewpoint of differential topology of manifolds with boundary admitting such maps into Euclidean spaces. When the target is $\mathbb{R}$, we obtain restrictions on the diffeomorphism types of the source manifolds by using previous results of Hajduk and Borodzik--Némethi--Ranicki. For Euclidean spaces with general dimensions, we consider submersions with definite folds whose restrictions to the boundary are round fold maps or image simple fold maps, both defined by imposing conditions on the singular point set. Then, we study the diffeomorphism types and Euler characteristics of the manifolds admitting such maps. These results are also applied to the study of non-singular extensions of definite fold maps.

Along the way, we give a singularity-theoretic proof of the equivariant Reidemeister theorem and study loops of equivariant Reidemeister moves. <details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

We show that such an algebra is equivalently described as a collection $\{\mathcal{X}(G/H)\}_{(H\leq G)}$ of commutative algebras in nonequivariant rational spectra, indexed by conjugacy classes of subgroups of $G$, together with compatible morphisms of commutative algebras $\mathcal{X}(G/K)\xrightarrow{}\mathcal{X}(G/H)$ whenever $K\leq H$ and the induced map $G/K\xrightarrow{}G/H$ is in $\mathcal{I}$. ### [4] [Euclidean distance geometry and the orthogonal beltway problem](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.26030)
*Dan Edidin,Arun Suresh*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 本文研究了正交腰带问题，证明了当 m > n 时，可以从自相关函数恢复一般二进制信号的 O(n) 轨道，并开发了多项式时间重构算法（R³中复杂度上限 O(m⁸)），对噪声具有鲁棒性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 该问题源于X射线晶体学中的经典腰带问题，受冷冻电镜技术启发，推广到高维空间。核心动机是从信号的二阶矩（自相关）中恢复其旋转轨道，这在结构生物学中具有重要应用价值。

Method: 1. 理论证明：利用欧几里得距离几何的平行理论，证明当 m > n 时，具有至少 ℓ 个等幅值点的二进制信号的 O(n) 轨道可从自相关中恢复；2. 算法设计：开发基于欧几里得距离几何的多项式时间重构算法，在R³中复杂度 O(m⁸)，并扩展至球面支撑向量的情况。

Result: 1. 证明了 m > n 时一般二进制信号的 O(n) 轨道可恢复性；2. 作为推论，证明了球面点集的未标记点间距离可恢复其O(n)轨道；3. 开发了多项式时间算法（R³中 O(m⁸)），实验表明实际复杂度更低；4. 算法对低水平噪声具有鲁棒性；5. 成功扩展至球面支撑向量恢复。

Conclusion: 本文从理论上解决了正交腰带问题的可恢复性条件，并提供了实用的多项式时间重构算法，连接了距离几何与信号恢复领域，为冷冻电镜等实际应用提供了理论基础和计算工具。

Abstract: The orthogonal beltway problem is the problem of recovering the $\mathrm{O}(n)$-orbit of a $δ$-function supported at a finite number of points in $\r^n$ from its auto-correlation or, equivalently, second moment. It was introduced as a generalization of the classical beltway problem in X-ray crystallography and was motivated by cryo-electron microscopy. In this paper we prove that if $m > n$, then the $\mathrm{O}(n)$-orbit of generic binary signal supported at $m$ points where at least $\ell$ of them have equal magnitude can be recovered from its auto-correlation. We also provide a connection to Euclidean distance geometry and prove, as a corollary of our main theorem, that if $m > n$, then the $\mathrm{O}(n)$-orbit of a generic collection of $m$ points on the sphere $S^{n-1}$ can be recovered from their unlabeled interpoint distances.
  We take advantage of the parallels to Euclidean distance geometry and develop a polynomial-time reconstruction algorithm for recovering the $Ø(n)$-orbits of binary $δ$-functions from their second-moment data when at least one of the points has distinct magnitude. In $\mathbb{R}^3$, the complexity of our algorithm is bounded from above by $O(m^8)$ but we show that in practice the complexity is much lower. We also demonstrate that the algorithm is robust to low levels of noise. Finally, we extend our algorithm to successfully perform recovery when all the support vectors lie on a common sphere, and in this case we match the time complexity of $O(m^8)$.

### [1] [Real bordered Floer homology](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20565)
*Robert Lipshitz,Peter Ozsváth*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文为带边界且具有对合对称性的3维流形构建了 bordered 实Heegaard Floer 模，并给出了计算 $\widehat{HFR}$ 的实用算法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 扩展实Heegaard Floer同调 $\widehat{HFR}(Y,τ)$ 到带边情形，建立配分定理以便于计算。

Method: 对合适的Heegaard图，关联一个 bordered Heegaard Floer 代数上的模，并证明配分定理。

Result: 这些模满足配分定理，推广了 $\widehat{HFR}$，并给出连通固定集情形的实用算法。

Conclusion: 建立了 bordered 版本的实Heegaard Floer理论，降低了计算门槛。

Abstract: Fix a 3-manifold $Y$ with boundary $F\amalg F$ and an orientation-preserving involution $τ: Y\to Y$ exchanging the boundary components, with nonempty fixed set. To an appropriate kind of Heegaard diagram for $Y$, we describe how to associate a module over the bordered Heegaard Floer algebra of $F$. These modules satisfy a gluing, or pairing, theorem, and extend the "hat" variant of Guth-Manolescu's real Heegaard Floer homology, $\widehat{HFR}(Y,τ)$. Using these modules, we give a practical algorithm to compute $\widehat{HFR}(Y,τ)$ for real 3-manifolds $(Y,τ)$ with connected fixed set.

### [2] [Twisted Alexander Polynomials, Ribbon Homology Cobordisms, and the Thurston Norm](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20785)
*Brian Sun*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究4维带形同调环绕（ribbon homology cobordism）对3维流形Thurston范数和纤维化性质的影响，证明不可约的负端流形的Thurston范数单位球包含正端流形的单位球，且正端的纤维化类对应于负端的纤维化类。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解4维带形同调环绕如何限制两端3维流形的几何与拓扑性质，特别是Thurston范数和纤维化结构之间的关系。

Method: 利用扭曲Alexander多项式（twisted Alexander polynomials），推广Friedl等人的工作，分析带形同调环绕中1-柄和2-柄的构造对同调信息和范数的影响。

Result: 若Y_-不可约且存在从Y_-到Y_+的带形同调环绕，则Y_-的Thurston范数单位球包含Y_+的单位球；Y_+的纤维化类对应于Y_-的纤维化类。

Conclusion: 带形同调环绕对3维流形的Thurston范数有包含关系约束，并保持纤维化类的对应性，深化了对4维同调环绕与3维拓扑之间联系的理解。

Abstract: Let $Y_-$ and $Y_+$ be two compact 3-manifolds with empty or toroidal boundary. A 4-dimensional ribbon homology cobordism is a homologically trivial cobordism built with 1-handles and 2-handles. In this note, following the work of Friedl and collaborators, we apply twisted Alexander polynomials to show that if there is a ribbon homology cobordism from $Y_-$ to $Y_+$ with $Y_-$ irreducible, then the unit ball of the Thurston norm of $Y_-$ contains that of $Y_+$. Moreover, we show in general that the fibered classes of $Y_+$ correspond to those of $Y_-$.

Main category: math.AT

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 持续模的同调代数工具（如张量积、内Hom、Künneth序列、万有系数定理）在单参数和多参数设定下尚不完善，需要系统化的理论构建以支持更复杂的拓扑数据分析。

Method: 通过引入依赖于保序映射φ:P×P→P的张量积⊗_φ，证明其具有右伴随Hom^φ，建立Künneth短正合序列；对ℝ_+情形具体化为ℓ^p_quasinorms，显式计算区间模的Tor和Ext导出函子。

Result: 建立了持续模的张量积-内Hom伴随对，得到了Künneth短正合序列（包括上同调版本）和万有系数定理，显式计算了ℓ^p_区间模的导出函子，证明了可用于加速乘积度量空间持续同调计算。

We prove that each $\otimes_{\varphi}$ has a right adjoint, $\mathbf{Hom}^{\varphi}$, the internal hom of persistence modules that also depends on $\varphi$. We prove that every $\otimes_{\varphi}$ yields a Künneth short exact sequence of chain complexes of persistence modules. We show that the Universal Coefficient Theorem developed can be used to compute persistent Borel-Moore homology of a filtration of non-compact spaces. Finally, we show that for every $p\in [1,\infty]$ the associated Künneth short exact sequence can be used to significantly speed up and approximate persistent homology computations in a product metric space $(X\times Y,d^p)$ with the distance $d^p((x,y),(x',y'))=||d_X(x,x'),d_Y(y,y')||_p$.

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 研究紧集凸包到其自身点的$l_p$ Hausdorff距离，证明在二维情况下该距离的$p$次幂关于闵可夫斯基求和满足次可加性（乘以常数因子$\max\{1,2^{p-2}\}$），且该界是紧的。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 理论分析与不等式推导，通过构造凸包中点到原集的距离上确界，并证明在$n=2$时$(d^{(l_p)})^p$的次可加性。

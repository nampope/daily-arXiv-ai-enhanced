### [1] [On extending results of Gluck and Warner on fibrations of spheres by great subspheres](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06712)
*Eric Yu*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文在Gluck和Warner（1983）的基础上，研究了将3球面上由大圆定向纤维化推广到更高维球面的问题，给出了两个Hopf纤维化在某一纤维上一致的条件完整刻画，并讨论了阻碍得到完全一般化结果的因素。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Gluck和Warner关于3球面正定向纤维化与2球面到自身距离递减映射之间双射的经典结果推广到更高维球面。

Method: 通过分析两个Hopf纤维化在$S^{2n-1}$上何时必然在某条纤维上一致，来研究推广的可能性。

Result: 给出了两个Hopf纤维化在某一纤维上一致这一现象的完整刻画。

Conclusion: 虽然得到了该现象的完整刻画，但存在障碍阻止我们获得Gluck和Warner结果的完全一般化版本。

Abstract: In this paper, we build upon the work of Gluck and Warner who showed in 1983 that the set of positively oriented fibrations of a 3-sphere by oriented great circles is in bijection with the set of distance-decreasing maps from the 2-sphere to itself. One approach to generalizing their result to higher-dimensional spheres involves understanding when exactly two Hopf fibrations of $S^{2n-1}$ are guaranteed to agree on a fiber. We give a complete characterization of this phenomenon, and we discuss the barriers which prevent us from obtaining a fully general version of Gluck and Warner's result.

### [2] [Plumbed 3-Manifolds and Neumann Moves](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06824)
*Noah Pope*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文通过构造性证明，利用Neumann演算和Duchon-Eisenbud-Neumann对角化算法，给出了将弱负定plumbing树转化为负定树的有限序列算法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 经典plumbing演算中，弱负定树与负定树之间的转化关系尚缺乏明确的构造性证明和显式算法。

Method: 结合Neumann的plumbing演算与Duchon、Eisenbud、Neumann的对角化算法，从树的组合结构中提取framing矩阵的特征值，证明正特征值仅出现在线性分支上，并通过受控的Neumann移动系统性地消除它们。

Result: 证明了每个弱负定plumbing树都可以通过有限次Neumann移动转化为负定树，并给出了显式的构造性算法。

Conclusion: 该工作填补了plumbing演算中弱负定到负定转化的构造性空白，提供了实用的算法工具。

Abstract: We give a constructive proof that every weakly negative definite plumbing tree can be transformed into a negative definite one by a finite sequence of Neumann moves. The argument combines Neumann's plumbing calculus with the diagonalization algorithm of Duchon, Eisenbud, and Neumann, which extracts the eigenvalues of the framing matrix directly from the combinatorics of the tree. We show that any positive eigenvalues are supported on linear branches and can be eliminated systematically via controlled applications of Neumann moves. This provides an explicit algorithm reducing weakly negative definite plumbing trees to negative definite ones.

Result: 证明了包含K_6作为子式的图的悬挂图，其任何嵌入到R^4的方式中都必然存在非平凡链接的1-环和2-环。

Abstract: We produce an infinite family of $2$-complexes that are intrinsically linked when embedded into four dimensions. In particular, we show that any embedding into $\mathbb{R}^4$ of the suspension of a graph containing $K_6$ as a minor contains a non-trivially linked 1 and 2-cycle.

### [4] [Translation Surfaces arising from Right Regular Prisms](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06967)
*Xun Gong,Zuo Lin,Anthony Sanchez*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究正 n 棱柱上的平直度量，发现其展开曲面仅在 n=4 时为格子曲面，但存在到超椭圆曲面的平移覆盖，利用超椭圆分支分类确定了 GL(2,R) 轨道闭包，进而获得了鞍连接计数问题的精确二次渐近和 Siegel-Veech 常数，为非格子曲面提供了可显式计算的无穷族实例。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Platonic 固体对应的曲面是格子曲面，但正棱柱的展开曲面是否也是格子曲面尚不清楚。作者希望探究正 n 棱柱的平直度量性质，并与 Platonic 固体的情形进行对比。

Method: 将正 n 棱柱视为 n-微分形式，通过展开（unfolding）方法分析其平直度量结构；证明其展开曲面到超椭圆曲面的平移覆盖存在性，并利用超椭圆分支的轨道闭包分类定理。

Result: 除 n=4 外，正 n 棱柱的展开曲面不是格子曲面；但存在到超椭圆曲面的平移覆盖，从而可确定其 GL(2,R) 轨道闭包；并得到了鞍连接计数问题的精确二次渐近和 Siegel-Veech 常数。

Conclusion: 正 n 棱柱构成一个自然无穷的非格子曲面族，但其轨道闭包和计数问题仍可显式计算，为相关领域提供了新的可处理例子。

Abstract: We study flat metrics arising from right regular $n$-prisms by viewing them as $n$-differentials and analyzing their associated unfoldings. We show that the unfolding of a right regular $n$-prism is never a lattice surface unless $n=4$, in contrast with the case of Platonic solids. Despite this, we prove that these surfaces admit translation coverings to hyperelliptic surfaces, allowing us to determine their $\mathrm{GL}(2,\mathbb{R})$-orbit closures using the classification of hyperelliptic components of strata.
  As a consequence, we obtain exact quadratic asymptotics for a certain average of the number of saddle connections on the base surfaces, their unfoldings, and the original prisms, including their Siegel--Veech constants. This provides a natural infinite family of non-lattice surfaces for which orbit closures and counting problems can be computed explicitly.

</details>

### [5] [Combinatorial Models for Linear Homotopy Theories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.07573)
*Atabey Kaygun*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文比较了特征0域上多种线性结构（链复形、半单纯向量空间、增强半单纯向量空间、半立方向量空间、树状向量空间）在小微分范畴代数下的关系，证明了半单纯模与增强半单纯模在Gabriel-Zisman局部化和Quillen模型范畴层次上等价于链复形同伦理论，而半立方模通过符号嵌入与增强半单纯模仅构成Quillen伴随而非等价，因为-1阶增强同调存在障碍。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 澄清特征0域上多种代数结构（链复形、半单纯、半立方、树状等）在同伦理论层面的精确关系，特别是半立方结构未能与链复形完全等价的深层原因。

Method: 通过小微分范畴代数框架，分别从Gabriel-Zisman局部化和Quillen模型范畴两个层次，系统比较多种向量空间结构之间的等价性与伴随关系。

Result: 1) 半单纯模与增强半单纯模在两种同伦理论层面均等价于链复形；2) 半立方模与增强半单纯模之间仅存在Quillen伴随而非Quillen等价，障碍来自-1阶增强同调。

Conclusion: 不同线性结构在模型范畴层次上的等价性并非普遍成立，半立方情形因-1阶同调障碍而无法实现完全等价，揭示了符号嵌入结构的局限性。

Abstract: For a field $k$ of characteristic $0$, we compare $k$-linear chain complexes, semisimplicial vector spaces, augmented semisimplicial vector spaces, semicubical vector spaces, and arboreal vector spaces through small differential categorical algebras. We prove that semisimplicial modules and augmented semisimplicial modules are equivalent to appropriate chain-complex homotopy theories, both at the Gabriel--Zisman localization and the Quillen model-categorical level. The semicubical sign embedding gives a natural comparison from semicubical modules to augmented semisimplicial modules and induces a Quillen adjunction, but not a Quillen equivalence on the full semicubical category since there is an obstruction in augmented homology at degree $-1$.

TL;DR: 本文定义了任意双不完全Tambara函子的素理想谱，统一推广了Lewis（Green函子）和Nakaoka（Tambara函子）的工作，并提供了多种计算工具与实例分析。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 已有理论仅针对Green函子和Tambara函子分别定义了素理想，缺乏对更一般的双不完全Tambara函子的统一处理，本文旨在填补这一空白。

Method: 通过定义双不完全Tambara函子的素理想谱，统一并推广Lewis和Nakaoka的构造，并配套开发计算工具进行实例验证。

In this work, we define the spectrum of prime ideals for an arbitrary bi-incomplete Tambara functor, simultaneously generalizing Lewis and Nakaoka's notions. We then produce many computational tools which we apply to several examples of interest.

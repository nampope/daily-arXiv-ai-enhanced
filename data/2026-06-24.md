### [1] [Non-asphericity of strata of genus-one differentials and stability spaces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24135)
*Dawei Chen,Jingyin Huang,Yu Qiu,Fei Yu*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文证明了在亏格为一的黎曼曲面上，当零点或极点的数量至少为四个时，具有给定零点和极点阶数的微分分量的每个连通分支都不是轨道$K(π,1)$。对于二次微分，这为Kontsevich的一个猜想以及关于Bridgeland稳定条件空间可收缩性的一个民间猜想提供了无限多个反例。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 挑战并否定两个重要猜想：Kontsevich关于轨道$K(π,1)$性质的猜想，以及关于Bridgeland稳定条件空间可收缩性的民间猜想。

Method: 通过分析亏格为一的微分分量的拓扑性质，证明当零点或极点数量至少为四个时，每个连通分支都不是轨道$K(π,1)$。

Result: 发现并构建了无限多个反例，否定了上述两个猜想。

Conclusion: Kontsevich的猜想和Bridgeland稳定条件空间可收缩性的民间猜想在亏格为一且零点/极点数量足够多的情况下不成立。

Abstract: We show that when the number of zeros or poles is at least four, every connected component of the strata of differentials in genus one with prescribed zero and pole orders is not an orbifold $K(π,1)$. For quadratic differentials, this provides infinitely many counterexamples to a conjecture attributed to Kontsevich, as well as to a folklore conjecture concerning the contractibility of spaces of Bridgeland stability conditions.

### [2] [A Combinatorial Characterization of Sol 3-Manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.23963)

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 基于正则语言（regular language）刻画Sol流形的特征，构造一个通用的紧致分支3-流形W，使得闭3-流形浸入W的条件等价于其具有Sol结构。通过这种构造，进一步将Sol流形与特定类型的三角剖分建立等价关系。

Result: 本文证明了一个闭3-流形M能浸入通用空间W当且仅当M是Sol流形，并且Sol流形等价于具有某类特定三角剖分的3-流形。

Conclusion: 该工作为Sol流形提供了新的几何和组合刻画方法，通过通用浸入空间和正则语言工具，将几何结构、组合三角剖分与形式语言理论联系起来，为三维流形的分类研究提供了新的视角。

Abstract: We show that there is a universal compact branched 3-manifold $W$ such that a closed 3-manifold $M$ immerses into $W$ if and only if $M$ admits a Sol structure. Equivalently, a closed 3-manifold is Sol if and only if it has a certain type of triangulation. The construction of $W$ is based on a regular language that characterizes Sol manifolds.

### [3] [Morse-Novikov theory for links](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24009)
*L. Chen,H. Endo,A. Pajitnov*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究S³中链环补空间的瑟斯顿多面体的纤维面，利用非交换诺维科夫同调，证明了两分支两桥链环的第一上同调类可被圆纤维化表示当且仅当其双变量亚力山大多项式是ξ-首一的，并计算了多数8交叉以内两分支素链环的Morse-Novikov数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Thurston引入了3维流形第一上同调群上的范数，其单位球是凸多面体，可被圆纤维化表示的类构成某些开面上的锥。本文旨在研究S³中链环补空间的此类纤维面。

Method: 基于链环补空间万有覆盖相关的非交换诺维科夫同调，通过对两分支两桥链环的2变量亚力山大多项式进行分析，建立其与纤维化表示之间的联系。

Result: 证明了两分支两桥链环L的上同调类ξ可被圆纤维化表示当且仅当其2变量亚力山大多项式是ξ-首一的。计算了多数交叉数≤8的两分支素链环的Morse-Novikov数。

Conclusion: 非交换诺维科夫同调为判断链环补空间中的纤维化提供了有效工具，给出了一个简洁的代数判据。

Abstract: For a compact 3-manifold W. Thurston introduced a norm on the first cohomology group of the manifold. The unit ball $B$ of this norm is a polyhedron and the set of cohomology classes that are representable by fibrations over a circle is a union of cones on some of the open faces of $B$. In the present paper we study the fibred faces of the Thurston polyhedra of exteriors of links in $S^3$. Our approach is based on the non-abelian Novikov homology associated with the universal covering of the exterior of the link. We prove in particular that for a 2-component 2-bridge link $L$ a cohomology class $ξ\in H^1(E(L))$ can be represented by a fibration over a circle if and only if its 2-variable Alexander polynomial is $ξ$-monic. We compute the Morse-Novikov numbers for a majority of 2-component prime links with number of crossings $\leq 8$.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

### [7] [Lengths of simple closed geodesics on hyperbolic surfaces in prescribed homology classes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24836)
*Igor M. Patsankov*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文研究了双曲曲面上表示固定原始非零同调类的简单闭测地线计数问题，给出了下界估计，并在双环面特例中得到更强的指数上界。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 经典问题是双曲曲面上闭测地线长度的计数。Mirzakhani给出了简单闭测地线总数的渐近公式，但对固定同调类的计数下界尚不明确。数值证据表明已有上界并非渐近最优，需要更精确的下界估计。

Method: 考虑带测地边界和穿刺的双曲曲面，在亏格g≥1且g+n+b≥3条件下，通过构造足够多的简单闭测地线来证明下界；对于双环面特例，利用更精细的几何分析得到更强的指数下界。

Result: 一般情形：h_S(L,x) ≥ C₁ L^{6(g-1)+2(n+b-1)}；双环面特例：h_{S₁,₂}(L,x) ≥ C₂ L^{3.011057...}。

Conclusion: 证明了固定同调类简单闭测地线的计数下界，修正了此前仅有的上界认识，且特例结果表明指数可能低于一般情况下的上界，揭示了更深层的几何结构。

Abstract: A classical question in the theory of hyperbolic surfaces is the study of lengths of closed geodesics under various constraints. A celebrated result in this area is M. Mirzakhani's asymptotic formula for the number of simple closed geodesics of length $\le L$ on a hyperbolic surface of genus $g$ with $n$ punctures. We investigate the number of simple closed geodesics of length $\le L$ representing a fixed primitive nonzero homology class $x$ on a hyperbolic surface $S$. We denote this number by $h_{S}(L, x)$. It follows from Mirzakhani's result that $h_{S}(L, x) \le C L^{6(g-1) + 2n}$. However, numerical evidence suggests that this bound is apparently not asymptotically sharp. We prove that for a surface $S$ of genus $g$ with $n$ punctures and $b$ geodesic boundary components, under the condition that $g \ge 1$ and $g+n+b \ge 3$, there exists a constant $C_1 > 0$ such that for sufficiently large $L$ the inequality \[ h_{S}(L, x) \ge C_1 L^{6(g-1) + 2(n + b-1)} \] holds. In the special case of a torus with two punctures $S_{1, 2}$, we  obtain the following stronger result: there exists a constant $C_2 > 0$ such that for sufficiently large $L$ the inequality \[ h_{S_{1, 2}}(L, x) \ge C_2 L^{3.011057 \ldots } \] holds.

</details>

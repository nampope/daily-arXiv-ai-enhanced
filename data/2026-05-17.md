### [1] [McShane-Rivin norm balls and simple-length multiplicities](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14574)
*Nhat Minh Doan,Xiaobin Li,Van Nguyen*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文利用McShane-Rivin范数球的法向估计，证明了完全有限面积双曲穿孔环面上简单闭测地线数目最多为C_X(log L)²，并改进了模环面的Markoff数纤维界，同时揭示了McShane-Rivin范数球在无理方向上的局部几何性质。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 旨在给出双曲穿孔环面上简单闭测地线的精确计数上界，并改善已有的Markoff数纤维界估计，同时探索McShane-Rivin范数球的局部几何性质。

Method: 利用McShane-Rivin范数球的法向估计作为主要技术工具，结合双曲几何与数论方法进行分析。

Result: 证明了对任意完全有限面积双曲穿孔环面，长为L≥2的简单闭测地线数目不超过C_X(log L)²；对模环面，Markoff数m对应的纤维数量满足C(log log(3m))²的上界，改进了对数阶估计。

Conclusion: 给出了更优的简单闭测地线计数上界，并揭示了McShane-Rivin范数球在无理方向上的非无限平坦性等新几何信息。

Abstract: We use normal-turn estimates for McShane--Rivin norm balls to prove that, for every complete finite-area hyperbolic once-punctured torus $X$, the number of simple closed geodesics of length exactly $L\geq 2$ is at most $C_X(\log L)^2$. For the modular torus, this gives $$ \#λ_M^{-1}(m)\leq C(\log\log(3m))^2 $$ for every Markoff number $m$, improving the previous logarithmic Markoff-fiber bounds. These estimates also give new quantitative information on the local geometry of McShane--Rivin norm balls, including obstructions to infinite-order flatness at certain irrational directions.

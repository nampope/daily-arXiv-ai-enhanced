### [2] [Reducibility of self-maps in monoid and its related invariants](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.22908)
*Gopal Chandra Dutta*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了自映射的k-可约性，证明了k-可约性可通过同调或上同调诱导同态确定，并建立了n-原子空间的概念及其基本性质，最终确定了不同n-原子空间楔和的同调自接近数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究自映射的k-可约性是一个微妙的问题，需要建立理论框架来分析楔和空间中自映射的性质，特别是当空间分解为原子空间时的情况。

Method: 通过分析自映射在同调群上诱导的同态来研究k-可约性，引入n-原子空间的概念，并研究其在楔和分解下的性质，最终计算楔和空间的同调自接近数。

Result: 证明了自映射的k-可约性可通过其诱导的同调或上同调同态确定；建立了n-原子空间的基本性质；确定了不同n-原子空间楔和的同调自接近数。

Conclusion: 该研究为分析楔和空间中自映射的k-可约性提供了系统框架，通过引入n-原子空间概念简化了同调自接近数的计算，在空间分解为不同n-原子空间时特别有效。

Abstract: Given a positive integer $k$, we investigate the $k$-redcibility of self-maps in the monoid $Å^k(X\vee Y)$, consisting of self-maps that induce isomorphisms on homology groups up to degree $k$. In general, verifying $k$-reducibility is a subtle problem. We show that the $k$-reducibility of a self-map is determine through its induced endomorphisms on homology or cohomology groups. Moreover, under the k-reducibility assumption, the computation of the homology self-closeness number of the wedge sum of spaces essentially reduces to the computation of the homology self-closeness numbers of the individual wedge summands. We generalize the notion of an atomic space to that of an $n$-atomic space and establish some of its fundamental properties. We show that the $k$-reducibility criteria for self-maps in a monoid $Å^k(X)$ is satisfied when the space $X$ decomposes as a wedge sum of distinct $n$-atomic spaces. Finally, we determine the homology self-closeness numbers of wedge sums of distinct $n$-atomic spaces.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Tambara函子的谱理论，类似于交换环的Zariski谱理论，探索Tambara函子中的幂零性和局部化性质

Method: 分析Tambara函子中的幂零概念，证明幂零根可以通过逐层计算得到，分类使局部化T[1/x]=0的元素

Abstract: Associated to each Tambara functor $T$ is its Nakaoka spectrum $\mathrm{Spec}(T)$, analogous to the Zariski spectrum of a commutative ring. We establish that this topological space is spectral. This result follows from an analysis of the notion of nilpotence in Tamabra functors. We prove that the nilradical of a Tambara functor $T$ (the intersection of all of its prime ideals) is computed levelwise, i.e. consists precisely of the nilpotent elements in $T$. As a corollary, we observe that the set of these elements in $π_\star^s$ (the equivariant stable stems, viewed as an $\mathrm{RO}(G)$-graded Tambara functor) forms an ideal.

### [5] [Tangents to Lipschitz and Sobolev images](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.22473)
*Matthew Badger,Jared Krandel,Vyron Vellis*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 该论文在几何测度论中建立了Rademacher和Calderón型可微性定理的几何版本，证明了Sobolev映射图像在几乎处处具有唯一的切集或度量切空间。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Sobolev映射图像的切结构，补充Federer关于n-可求长集近似切空间存在唯一性定理，但关注的是映射图像而非可求长集本身。

Method: 1. 首先证明Sobolev映射图像的n-填充容量有限；2. 然后证明若在正测度集上无法将近似切提升为Attouch-Wets或Gromov-Hausdorff切，则会导致无限填充容量，从而导出矛盾。

Result: 对于p>n的Lipschitz或连续W^{1,p} Sobolev映射f: [0,1]^n→ℝ^d，图像f([0,1]^n)在几乎处处（关于n维Hausdorff测度）具有唯一的Attouch-Wets切集；对于连续N^{1,p}映射f: [0,1]^n→度量空间，图像在几乎处处具有唯一的Gromov-Hausdorff度量切。

Conclusion: 该工作建立了Sobolev映射图像的几何可微性理论，将经典的可微性定理推广到几何框架，为研究度量空间中映射图像的切结构提供了新工具。

Abstract: We develop geometric versions of Rademacher and Calderon type differentiability theorems in two categories. A special case of our results is that for any Lipschitz or continuous $W^{1,p}$ Sobolev map $f$ from $[0,1]^n$ into a Euclidean space with $p>n$, the image $f([0,1]^n)$ has a unique tangent set (Attouch-Wets convergence) at almost every point with respect to the $n$-dimensional Hausdorff measure. In the analogous case when $f$ is a continuous $N^{1,p}$ map from $[0,1]^n$ into a metric space, we show that the image $f([0,1]^n)$ has a unique metric tangent (Gromov-Hausdorff convergence) almost everywhere. These results complement, but are distinct from Federer's theorem on existence and uniqueness of approximate tangents of $n$-rectifiable sets in $\mathbb{R}^d$. We show that approximate tangents to Sobolev images can be upgraded to Attouch-Wets or Gromov-Hausdorff tangents by first proving that the $n$-packing content of Sobolev images is finite, then proving that the inability to upgrade on a set of positive measure implies infinite packing content.

### [6] [Rigidity of circle polyhedra and hyperideal polyhedra: the tangency case](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.22903)
*John C. Bowers,Philip L. Bowers,Carl O. R. Lutz*

Main category: math.MG

TL;DR: 证明了球面上适当三角剖分的凸双曲圆多面体的全局刚性，推广了先前结果并包含边与双曲球相切的新情况。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究双曲圆多面体的刚性理论，特别是当多面体的边可能与双曲球相切的情况，这扩展了经典的Koebe-Andre'ev-Thurston定理。

Method: 使用Beltrami-Klein模型中的适当三角剖分凸双曲多面体，其顶点在双曲球外，面与双曲球相交，边可能与双曲球相切。

Result: 证明了这类双曲圆多面体的全局刚性，推广了Bao-Bonahon和arXiv:1703.09338的结果，并包含相邻圆不必相切的情况。

Conclusion: 该结果将经典的Koebe-Andre'ev-Thurston定理的唯一性部分推广到相邻圆不必相切的情况，为双曲几何中的刚性理论提供了新的理解。

Abstract: We prove the global rigidity of proper triangulated convex hyperbolic circle polyhedra on the sphere $\mathbb{S}^2$. These circle polyhedra correspond to proper triangulated convex hyperbolic polyhedra in the Beltrami-Klein model $\mathbb{B}^{3}$ of hyperbolic space with hyperideal vertices whose faces meet $\mathbb{B}^{3}$. Although the vertices of these polyhedra lie outside $\mathbb{B}^{3} \cup \mathbb{S}^{2}$ and the faces meet $\mathbb{B}^{3}$, the edges may miss $\mathbb{B}^{3}$ entirely, meet $\mathbb{B}^{3}$, or, more importantly, lie tangent to $\mathbb{B}^{3}$ at ideal points on the boundary $\partial \mathbb{B}^{3} = \mathbb{S}^{2}$. The latter case is new and generalizes the global rigidity results of both Bao-Bonahon and arXiv:1703.09338. This result also generalizes the uniqueness part of the celebrated Koebe-Andre'ev-Thurston theorem to the case where adjacent circles need not touch.

</details>
